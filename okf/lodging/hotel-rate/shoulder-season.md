> The period of moderate travel demand and occupancy that falls between a destination's peak season (highest demand) and off-peak or low season (lowest demand). Shoulder season typically offers intermediate pricing, shorter booking lead times, and less crowding than peak, while still providing reasonable weather or activity conditions. Hoteliers, tour operators, and carriers use shoulder-season rate strategies to smooth demand curves and optimise annual revenue.

Shoulder season boundaries vary by destination, product type, and traveler segment: a beach resort may define its shoulder season as spring and autumn, while a ski resort treats spring as shoulder and late autumn as the transition from low to shoulder. Revenue managers use shoulder season pricing to stimulate demand when the property would otherwise be underoccupied, offering promotional rates, added-value packages, or minimum-stay waivers without undermining peak-season rate integrity. In hotel performance benchmarking (STR/CoStar), properties frequently compare their ADR, occupancy, and RevPAR against competitive sets separately for each season to assess relative performance. Online travel agencies and tour operators similarly segment inventory and marketing spend by season, bidding more aggressively in shoulder periods where conversion costs are lower.

**한국어 / Korean** — **숄더 시즌(어깨 시즌)** — 목적지의 성수기(최고 수요)와 비수기 또는 저수기(최저 수요) 사이에 해당하는 중간 수준의 여행 수요 및 점유율 기간. 숄더 시즌은 일반적으로 성수기보다 저렴한 요금, 짧은 예약 리드 타임, 덜 붐비는 환경을 제공하면서도 합리적인 날씨 또는 활동 여건을 유지한다. 호텔리어, 투어 운영사, 항공사는 숄더 시즌 요금 전략을 활용해 수요 곡선을 평탄화하고 연간 수익을 최적화한다.

숄더 시즌의 경계는 목적지, 상품 유형, 여행자 세그먼트에 따라 다르다. 해변 리조트는 봄·가을을 숄더 시즌으로 정의할 수 있는 반면, 스키 리조트는 봄을 숄더로, 늦가을을 비수기에서 숄더로의 전환기로 본다. 수익 관리자는 성수기 요금 무결성을 훼손하지 않으면서 숙박 시설이 저조한 점유율을 보일 시기에 수요를 촉진하기 위해 프로모션 요금, 부가가치 패키지, 최소 숙박 면제 등을 제공하는 숄더 시즌 요금 전략을 활용한다.

# Distinctions
- **Shoulder Season** vs [Rate Plan](/lodging/hotel-rate/rate-plan.md) — A rate plan is a configured pricing product with specific conditions; shoulder season is a demand period that drives which rate plans are activated, promoted, or restricted. Shoulder-season promotions are commonly implemented by creating or enabling specific rate plans.
- **Shoulder Season** vs [ADR](/lodging/hotel-rate/adr.md) — ADR measures the average rate actually achieved per sold room in any given period; shoulder season is the demand context that determines whether rate management targets higher or lower ADR relative to peak and off-peak periods.
