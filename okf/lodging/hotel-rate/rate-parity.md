> Rate parity is the practice or contractual commitment of maintaining consistent rates (and often availability/conditions) for the same room type and dates across distribution channels, so that, for example, an OTA does not show a different public rate than the hotel's own website. It is frequently enforced through parity clauses in distribution contracts.

Rate parity typically applies to public/BAR-type rates and aims to prevent channel undercutting. Clauses range from 'wide parity' (parity required across all channels including direct) to 'narrow parity' (parity only relative to the hotel's own direct channels). Such clauses have faced regulatory and competition-law scrutiny in several jurisdictions, leading to restrictions or bans on wide parity, so the term's applicability varies by market.

**한국어 / Korean** — **요금 동등성(Rate Parity)** — Rate parity는 동일한 객실 유형과 날짜에 대해 유통 채널 전반에서 일관된 요금(그리고 흔히 가용 여부·조건)을 유지하는 관행 또는 계약상 약정으로, 예를 들어 OTA가 호텔 자체 웹사이트와 다른 공개 요금을 표시하지 않도록 한다. 유통 계약의 parity 조항을 통해 시행되는 경우가 많다.

Rate parity는 일반적으로 공개 요금/BAR 유형 요금에 적용되며 채널 간 가격 후려치기를 방지하는 것을 목표로 한다. 조항은 '광의 parity'(직판을 포함한 모든 채널에서 동등성 요구)부터 '협의 parity'(호텔 자체 직판 채널에 대해서만 동등성 요구)까지 다양하다. 이러한 조항은 여러 관할권에서 규제 및 경쟁법상 검토를 받아 광의 parity에 대한 제한이나 금지로 이어졌으므로, 이 용어의 적용 가능성은 시장마다 다르다.

# Distinctions
- **Rate Parity** vs [BAR](/lodging/hotel-rate/bar.md) — BAR is the actual best public rate; rate parity is the policy that this rate stays consistent across channels.
- **Rate Parity** vs [Onboard Credit (OBC)](/cruise/cruise/onboard-credit-obc.md) — Onboard credit is a way to sweeten a booking without breaking rate parity — the advertised fare stays identical across channels while value is added through spendable credit rather than a lower price.
- **Rate Parity** vs [Opaque Inventory](/lodging/hotel-dist/opaque-inventory.md) — Opaque inventory is a way to discount that does not breach rate parity, because the discount is detached from an identifiable, publicly listed property rate; rate parity is the consistency requirement that opaque selling is specifically designed to sidestep.
- **Rate Parity** vs [Rate Parity Clause](/lodging/hotel-dist/rate-parity-clause.md) — Rate Parity is the general state/policy of keeping a hotel's rate consistent across channels; the rate parity clause is the specific contractual MFN instrument (wide or narrow) by which an OTA enforces that state — and it is the object of the DMA prohibition and the 2024 ECJ ruling.
- **Rate Parity** vs [Rate Shopping](/lodging/hotel-dist/rate-shopping.md) — Rate parity is the policy of keeping a hotel's rate consistent across channels; rate shopping is the monitoring activity that detects whether parity is actually being maintained.
