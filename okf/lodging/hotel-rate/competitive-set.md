> A Competitive Set (CompSet) is the defined peer group of hotels — typically three to five properties with similar location, service level, amenities, and price tier — against which a hotel benchmarks its own revenue performance. CompSet comparisons are the backbone of hotel revenue management benchmarking, producing the RevPAR Index (RGI), Occupancy Index, and ADR Index through standardised reports such as the STR STAR Report.

A hotel selects its CompSet based on market criteria: properties that travellers would genuinely consider as alternatives when booking. Once submitted to a benchmarking service (most commonly STR, now part of CoStar Group), the hotel receives weekly or monthly STAR Reports comparing its own occupancy, ADR, and RevPAR against the aggregated CompSet average. Three key indices result: the Occupancy Index (also called Market Penetration Index, MPI) measures whether the hotel is capturing its fair share of occupancy; the ADR Index measures relative average rate performance; and the RevPAR Index (RGI) is the composite measure of revenue per available room performance against the CompSet. An RGI above 100 indicates the hotel is outperforming its peer group. Ownership, asset management, and revenue management teams use CompSet tracking to assess strategy effectiveness, set performance targets, and validate pricing decisions. Selecting the wrong CompSet — peers that are not genuine alternatives — is a common pitfall that distorts benchmarking signals.

**한국어 / Korean** — **경쟁 비교군(Competitive Set)** — Competitive Set(CompSet)은 호텔이 자체 수익 성과를 벤치마킹하는 비교 대상 호텔 군으로, 보통 위치·서비스 수준·편의시설·가격대가 유사한 3~5개 호텔로 구성된다. CompSet 비교는 RevPAR Index(RGI), 객실 점유율 지수, ADR 지수 등을 통해 호텔 수익 관리 벤치마킹의 핵심을 이루며, STR STAR Report 등 표준 보고서를 통해 제공된다.

호텔은 여행자가 예약 시 실제 대안으로 고려할 만한 호텔을 기준으로 CompSet을 선정한다. 벤치마킹 서비스(가장 일반적으로 CoStar Group 소속 STR)에 제출하면, 호텔은 자체 점유율·ADR·RevPAR을 CompSet 집계 평균과 비교하는 주간 또는 월간 STAR Report를 받는다. 세 가지 핵심 지수가 도출된다: 점유율 지수(시장 침투 지수, MPI)는 호텔이 적정 점유 점유율을 확보하고 있는지를 측정하고, ADR 지수는 상대적 평균 요금 성과를 측정하며, RevPAR 지수(RGI)는 CompSet 대비 객실당 수익의 종합 지표이다. RGI가 100 이상이면 CompSet 대비 초과 성과를 나타낸다. 오너십·자산 관리·수익 관리 팀은 CompSet 추적을 통해 전략 효과를 평가하고, 성과 목표를 설정하며, 가격 결정을 검증한다. 실제 대안이 아닌 호텔로 CompSet을 잘못 구성하는 것은 벤치마킹 신호를 왜곡하는 흔한 실수이다.

# Distinctions
- **Competitive Set** vs [Rate Shopping](/lodging/hotel-dist/rate-shopping.md) — Rate Shopping monitors competitor room rates in real time or near-real time to inform pricing decisions; Competitive Set benchmarking tracks actual achieved RevPAR, ADR, and occupancy relative to the peer group over time — one is a live pricing intelligence tool, the other is a retrospective performance scorecard.
- **Competitive Set** vs [Revenue Management](/lodging/hotel-rate/revenue-management.md) — Revenue Management is the overall discipline of optimising room revenue through demand forecasting, pricing, and inventory control; a Competitive Set is an external benchmarking input that tells the revenue manager how the hotel is performing relative to its peers, not how to set prices per se.
