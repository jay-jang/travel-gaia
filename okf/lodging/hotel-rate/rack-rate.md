> The rack rate is a hotel's highest published, undiscounted reference price for a room type — the official 'full' rate from which discounts, negotiated rates, and promotions are subtracted, and the price historically quoted to walk-in guests with no booking. It is the traditional anchor of hotel pricing that dynamic Best Available Rate (BAR) has largely replaced as the practical selling benchmark.

Historically the rack rate was literally the rate posted on the room rack at the front desk and printed in brochures and tariffs, representing the maximum a hotel would charge before any discounting. Almost no guest paid it in practice; it functioned as the ceiling against which corporate, group, package, and promotional discounts were expressed and as the legally displayed maximum in some jurisdictions. With the rise of revenue management and dynamic pricing, the practical reference point shifted from a static rack rate to a demand-driven BAR that moves by date, so the rack rate now survives chiefly as a high anchor, a fallback walk-in quote, and a comparison baseline rather than a live selling rate. It is still loaded in many PMS and CRS systems as the master public rate.

**한국어 / Korean** — **Rack Rate (정찰가/공시 요금)** — Rack Rate(정찰가/공시 요금)는 호텔이 객실 유형에 대해 게시하는 최고·무할인 기준 가격으로, 할인·협정 요금·프로모션이 차감되는 공식 '정가'이자 예약 없이 찾아온 워크인(walk-in) 고객에게 역사적으로 제시되던 가격이다. 동적 BAR(Best Available Rate)이 실무 판매 기준으로서 대체로 대신하게 된, 호텔 가격의 전통적 기준점이다.

역사적으로 rack rate는 말 그대로 프런트 데스크의 객실 랙(rack)에 게시되고 브로슈어·요금표(tariff)에 인쇄되던 요금으로, 호텔이 할인 전에 청구할 수 있는 최대치를 나타냈다. 실제로 그 가격을 낸 고객은 거의 없었으며, 기업·단체·패키지·프로모션 할인이 그에 대해 표현되는 상한이자 일부 관할권에서는 법적으로 게시되는 최대치로 기능했다. 수익 관리와 dynamic pricing의 부상으로 실무 기준점은 정적 rack rate에서 날짜별로 움직이는 수요 기반 BAR로 옮겨갔고, 이제 rack rate는 라이브 판매 요금이라기보다 높은 기준점, 워크인 폴백 견적, 비교 기준선으로 주로 남아 있다. 여전히 많은 PMS·CRS 시스템에 마스터 공개 요금으로 적재된다.

# Distinctions
- **Rack Rate** vs [BAR](/lodging/hotel-rate/bar.md) — Rack rate is the static maximum published price that rarely changes and that almost no one pays; BAR is the dynamic, demand-driven lowest unrestricted public rate actually offered and adjusted by date. BAR is the modern practical successor to the rack rate as the selling benchmark.
- **Rack Rate** vs [ADR](/lodging/hotel-rate/adr.md) — Rack rate is a single published ceiling price; ADR (Average Daily Rate) is the realised average of prices actually achieved across sold rooms. One is an aspirational list price, the other a backward-looking performance metric.
- **Rack Rate** vs [Corporate Rate](/lodging/hotel-rate/corporate-rate.md) — A corporate rate is a confidential negotiated discount typically expressed relative to rack or BAR; rack rate is the public undiscounted reference it is discounted from.
