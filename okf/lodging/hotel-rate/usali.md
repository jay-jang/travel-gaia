> The hotel industry standard chart of accounts and financial reporting framework published by the Hospitality Financial and Technology Professionals (HFTP) and the American Hotel & Lodging Association (AHLA), defining how revenue, expenses, and operating metrics—including ADR, RevPAR, and GOPPAR—are categorised and reported across departments.

First published in 1926 and now in its 12th Revised Edition, USALI establishes a common language for hotel financial reporting, enabling benchmarking across properties, ownership structures, and management companies. It defines the Uniform Operating Statement and schedules for all major revenue and expense centres — Rooms, Food & Beverage, Spa, Retail, and Undistributed Operating Expenses. USALI standardises the calculation of key performance indicators: ADR (Average Daily Rate), RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room), TRevPAR (Total Revenue Per Available Room), GOPPAR (Gross Operating Profit Per Available Room), and others. Lenders, asset managers, and franchise agreements routinely require USALI-compliant reporting. The framework complements but does not replace GAAP or IFRS; it is an industry-specific chart of accounts overlay.

**한국어 / Korean** — **숙박산업 통일 회계 시스템(USALI)** — HFTP와 AHLA가 공동 발행하는 호텔 업계 표준 계정과목표 및 재무 보고 체계로, ADR, RevPAR, GOPPAR 등 핵심 성과 지표의 산출 방식을 부문별로 정의한다.

1926년 처음 발행되어 현재 제12차 개정판인 USALI는 호텔 재무 보고의 공통 언어를 확립하여 시설·소유 구조·운영사 간 벤치마킹을 가능하게 한다. USALI는 ADR, RevPAR, TRevPAR, GOPPAR 등 KPI의 산출 방식을 표준화하며, 대주·자산 관리자·프랜차이즈 계약에서 USALI 준수 보고를 요건으로 삼는 경우가 많다. GAAP이나 IFRS를 대체하지 않고 보완하는 업계 특화 체계다.

# Distinctions
- **USALI (Uniform System of Accounts for the Lodging Industry)** vs [RevPAR](/lodging/hotel-rate/revpar.md) — RevPAR is a single KPI defined and calculated within the USALI framework; USALI is the comprehensive accounting standard that defines RevPAR, GOPPAR, ADR, and dozens of other metrics alongside the full chart of accounts for hotel financial reporting.
- **USALI (Uniform System of Accounts for the Lodging Industry)** vs [GOPPAR](/lodging/hotel-rate/goppar.md) — GOPPAR is one of the profit-per-room metrics standardised by USALI; USALI is the broader financial reporting framework that defines how gross operating profit is calculated across all hotel departments before arriving at GOPPAR.
