> Total Revenue Per Available Room (TRevPAR) is a hotel performance metric equal to all revenue generated by a property—across every department including rooms, food & beverage, spa, parking, and ancillaries—divided by the total number of available rooms. It extends RevPAR beyond room revenue to reveal the full revenue-generating breadth of a hotel and is especially significant for full-service and resort properties with substantial non-room income.

TRevPAR = Total Hotel Revenue / Rooms Available, where Total Hotel Revenue encompasses all operated departments and other income before any operating expense deductions. It is the broadest single top-line KPI for a lodging property. Comparing TRevPAR to RevPAR highlights the contribution of ancillary and non-room revenue streams, useful for assessing the performance of spa, golf, F&B, and event facilities alongside room operations. For profitability benchmarking, TRevPAR is typically paired with GOPPAR, which applies expense deductions to the same total revenue base.

**한국어 / Korean** — **가용 객실당 총매출(TRevPAR)** — TRevPAR(Total Revenue Per Available Room)은 객실·F&B·스파·주차·부대 서비스 등 모든 부문의 호텔 총매출을 가용 객실 수로 나눈 지표다. RevPAR이 객실 매출에 한정되는 것과 달리, TRevPAR은 숙소의 전체 매출 창출 역량을 드러내며, 객실 외 수입이 상당한 풀서비스·리조트 호텔에 특히 의미 있다.

TRevPAR = 총 호텔 매출 / 가용 객실 수. 총 호텔 매출은 운영 중인 모든 부문의 수익과 기타 수입을 포함하되 운영비용은 차감하기 전이다. 숙박 시설의 가장 포괄적인 단일 톱라인 KPI다. TRevPAR과 RevPAR을 비교하면 스파·골프·F&B·행사 시설의 성과를 객실 운영과 나란히 평가할 때 유용한 부대·비객실 매출 기여도를 파악할 수 있다. 수익성 벤치마킹에는 동일한 총매출 기반에서 비용을 차감하는 GOPPAR과 함께 활용하는 것이 일반적이다.

# Distinctions
- **TRevPAR** vs [RevPAR](/lodging/hotel-rate/revpar.md) — RevPAR measures only room revenue per available room; TRevPAR measures total revenue across all hotel departments per available room. For limited-service hotels with minimal non-room revenue the two converge, but for full-service hotels TRevPAR is substantially higher.
- **TRevPAR** vs [GOPPAR](/lodging/hotel-rate/goppar.md) — TRevPAR is a revenue metric (before costs); GOPPAR is a profitability metric that deducts departmental and undistributed operating expenses from that same total revenue base. TRevPAR shows how much revenue the hotel generates; GOPPAR shows how much profit it keeps.
