> A direct booking is a reservation made by the guest through the hotel's own channels — its website and booking engine, brand.com, mobile app, call center, or front desk — rather than through a third-party intermediary such as an OTA, GDS, or wholesaler. Direct bookings let the hotel own the guest relationship and data and typically avoid third-party commission.

Direct booking is the counterpart to indirect (third-party) distribution. Its commercial appeal is a lower distribution cost and full ownership of guest data, enabling loyalty, personalization, and repeat marketing; OTA commissions of roughly 15–25% are replaced by direct acquisition costs (payment processing, booking-engine fees, paid marketing, parity monitoring), which can be low when efficient but rise toward OTA levels when fragmented. 'Direct booking strategy' refers to deliberate tactics — member rates, loyalty perks, best-rate guarantees, metasearch and paid-search investment — to shift share from OTAs to the direct channel. The direct channel depends operationally on a booking engine connected to the CRS/PMS for real-time availability and rates.

**한국어 / Korean** — **다이렉트 부킹 (Direct Booking)** — 다이렉트 부킹은 OTA·GDS·도매업체 같은 제3자 중개를 거치지 않고, 호텔 자체 채널(웹사이트와 booking engine, brand.com, 모바일 앱, 콜센터, 프런트 데스크)을 통해 투숙객이 직접 하는 예약이다. 다이렉트 부킹은 호텔이 고객 관계와 데이터를 직접 소유하게 하며 일반적으로 제3자 수수료를 피한다.

다이렉트 부킹은 간접(제3자) 유통의 대척점이다. 상업적 매력은 더 낮은 유통 비용과 고객 데이터의 완전한 소유로, 이는 로열티·개인화·재방문 마케팅을 가능하게 한다. 약 15~25%의 OTA 수수료가 직접 획득 비용(결제 처리, booking engine 수수료, 유료 마케팅, parity 모니터링)으로 대체되는데, 효율적으로 운영되면 낮지만 분산되면 OTA 수준까지 올라갈 수 있다. 'direct booking 전략'은 회원 요금, 로열티 혜택, 최저가 보장, 메타서치·유료검색 투자 등 OTA에서 직접 채널로 점유율을 이동시키려는 의도적 전술을 가리킨다. 직접 채널은 운영상 실시간 가용성·요금을 위해 CRS/PMS에 연결된 booking engine에 의존한다.

# Distinctions
- **Direct Booking** vs [OTA (Online Travel Agency)](/common/standards/ota-online-travel-agency.md) — An OTA is a third-party intermediary that sells the hotel's rooms for a commission and typically owns the guest relationship; a direct booking is made through the hotel's own channels with no intermediary, keeping the guest data and avoiding commission.
- **Direct Booking** vs [Booking Engine](/lodging/hotel-dist/booking-engine.md) — The booking engine is the software tool that processes a direct online reservation; direct booking is the resulting transaction/channel concept, of which the booking engine is the primary enabler.
