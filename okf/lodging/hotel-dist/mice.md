> MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions) is the industry designation for the business-events segment of travel: the planning, facilitation, and hosting of professional group gatherings for corporate, association, and governmental clients. It represents a major demand category for hotels, convention centres, and destinations worldwide and is increasingly referred to simply as the "Meetings Industry" or "Business Events" by leading trade bodies such as ICCA and MPI.

MICE breaks into four components: Meetings (internal corporate gatherings or association business meetings), Incentives (reward travel programmes offered to top-performing employees or distribution partners), Conferences/Conventions (larger participatory events designed for discussion, education, and networking — often run by associations or governments), and Exhibitions/Expositions (trade fairs and public shows at which products and services are displayed). Hotels and venues pursue MICE business because it drives high-value contracted blocks that fill low-demand periods, generates room-night volume, and yields significant ancillary spend (food and beverage, audio-visual, transportation). For a destination, MICE is valued for its high average spend per delegate, off-peak demand generation, and long lead-time that aids forward-booking certainty. Destination Management Companies (DMCs) are often engaged to handle on-the-ground logistics for MICE groups.

**한국어 / Korean** — **기업 행사(MICE)** — MICE(Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions)는 여행 산업에서 기업·협회·정부 고객을 위한 전문 단체 모임의 기획·진행·개최를 아우르는 비즈니스 이벤트 부문의 산업 명칭이다. 호텔·컨벤션센터·목적지에 있어 가장 중요한 수요 카테고리 중 하나이며, ICCA·MPI 등 주요 단체는 이를 '기업 행사(Business Events)' 또는 '회의 산업(Meetings Industry)'이라고도 부른다.

MICE는 네 가지 구성 요소로 나뉜다: Meetings(기업 내부 모임 또는 협회 업무 회의), Incentives(성과 우수 임직원·유통 파트너에게 제공하는 포상 여행 프로그램), Conferences/Conventions(협회·정부가 주관하는 토의·교육·네트워킹 중심의 대규모 참여형 행사), Exhibitions/Expositions(제품·서비스 전시 무역 박람회). 호텔·행사장이 MICE를 추구하는 이유는 수요가 낮은 시기를 채우는 고가 확정 블록을 유발하고, 객실 수 물량을 확보하며, F&B·AV·교통 등 부가 매출을 창출하기 때문이다. 목적지 입장에서 MICE는 대표단 1인당 지출이 높고, 비수기 수요를 창출하며, 예약 리드타임이 길어 예측 가능성이 높다는 점에서 가치가 크다. DMC는 MICE 그룹의 현장 물류를 처리하기 위해 종종 참여한다.

# Distinctions
- **MICE** vs [Group Block](/lodging/hotel-rate/group-block.md) — A Group Block is the hotel inventory mechanism — the contracted set of rooms reserved at a negotiated rate for a group; MICE is the demand segment label for the broader category of business-events clients that generates group blocks.
- **MICE** vs [Destination Management Company](/tours/tour-ops/destination-management-company.md) — A DMC is a private local company that executes on-the-ground logistics, transfers, and activities; MICE is the demand-segment classification for the business-events travel market that DMCs often serve.
