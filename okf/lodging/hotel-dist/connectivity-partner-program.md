> A Connectivity Partner Program is an OTA-run certification and tiering scheme that qualifies and ranks the technology providers—channel managers, PMSs, and CRSs—that integrate with the OTA's platform on behalf of hotels. Providers earn tiers (e.g., Booking.com Certified / Premier / Premier Plus, or Expedia Group Preferred / Elite) based on connection quality and the breadth of supported features.

Major OTAs require third-party software to certify against their connectivity APIs before it can push availability, rates, and bookings on behalf of properties, and they layer a partner program on top to rank certified providers. On Booking.com, providers start as Certified and can earn points—by connecting more properties and implementing more of Booking.com's product features—to reach Premier and Premier Plus. Expedia Group's program recognizes channel managers, PMSs, CRSs, and other vendors with Preferred or Elite designations for high-quality connections and comprehensive functionality. The tier a hotel's provider holds affects how quickly new OTA features become available to that hotel and how reliable its two-way connection is, making the program a real-world governance mechanism for OTA distribution rather than a mere marketing badge.

**한국어 / Korean** — **연결 파트너 프로그램(Connectivity Partner Program)** — Connectivity Partner Program(연결 파트너 프로그램)은 OTA가 운영하는 인증·등급 체계로, 호텔을 대신해 OTA 플랫폼과 연동하는 기술 제공업체—Channel Manager, PMS, CRS—를 자격 검증하고 순위를 매긴다. 제공업체는 연결 품질과 지원 기능의 폭에 따라 등급(예: Booking.com Certified / Premier / Premier Plus, 또는 Expedia Group Preferred / Elite)을 획득한다.

주요 OTA는 제3자 소프트웨어가 호텔을 대신해 가용성·요금·예약을 푸시하려면 먼저 자사 연결 API에 대해 인증을 받도록 요구하며, 그 위에 인증된 제공업체의 순위를 매기는 파트너 프로그램을 둔다. Booking.com에서는 제공업체가 Certified로 시작해, 더 많은 호텔을 연결하고 Booking.com의 제품 기능을 더 많이 구현함으로써 포인트를 얻어 Premier 및 Premier Plus에 도달할 수 있다. Expedia Group의 프로그램은 Channel Manager, PMS, CRS 및 기타 벤더를 고품질 연결과 포괄적 기능에 대해 Preferred 또는 Elite로 인정한다. 호텔의 제공업체가 보유한 등급은 새로운 OTA 기능이 그 호텔에 얼마나 빨리 제공되는지, 양방향 연결이 얼마나 안정적인지에 영향을 주므로, 이 프로그램은 단순한 마케팅 배지가 아니라 OTA 유통의 실질적 거버넌스 메커니즘이다.

**Aliases:** `Connectivity Provider Program`, `Preferred Connectivity Partner`, `Premier Connectivity Partner`, `Certified Connectivity Partner`

# Distinctions
- **Connectivity Partner Program** vs [Channel Manager](/lodging/hotel-dist/channel-manager.md) — A channel manager is the software that connects a hotel to OTAs; the Connectivity Partner Program is the OTA's certification/tiering scheme that qualifies and ranks such software.
- **Connectivity Partner Program** vs [Two-Way XML](/lodging/hotel-dist/two-way-xml.md) — Two-Way XML is the technical two-direction data exchange between systems; the Connectivity Partner Program is the commercial certification governing which providers are approved to use an OTA's connectivity APIs and at what tier.
