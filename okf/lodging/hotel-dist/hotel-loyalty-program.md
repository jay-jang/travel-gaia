> A hotel loyalty program (also called a hotel rewards program) is a customer-retention scheme operated by a hotel brand, collection, or consortium in which members earn points, qualifying nights, or credits for paid stays and can redeem them for free nights, room upgrades, dining, and partner rewards. Programs are among the most powerful drivers of direct booking, enabling hotel companies to shift demand away from OTA channels — where commissions range from 15–25% — toward the brand website at a fraction of the cost.

Hotel loyalty programs operate on an earn-and-burn model: members earn a fixed number of points per dollar spent, accumulate them, and redeem them against award night prices. Large programs — Marriott Bonvoy (220 M+ members), Hilton Honors (180 M+), IHG One Rewards, World of Hyatt, Wyndham Rewards, Choice Privileges — carry enormous unredeemed point liabilities. Tier structures (Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond) grant elite benefits to high-frequency guests. Co-branded credit cards are the largest driver of new member acquisition and point issuance outside stays.

**한국어 / Korean** — **호텔 로열티 프로그램(Hotel Loyalty Program)** — 호텔 로열티 프로그램은 호텔 브랜드·컬렉션·컨소시엄이 운영하는 고객 유지 제도로, 회원들이 유상 숙박에 대해 포인트·자격 박 수·크레딧을 적립하고 무료 숙박·객실 업그레이드·식음료·파트너 리워드로 교환할 수 있다. OTA 수수료(15~25%)보다 낮은 비용으로 브랜드 웹사이트 직접 예약을 유도하는 가장 강력한 동인 중 하나다.

대형 프로그램(Marriott Bonvoy 2억 2천만 명+, Hilton Honors 1억 8천만 명+, IHG One Rewards, World of Hyatt, Wyndham Rewards, Choice Privileges)은 대규모 미상환 포인트 부채를 보유한다. 티어 구조(실버·골드·플래티넘·다이아몬드)는 고빈도 이용자에게 우대 혜택을 제공하고, 공동 브랜드 신용카드는 신규 회원 유치와 포인트 발급의 최대 동인이다.

# Distinctions
- **Hotel Loyalty Program** vs [Frequent Flyer Program](/air/air-partner/frequent-flyer-program.md) — A Frequent Flyer Program (FFP) is the loyalty scheme operated by an airline rewarding flight activity; a Hotel Loyalty Program rewards hotel stays. The two are distinct systems but often linked through airline-hotel transfer partnerships. Hotel points typically have lower cents-per-point values than airline miles when redeemed for premium award flights.
- **Hotel Loyalty Program** vs [Loyalty Tier Status](/common/customer/loyalty-tier-status.md) — Loyalty Tier Status is the specific tier level (Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Globalist etc.) earned by a member through qualifying activity; Hotel Loyalty Program is the overall reward scheme that defines and operates those tiers.
- **Hotel Loyalty Program** vs [Direct Booking](/lodging/hotel-dist/direct-booking.md) — Direct Booking is the mechanism (booking through the hotel's own brand channel) that hotel loyalty programs are designed to incentivize; the loyalty program is the reward structure motivating the direct booking behavior.
