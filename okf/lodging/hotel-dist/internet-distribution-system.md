> An Internet Distribution System (IDS) is the category of online, non-GDS electronic channels through which a hotel's rooms are sold over the internet—chiefly online travel agencies, travel portals, and online consolidators. It is frequently used as a synonym for ADS (Alternative Distribution System) and is grouped alongside the GDS as one of the hotel's main electronic distribution channels.

IDS is an umbrella term for the internet-based sales channels that distribute hotel inventory to the public outside the traditional GDS—online travel agencies (Expedia, Booking.com, etc.), travel search engines and directories, online hotel consolidators, and similar web portals. In much of the industry IDS and ADS (Alternative Distribution System) are used interchangeably, both contrasting the internet channel with the GDS, which is the agent-facing network. Hotels typically reach IDS channels through a channel manager or CRS, and the term remains common in CRS/connectivity marketing even as more granular labels (OTA, Metasearch) have become standard. Because it lumps together several distinct channel types, IDS is best understood as a channel-category label rather than a single technical system.

**한국어 / Korean** — **인터넷 유통 시스템(IDS / ADS)** — Internet Distribution System(IDS, 인터넷 유통 시스템)은 호텔 객실이 인터넷을 통해 판매되는 GDS가 아닌 온라인 전자 채널의 범주를 가리키며, 주로 OTA, 여행 포털, 온라인 콘솔리데이터를 포함한다. ADS(Alternative Distribution System)와 흔히 동의어로 쓰이며, GDS와 더불어 호텔의 주요 전자 유통 채널 중 하나로 묶인다.

IDS는 전통적 GDS 외부에서 호텔 인벤토리를 일반 대중에게 분배하는 인터넷 기반 판매 채널을 아우르는 포괄 용어로, OTA(Expedia, Booking.com 등), 여행 검색 엔진·디렉터리, 온라인 호텔 콘솔리데이터, 유사 웹 포털을 포함한다. 업계에서는 IDS와 ADS(Alternative Distribution System)를 서로 바꿔 쓰는 경우가 많으며, 둘 다 에이전트를 대상으로 하는 네트워크인 GDS와 인터넷 채널을 대비시킨다. 호텔은 보통 Channel Manager나 CRS를 통해 IDS 채널에 도달하며, OTA·Metasearch 같은 더 세분화된 명칭이 표준이 된 지금도 CRS/연결 마케팅에서 이 용어는 여전히 흔히 쓰인다. 여러 이질적 채널 유형을 한데 묶기 때문에 IDS는 단일 기술 시스템이 아니라 채널 범주를 나타내는 라벨로 이해하는 것이 가장 적절하다.

# Distinctions
- **Internet Distribution System (IDS)** vs [GDS](/common/standards/gds.md) — A GDS is the agent-facing reservation network used by travel agencies; an IDS/ADS is the set of consumer-facing internet channels (OTAs, portals) outside the GDS.
- **Internet Distribution System (IDS)** vs [OTA (Online Travel Agency)](/common/standards/ota-online-travel-agency.md) — An OTA is a single type of online seller; IDS is the broader category that includes OTAs along with travel portals, search engines, and online consolidators.
- **Internet Distribution System (IDS)** vs [Metasearch](/common/standards/metasearch.md) — Metasearch sites aggregate and compare rates and refer the booking elsewhere; IDS channels (OTAs, portals) are where the room is actually sold online.
