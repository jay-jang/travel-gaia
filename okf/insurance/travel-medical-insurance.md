> Travel medical insurance is a travel-insurance product class that covers emergency medical and dental costs incurred while traveling, filling gaps left by a traveler's domestic health plan abroad. It is distinct from trip-cost (cancellation) coverage and is one of the core coverage types in the travel-insurance market.

Travel medical plans focus on health events during the trip: emergency hospitalization, physician and dental treatment, prescriptions, and often the evacuation/repatriation benefits. They are bought heavily by international travelers, expatriates and visitors whose home health insurance does not travel with them, and can be issued as standalone medical-only policies (common for inbound and long-stay travelers) or bundled inside a comprehensive plan. Coverage typically interacts with the pre-existing condition exclusion and its waiver, and may be written as primary or secondary to other health coverage.

**한국어 / Korean** — **여행의료보험(Travel Medical Insurance)** — Travel Medical Insurance(여행의료보험)는 여행 중 발생한 응급 의료·치과 비용을 보장하여 자국 건강보험이 해외에서 비워두는 공백을 메우는 여행보험 상품군이다. 여행비용(취소) 보장과는 구분되며, 여행보험 시장의 핵심 보장 유형 중 하나다.

여행의료 보험은 여행 중 건강 사고에 초점을 둔다. 응급 입원, 의사·치과 진료, 처방약, 그리고 흔히 후송/송환 보장을 포함한다. 자국 건강보험이 함께 따라오지 않는 국제 여행자, 주재원, 방문객이 많이 가입하며, 단독 의료 전용 보험(인바운드·장기 체류자에게 흔함)으로 또는 종합 보험 내에 묶여 발행될 수 있다. 보장은 일반적으로 기왕증 면책과 그 면제(waiver)와 연동되며, 다른 건강보험에 대해 primary 또는 secondary로 설계될 수 있다.

# Distinctions
- **Travel Medical Insurance** vs [Trip Cancellation](/insurance/trip-cancellation.md) — Trip cancellation reimburses lost prepaid trip cost when plans fall through; travel medical insurance pays for healthcare received while traveling. They cover entirely different exposures and are often sold separately.
- **Travel Medical Insurance** vs [Comprehensive Travel Insurance](/insurance/comprehensive-travel-insurance.md) — A comprehensive plan bundles medical coverage with cancellation, delay and baggage benefits; standalone travel medical insurance covers only the health/evacuation exposure.
