> A General Declaration (GenDec) is the standard ICAO Annex 9 form submitted by the aircraft commander (or agent) to customs, immigration, and health authorities at each international arrival port. It identifies the aircraft, flight, crew members, and carries declarations covering health, security, and stores — serving as the master arrival document that gates the release of crew, passengers, and cargo.

The General Declaration format is prescribed in Appendix 1 to Chapter 3 of ICAO Annex 9 (Facilitation). It must be filed for every international arrival and typically contains: aircraft registration and type, operator name, flight number, departure and arrival airports and dates, crew list with nationalities and passport numbers, a passenger summary (total count), a health declaration signed by the commander, a declaration that no prohibited items are on board, and an inventory of dutiable stores. National authorities may add supplementary fields. In practice, the GenDec is submitted electronically via APIS-type advance notification in many states, but a paper original or certified facsimile is often required at the gate. The form is separate from, but related to, the passenger manifest (which lists individual passengers), the cargo manifest, and APIS transmissions that carry individual traveler data for border control pre-screening.

**한국어 / Korean** — **항공기 일반 신고서(General Declaration / GenDec)** — 일반 신고서(General Declaration, GenDec)는 항공기 기장(또는 대리인)이 국제선 입항 시 각 도착지의 세관·출입국·검역 당국에 제출하는 ICAO Annex 9 표준 양식이다. 항공기, 항공편, 승무원을 특정하고, 위생·보안·탑재 물품에 관한 신고를 담아 승무원·승객·화물의 통관을 개시하는 핵심 입항 서류 역할을 한다.

일반 신고서 양식은 ICAO Annex 9(출입국 편의) 제3장 부록 1에 규정되어 있다. 국제선 입항 시마다 제출해야 하며 일반적으로 다음을 포함한다: 항공기 등록번호 및 기종, 운항사 이름, 편명, 출발·도착 공항 및 날짜, 승무원 명단(국적·여권번호 포함), 승객 요약(총 인원), 기장 서명 위생 신고서, 금지 물품 미탑재 신고, 면세 한도 초과 물품 목록. 각국 당국은 추가 항목을 요구할 수 있다. 실무적으로 많은 국가에서 APIS 형태의 전자 사전 통보로 제출하지만, 탑승구에서 종이 원본을 요구하는 경우도 있다.

# Distinctions
- **General Declaration** vs [Advance Passenger Information System](/common/customer/advance-passenger-information-system.md) — The General Declaration is filed by the aircraft commander at each port of arrival covering the entire flight, crew, and aggregate health/stores declarations; APIS transmits individual traveler identity and travel-document data for each passenger to border authorities, usually before departure. The GenDec is aircraft-level; APIS is passenger-level.
- **General Declaration** vs [PNRGOV](/air/air-ops/pnrgov.md) — PNRGOV transmits the reservation record (PNR) data — booking details, itineraries, contact info — from carriers to government agencies for security analysis; the General Declaration is the arrival-port declaration of aircraft identity, crew, and operational matters submitted by the commander.
