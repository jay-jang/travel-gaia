> Ground handling encompasses all services provided to an aircraft between arrival and departure at an airport — including ramp handling (pushback, towing, fuelling, loading), passenger and baggage handling, aircraft cleaning, catering uplift, and de-icing — governed by the IATA Standard Ground Handling Agreement (SGHA).

Ground handling is commonly divided into two main domains: (1) Ramp / Apron handling — the activities directly around the aircraft, including pushback, towing, aircraft servicing (lavatory, water, power), fuelling, cargo and baggage loading/unloading, and de-icing; and (2) Passenger handling — check-in, boarding gate management, special assistance for passengers with reduced mobility (PRM), and lost-baggage handling. Airlines may self-handle (operating their own ground-handling subsidiary), contract a specialist ground-handling agent (GHA) such as Swissport, Dnata, or Menzies Aviation, or use a combination. The IATA Standard Ground Handling Agreement (SGHA), published in the IATA Airport Handling Manual (AHM), is the industry-standard contract template used to define scope, liability, and payment terms between airlines and GHAs. Ground-handling quality directly affects On-Time Performance and is a critical factor in turn-around time at a Slot.

**한국어 / Korean** — **지상 조업(Ground Handling)** — 지상 조업은 공항에서 항공기의 도착부터 출발까지 제공되는 모든 서비스를 말한다. 램프 핸들링(푸시백, 견인, 급유, 화물 탑재), 승객·수하물 처리, 기내 청소, 기내식 탑재, 제빙 작업 등이 포함되며, IATA 표준 지상 조업 협약(SGHA)으로 규율된다.

지상 조업은 크게 두 영역으로 나뉜다. (1) 램프/에이프런 조업 — 항공기 주변의 직접적인 작업으로 푸시백, 견인, 항공기 서비스(화장실·용수·전원 공급), 급유, 화물·수하물 탑재/하기, 제빙 포함. (2) 승객 조업 — 체크인, 탑승 게이트 관리, 교통약자(PRM) 특별 지원, 분실 수하물 처리. 항공사는 자체 자회사를 통해 직접 조업(Self-handling)하거나 Swissport, Dnata, Menzies Aviation 같은 전문 지상 조업사(GHA)에 위탁하거나 혼합 방식을 택할 수 있다. IATA 표준 지상 조업 협약(SGHA)은 IATA 공항 핸들링 매뉴얼(AHM)에 게재되며, 항공사와 GHA 간의 범위·책임·결제 조건을 정의하는 업계 표준 계약 양식이다.

# Distinctions
- **Ground Handling** vs [CUTE](/common/standards/cute.md) — CUTE is a shared IT infrastructure at airports enabling multiple airlines to use common check-in desks; ground handling is the broader set of physical and service operations for the aircraft and passengers between flights.
- **Ground Handling** vs [AOG](/air/air-ops/aog.md) — An AOG is an aircraft grounded due to a technical defect requiring urgent maintenance; ground handling is the routine servicing between scheduled flights when the aircraft is airworthy.
