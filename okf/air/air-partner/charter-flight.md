> A charter flight is an aircraft operation hired in its entirety by a charterer — typically a tour operator, corporation, sports team, or government — rather than being sold seat-by-seat to the general public as a scheduled service. The charterer contracts directly with the airline or aircraft operator for exclusive use of the aircraft on a specified route and date, bearing the commercial risk of filling the seats themselves.

Charter operations are classified by ICAO and national regulators as non-scheduled air transport, subject to separate operating rules from scheduled service. Common charter models include: (1) Inclusive Tour (IT) charters, where a tour operator blocks the aircraft to serve its own holiday packages — historically the backbone of European leisure aviation; (2) Affinity charters, restricted to members of a qualifying group; (3) Public charters sold through travel agents to the general public, subject to special consumer protection rules (e.g. DOT Part 380 in the US); and (4) Private/corporate charters for business or government use. A wet-lease arrangement (ACMI) is closely related but distinct: under ACMI the lessee uses the lessor airline's aircraft, crew, maintenance, and insurance under the lessor's Air Operator Certificate (AOC), whereas in a charter the charterer may contract with an airline that itself operates under its own AOC. Charter operations are key to group travel and leisure tourism, enabling carriers to deploy surplus capacity and tour operators to control cost and scheduling, but they are excluded from most GDS-based scheduled-service distribution and must be sold through direct or package channels. IATA's MITA and interline agreements do not automatically apply to charter flights.

전세 운항은 ICAO 및 각국 규제 당국에 의해 비정기 항공 운송으로 분류되어 정기편과는 별도의 운항 규칙이 적용된다. 주요 전세기 유형으로는 (1) 항공기 전체를 자사 패키지 여행에 사용하는 투어 운영사의 포괄 여행(IT) 전세기; (2) 친목 전세기; (3) 미국 DOT Part 380 등 소비자 보호 규정이 적용되는 공공 전세기; (4) 기업·정부용 사적 전세기가 있다. 웻 리스(ACMI)는 밀접하게 관련되어 있으나 구별된다. ACMI에서는 임차인이 임대인 항공사의 항공기·승무원·정비·보험을 AOC 하에 이용하는 반면, 전세기에서 전세 계약자는 자체 AOC 하에 운항하는 항공사와 계약한다. 전세 운항은 단체 여행과 레저 관광의 핵심이나, GDS 정기편 유통에서 제외되어 직판 또는 패키지 채널을 통해 판매해야 한다.

# Distinctions
- **Charter Flight** vs [Wet Lease](/air/air-partner/wet-lease.md) — In a wet lease (ACMI) an airline provides its aircraft, crew, maintenance, and insurance to another airline (the lessee), which operates the flight under its own flight number and commercial responsibility while the lessor retains its AOC; a charter flight is sold commercially by the airline or charterer directly, with the charterer controlling the distribution of seats.
- **Charter Flight** vs [Codeshare](/air/air-partner/codeshare.md) — A codeshare places an airline's marketing designator on a scheduled flight operated by a different carrier; a charter flight is not a scheduled service at all — the charterer contracts for the entire aircraft and seat distribution is handled outside standard GDS scheduled-service channels.
