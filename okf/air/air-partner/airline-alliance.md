> A long-term commercial cooperation among multiple airlines that pools networks, frequent-flyer programs, lounges, and customer benefits under a common brand, typically built on a web of codeshare and interline agreements. The three major global passenger alliances are Star Alliance, Oneworld, and SkyTeam.

An airline alliance coordinates schedules, reciprocal loyalty earning and redemption, tier-status recognition, shared lounges, and joint customer handling across member carriers, while each airline remains a legally and financially independent company. Alliances generally do not involve revenue or profit sharing on specific markets; that deeper integration is the role of a joint venture, which is often formed among a subset of alliance members on particular regions or routes. Alliance membership provides a broad framework of codeshare and interline relationships over which bilateral commercial deals are layered.

**한국어 / Korean** — **항공 동맹(얼라이언스)** — 여러 항공사가 공동 브랜드 아래 네트워크, 상용고객(frequent-flyer) 프로그램, 라운지, 고객 혜택을 통합하는 장기적 상업 협력으로, 일반적으로 공동운항(codeshare) 및 인터라인 협정의 망 위에 구축된다. 대표적인 3대 글로벌 여객 동맹은 Star Alliance, Oneworld, SkyTeam이다.

항공 동맹은 회원 항공사 전반에 걸쳐 스케줄 조정, 상호 마일리지 적립·사용, 등급(tier) 지위 인정, 라운지 공유, 공동 고객 응대를 조율하지만, 각 항공사는 법적·재무적으로 독립된 회사로 남는다. 동맹은 일반적으로 특정 시장에서의 수익이나 이익 공유를 수반하지 않으며, 그러한 더 깊은 통합은 조인트 벤처(joint venture)의 역할로, 흔히 동맹 회원 중 일부가 특정 지역이나 노선에서 형성한다. 동맹 가입은 양자 간 상업 거래가 얹히는 공동운항 및 인터라인 관계의 폭넓은 기반을 제공한다.

**Aliases:** `Global Airline Alliance`, `Aviation Alliance`

# Distinctions
- **Airline Alliance** vs [Joint Venture](/air/air-partner/joint-venture.md) — An alliance is a broad multilateral cooperation without shared revenue on specific markets; a joint venture is a deeper, usually bilateral or small-group arrangement with revenue/profit sharing on defined routes, often requiring antitrust immunity.
- **Airline Alliance** vs [Codeshare](/air/air-partner/codeshare.md) — Codeshare is a specific flight-level arrangement placing one carrier's code on another's flight; an alliance is the overarching partnership framework that typically encompasses many codeshares among members.
- **Airline Alliance** vs [Antitrust Immunity](/air/air-partner/antitrust-immunity.md) — Alliance membership is broad cooperation that generally does not require immunity; antitrust immunity is needed specifically when partners go further and coordinate pricing/capacity and share revenue on defined markets.
