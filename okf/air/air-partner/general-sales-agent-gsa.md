> A General Sales Agent (GSA) is an organisation appointed by an airline to act as its exclusive or primary sales and marketing representative in a defined geographic market where the airline has no direct commercial presence or only limited resources. The GSA sells and promotes the carrier's flights, handles reservations and ticketing, liaises with travel agencies, and remits revenues to the airline, typically in exchange for a commission on sales.

GSAs are a strategic tool for airlines to achieve market coverage without the fixed costs of establishing a local office with full commercial, operational, and administrative staff. The GSA arrangement is formalised through a dedicated GSA agreement that specifies territory, exclusivity, responsibilities (passenger and/or cargo), commercial targets, commission rate, and the handling of claims. IATA defines a GSA in its Passenger Sales Agency Programme and imposes obligations on airlines appointing them; IATA-accredited GSAs may themselves be authorised to issue tickets on behalf of the airline under the airline's own IATA Numeric Code and TIDS arrangements. GSAs are common where: (a) an airline operates seasonal or charter services but no year-round scheduled routes; (b) the market is strategically important but not large enough to justify a resident sales force; or (c) regulatory barriers or limited bilateral rights restrict direct establishment. In cargo, a Cargo General Sales Agent (CGSA) performs equivalent functions for air freight. A GSA is categorically different from a travel agency: the travel agency is the retailer selling to the end traveller, while the GSA is the airline's wholesale/commercial representative in the market, managing agency relationships on the airline's behalf.

**한국어 / Korean** — **일반 판매 대리점(GSA, General Sales Agent)** — 일반 판매 대리점(GSA)은 항공사가 직접 상업 거점을 두지 않거나 자원이 제한된 특정 지역 시장에서 자사를 대신하여 독점 또는 주요 영업·마케팅 대리인으로 임명한 조직이다. GSA는 해당 항공사의 항공편을 판매·홍보하고, 예약·발권을 처리하며, 여행사와 연락을 취하고, 통상 판매 커미션을 받는 대가로 항공사에 수익을 송금한다.

GSA는 항공사가 전임 상업·운영·관리 인력을 갖춘 현지 사무소를 설립하는 고정 비용 없이 시장 커버리지를 달성하는 전략적 수단이다. GSA 약정은 영역, 독점 여부, 책임, 상업 목표, 커미션율 등을 명시하는 전용 GSA 계약으로 공식화된다. IATA는 여객 판매 대리점 프로그램에서 GSA를 정의하고, IATA 공인 GSA는 항공사를 대신하여 발권 권한을 얻을 수 있다. GSA는 여행사와 근본적으로 다르며, 여행사가 최종 여행자에게 소매 판매를 한다면 GSA는 시장 내 항공사의 도매/상업 대리인으로 대리점 관계를 관리한다.

# Distinctions
- **General Sales Agent (GSA)** vs [Marketing Carrier](/air/air-partner/marketing-carrier.md) — A marketing carrier is an airline that sells seats under its own designator on a flight operated by another airline (codeshare); a GSA is a third-party organisation that represents an airline in a local market — it is a distribution intermediary, not itself a carrier.
- **General Sales Agent (GSA)** vs [Charter Flight](/air/air-partner/charter-flight.md) — Charter flights are commonly distributed through GSAs in markets without a scheduled service by the operating carrier; the GSA is the distribution channel, while the charter flight is the product it sells.
