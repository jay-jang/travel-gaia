> Revenue earned by an airline from sources other than the base ticket price, including fees for optional services (checked bags, seat upgrades, priority boarding, inflight meals, wi-fi) and indirect revenue (co-branded credit-card royalties, loyalty program sales, commissions from hotel and car rental partners). Ancillary revenue is tracked as a per-passenger and absolute figure and has become a structurally significant component of airline economics.

IdeaWorksCompany (in partnership with CarTrawler) publishes the definitive annual "Airline Ancillary Revenue Report," which monitors worldwide airline ancillary revenues. By 2023, global airline ancillary revenues had reached an estimated USD 117 billion across the largest carriers. Ancillary revenue divides into two categories: (1) à la carte fees — direct charges for services that were previously bundled (bags, seats, change fees, food, amenity kits); and (2) commission-based / loyalty revenues — payments from co-brand credit-card partners, points sold to loyalty program partners, and commissions from hotel, car-rental, and insurance cross-sells. For LCCs, ancillary revenue commonly exceeds 40–50% of total revenue per passenger; for full-service carriers it typically represents 10–30%. Airlines report this metric in investor disclosures and as a KPI alongside RASM and load factor.

**한국어 / Korean** — **부가(비항공권) 수익(Ancillary Revenue)** — 기본 항공권 가격 이외의 원천에서 항공사가 얻는 수익으로, 선택적 서비스(위탁 수하물, 좌석 업그레이드, 우선 탑승, 기내식, 와이파이) 수수료와 간접 수익(공동 브랜드 카드 로열티, 로열티 프로그램 판매, 호텔·렌터카 파트너 커미션)을 포함한다. 부가 수익은 승객당 및 총액 지표로 추적되며, 항공사 경제 구조에서 중요한 비중을 차지하게 되었다.

IdeaWorksCompany(CarTrawler와 공동)는 전 세계 항공사 부가 수익을 추적하는 권위 있는 연간 "항공사 부가 수익 보고서"를 발간한다. 2023년 기준 전 세계 대형 항공사의 총 부가 수익은 약 1,170억 달러로 추정된다. 부가 수익은 두 범주로 나뉜다: (1) 알라카르트 수수료 — 기존에 묶음으로 제공되던 서비스에 대한 직접 요금; (2) 커미션 기반/로열티 수익 — 공동 브랜드 신용카드 파트너 수수료, 로열티 파트너에 판매되는 포인트, 호텔·렌터카·보험 교차 판매 커미션. LCC의 경우 부가 수익이 승객당 총 매출의 40~50% 이상을 차지하는 것이 일반적이다.

# Distinctions
- **Ancillary Revenue** vs [Ancillary Service](/air/air-ticket/ancillary-service.md) — An ancillary service is the product or service itself (a checked bag, an upgrade, a seat selection) that generates the charge; ancillary revenue is the financial metric — the aggregate receipts those services produce. A given ancillary service is the unit; ancillary revenue is the KPI.
- **Ancillary Revenue** vs [RASM](/air/air-shop/rasm.md) — RASM (Revenue per Available Seat Mile) is a capacity-normalized total-revenue metric that includes ticket and ancillary revenue; ancillary revenue is a subset of total revenue that explicitly excludes base ticket receipts. RASM can rise even if ancillary revenue falls if base fares increase, and vice versa.
