> Standard Traffic Document (STD) is the IATA collective name for the neutral, accountable documents issued by accredited agents from controlled stock under the Billing and Settlement Plan (BSP) — including neutral electronic tickets, virtual MPDs/EMDs, and agent refund vouchers. Such documents carry no carrier identity until validation and must be issued in strict numerical sequence so that each accountable document can be tracked and settled.

STDs are the document-control foundation beneath agent ticketing. Under BSP, IATA-accredited agents draw from a numerically controlled inventory of neutral accountable documents; the carrier identity is applied only upon validation (selecting the validating/plating carrier). Because every document number is accountable, the agent must report sales, refunds, and adjustments (ADM/ACM) against the stock, and security depends on strict numerical issuance. With near-universal electronic ticketing the physical document has largely become a neutral electronic record, but the STD concept persists as the unit of accountability reconciled through BSP reporting and settlement. The category historically also covered paper instruments such as the MPD (Multiple Purpose Document) / MCO, now superseded by the EMD.

**한국어 / Korean** — **표준 운송 서류 (STD)** — STD(Standard Traffic Document)는 BSP(Billing and Settlement Plan)하에서 인가 대리점이 통제된 재고(stock)에서 발행하는 중립적(neutral)·회계대상(accountable) 서류를 일컫는 IATA의 총칭으로, 중립 전자항공권(neutral e-ticket), virtual MPD/EMD, 대리점 환불 바우처(agent refund voucher) 등을 포함한다. 이러한 서류는 validation 이전에는 특정 항공사 표시를 담지 않으며, 각 회계대상 서류를 추적·정산할 수 있도록 엄격한 일련번호 순서로 발행되어야 한다.

STD는 대리점 발권의 기저에 있는 서류 통제(document-control) 기반이다. BSP하에서 IATA 인가 대리점은 일련번호로 통제되는 중립 회계대상 서류 재고에서 서류를 가져오며, 항공사 표시는 validation 시점(validating/plating carrier 선택)에서만 부여된다. 모든 서류 번호가 회계대상이므로 대리점은 판매, 환불 및 조정(ADM/ACM)을 해당 재고에 대해 보고해야 하며, 보안은 엄격한 일련번호 발행에 의존한다. 전자발권의 사실상 보편화로 물리적 서류는 대부분 중립 전자 레코드로 대체되었으나, STD 개념은 BSP 보고·정산을 통해 대사되는 회계 단위로 여전히 존속한다. 이 범주는 역사적으로 MPD(Multiple Purpose Document)/MCO 같은 종이 서류도 포함했으며, 이는 현재 EMD로 대체되었다.

# Distinctions
- **Standard Traffic Document (STD)** vs [BSP](/common/pay/bsp.md) — BSP is the centralized billing-and-settlement system between agents and airlines; the STD is the accountable document (the unit of stock) that agents issue and report through BSP. BSP is the process; STDs are the documents it controls.
- **Standard Traffic Document (STD)** vs [E-ticket](/air/air-ticket/e-ticket.md) — An e-ticket is one type of STD (the neutral electronic ticket). STD is the broader document-control category that also covers EMDs/virtual MPDs and refund vouchers issued from the same controlled, neutral stock.
