> A per-enplanement fee, capped at $4.50, that US commercial airports collect from air carriers and pass through to passengers, authorised under 49 USC 40117 to fund FAA-approved airport capital improvement projects independent of federal AIP grants.

PFCs are approved by the FAA on a project-by-project basis; an airport applies, FAA evaluates, and the airport then notifies carriers to begin or stop collecting. The maximum level of $4.50 per enplanement was set by 49 USC 40117 and has been the statutory cap since 2000. On a round-trip itinerary, a passenger pays at most $9.00 in PFCs (two $4.50 segments). Carriers retain a small handling fee from each PFC collected and remit the balance to the airport monthly. PFCs appear in the tax/fee box of an airline ticket and are separately disclosed on itinerary receipts. They differ from taxes (government-levied) and carrier-imposed surcharges (airline-set) in that they are airport-set, federally capped, and restricted to approved capital use.

**한국어 / Korean** — **여객 시설 이용료(PFC)** — 미국 상업 공항이 항공사를 통해 승객에게 탑승당 최대 4.50달러를 징수하는 수수료로, 49 USC 40117에 의거해 FAA가 승인한 공항 자본 개선 사업 재원으로 활용된다.

PFC는 사업별로 FAA의 승인을 받으며, 탑승당 최대 4.50달러라는 상한선은 2000년부터 법정 상한으로 유지되고 있다. 왕복 여정에서 승객은 최대 9.00달러의 PFC를 납부한다. PFC는 항공권의 세금·수수료 항목에 표시되며, 정부세(tax)나 항공사 부과 할증료(surcharge)와는 달리 공항이 설정하고 연방법으로 상한이 제한되며 승인된 자본 사용 목적으로만 쓸 수 있다.

# Distinctions
- **Passenger Facility Charge (PFC)** vs [Tax (YQ/YR)](/air/air-shop/tax-yq-yr.md) — A YQ/YR carrier-imposed surcharge and a government-levied airport or aviation tax are set by the airline or government respectively; a PFC is set by the individual airport (up to the federally capped $4.50) and approved by the FAA for specific capital projects.
- **Passenger Facility Charge (PFC)** vs [Surcharge](/air/air-shop/surcharge.md) — A Surcharge is an airline-defined additional charge on top of the base fare (e.g., fuel surcharge YQ); a PFC is an airport-defined, FAA-approved fee earmarked for airport capital improvements and collected by the carrier on the airport's behalf.
