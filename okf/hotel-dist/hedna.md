> HEDNA (Hotel Electronic Distribution Network Association) is a not-for-profit trade association, founded in 1991, dedicated to advancing hotel electronic distribution through education, networking, and the development of distribution best practices and standards. Its members span hotels, representation/management companies, GDSs, switches, OTAs, payment and software providers, making it a primary authoritative body in the field.

HEDNA positions itself as the leading global forum for hospitality distribution, providing its members with networking, education, and professional resources for over three decades. It convenes working groups that produce best practices and guidance across distribution topics (for example GDS representation practices and the use of digital assets/content in distribution). In 2019 HEDNA joined HTNG and the OpenTravel Alliance in a distribution-focused partnership, and the three bodies co-developed the Open Payments Alliance (OPA) standards — which advocate decoupling the B2B payment channel from the reservation channel to streamline payments between TMCs, OTAs, wholesalers, and hotels. As a trade/standards body it differs from the technical specification organizations: HTNG and OpenTravel author detailed messaging/data specifications, while HEDNA focuses on convening the distribution community and publishing distribution best practice, collaborating with them on shared standards such as OPA.

**한국어 / Korean** — **헤드나 (HEDNA, 호텔 전자 유통 네트워크 협회)** — HEDNA(Hotel Electronic Distribution Network Association, 호텔 전자 유통 네트워크 협회)는 1991년에 설립된 비영리 업계 협회로, 교육·네트워킹과 유통 모범사례·표준 개발을 통해 호텔 전자 유통을 발전시키는 데 전념한다. 회원은 호텔, 대표/운영 회사, GDS, 스위치, OTA, 결제·소프트웨어 공급자에 이르며, 이 분야의 주요 권위 기구다.

HEDNA는 스스로를 호스피탈리티 유통을 위한 선도적 글로벌 포럼으로 규정하며, 30여 년간 회원들에게 네트워킹·교육·전문 자원을 제공해 왔다. 유통 주제 전반(예: GDS 대표 관행, 유통에서의 디지털 자산/콘텐츠 활용)에 걸쳐 모범사례와 지침을 만드는 워킹그룹을 운영한다. 2019년 HEDNA는 HTNG 및 OpenTravel Alliance와 유통 중심 파트너십에 합류했고, 세 기구는 Open Payments Alliance(OPA) 표준을 공동 개발했다. OPA는 B2B 결제 채널을 예약 채널에서 분리하여 TMC·OTA·도매업체·호텔 간 결제를 간소화할 것을 제안한다. 업계/표준 기구로서 HEDNA는 기술 사양 조직과 구별된다. HTNG와 OpenTravel은 상세한 메시징/데이터 사양을 집필하는 반면, HEDNA는 유통 커뮤니티를 소집하고 유통 모범사례를 발간하는 데 초점을 두며 OPA 같은 공동 표준에서 그들과 협력한다.

# Distinctions
- **HEDNA** vs [OpenTravel Alliance](/standards/opentravel-alliance.md) — OpenTravel authors detailed XML messaging specifications used to exchange ARI and reservations; HEDNA is a distribution trade body that convenes the community and publishes distribution best practice, partnering with OpenTravel (and HTNG) on shared standards such as the Open Payments Alliance.
- **HEDNA** vs [HTNG](/standards/htng.md) — HTNG focuses on hospitality technology interoperability specifications among hotel systems; HEDNA focuses specifically on the electronic distribution side and the distribution-partner community, though the two collaborate (e.g. on OPA).
