> The EU Flight Emissions Label (FEL) is a voluntary environmental labelling scheme established under Article 14 of ReFuelEU Aviation (Regulation (EU) 2023/2405), with its core elements set out in Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2024/3170 and operated by EASA, which surfaces estimated, verified per-flight CO2-equivalent emissions to passengers at booking. Labels express two metrics: a passenger carbon footprint in kgCO2e per passenger and a flight carbon efficiency in gCO2e per passenger-kilometre.

Airlines volunteer to participate, reporting operational data (fuel burn, passenger load, SAF share) to EASA, which estimates the expected emissions of future flights and issues an approved label. Once an operator opts in, the label must be displayed for all in-scope flights, ensuring non-selective, consistent disclosure. As an emerging 'carbon label' at the point of sale, the FEL directly affects travel distribution and shopping displays and complements calculators such as IATA CO2 Connect and the ICAO ICEC.

**한국어 / Korean** — **항공편 배출 라벨(FEL)** — EU 항공편 배출 라벨(FEL)은 ReFuelEU Aviation(Regulation (EU) 2023/2405) 제14조에 따라 마련되고, 그 핵심 요소가 Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2024/3170에 규정되며, EASA가 운영하는 자발적 환경 라벨링 제도로, 예약 시점에 추정·검증된 항공편별 CO2 환산 배출량을 승객에게 표시한다. 라벨은 승객 1인당 탄소발자국(kgCO2e per passenger)과 승객-킬로미터당 탄소효율(gCO2e per passenger-kilometre)의 두 지표로 표현된다.

항공사는 자발적으로 참여하여 운영 데이터(연료 소모, 승객 탑승률, SAF 비율)를 EASA에 보고하고, EASA는 향후 항공편의 예상 배출량을 추정하여 승인된 라벨을 발급한다. 운영자가 일단 참여하면 적용 범위 내 모든 항공편에 라벨을 표시해야 하므로, 선별적이지 않은 일관된 공개가 보장된다. 판매 시점의 신흥 '탄소 라벨'로서 FEL은 여행 유통과 조회 화면에 직접 영향을 주며, IATA CO2 Connect, ICAO ICEC 같은 계산기를 보완한다.

# Distinctions
- **Flight Emissions Label (FEL)** vs [IATA CO2 Connect](/sustainability/iata-co2-connect.md) — The FEL is an EU regulatory labelling/display scheme producing an official verified label per flight; IATA CO2 Connect is an industry calculator/methodology that estimates emissions but is not itself a regulatory label.
- **Flight Emissions Label (FEL)** vs [ReFuelEU Aviation](/sustainability/refueleu-aviation.md) — ReFuelEU Aviation is the parent regulation mandating SAF blending; the FEL is the specific labelling scheme created under its Article 14.
