# Travel Wiki — Knowledge Bundle

여행 산업 용어 백과사전  (407 concepts · 5 verticals · 16 subcategories · bilingual KO/EN)

A cross-provider travel-industry terminology catalog as an OKF v0.1 bundle, organized as **verticals → subcategories → concepts**. Each concept is one markdown file with YAML frontmatter; relationships and distinctions are bundle-relative cross-links.

# Verticals

* [Air — 항공](air/) - Airline reservation, shopping, ticketing and inter-carrier concepts. (4 subcategories, 115 concepts)
* [Lodging — 숙박](lodging/) - Hotel inventory, rates and distribution. (2 subcategories, 63 concepts)
* [Ground Transport — 지상교통](ground/) - Car rental, rail and multimodal ground transport. (2 subcategories, 36 concepts)
* [Cruise — 크루즈](cruise/) - Cruise products, cabins and sea-travel distribution. (1 subcategories, 20 concepts)
* [Cross-Industry — 산업 공통](common/) - Payments, identifiers, standards, customer, regulation — shared across verticals. (7 subcategories, 173 concepts)
