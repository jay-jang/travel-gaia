> An Interline Electronic Ticket (IET) is an IATA-standard electronic ticket whose coupons span the flights of two or more carriers, enabled by interline e-ticketing agreements between those carriers. It lets multiple airlines' segments sit on a single ticket and underpins interline coupon proration and settlement.

Interline electronic ticketing requires that the validating/issuing carrier and each operating carrier have an active interline e-ticketing relationship - established by being a party to the IATA Multilateral Interline Traffic Agreement (MITA) and concurring with the other party, or via a bilateral interline traffic agreement; IATA publishes the parties that issue and accept each other's electronic tickets (including through MITA and Bilateral Interline E-Ticketing Agreements, BIETA). On an IET, each flight coupon names the operating carrier, allowing the journey to be flown across airlines and the revenue to be apportioned among them through proration and cleared via the IATA Clearing House. It contrasts with a single-carrier e-ticket where every coupon is on the validating carrier.

**한국어 / Korean** — **인터라인 전자항공권 (IET)** — Interline Electronic Ticket(IET)은 쿠폰이 둘 이상 항공사의 항공편에 걸쳐 있는 IATA 표준 전자항공권으로, 해당 항공사 간 인터라인 전자발권 협정에 의해 가능해진다. 여러 항공사의 구간을 하나의 항공권에 담을 수 있게 하며 인터라인 쿠폰 안분(proration)과 정산의 토대가 된다.

인터라인 전자발권은 발권/검증 항공사와 각 운항 항공사가 활성화된 인터라인 전자발권 관계를 가져야 하며, 이는 IATA 다자간 인터라인 운송 협정(MITA)의 당사자가 되어 상대방과 동의(concur)하거나 양자 인터라인 운송 협정을 통해 성립한다. IATA는 서로의 전자항공권을 발행·수용하는 당사자 목록을(MITA 및 양자 인터라인 전자발권 협정 BIETA 포함) 공시한다. IET에서는 각 Flight Coupon이 운항 항공사를 명시하여 여정이 여러 항공사에 걸쳐 운항되고, 그 수익이 proration을 통해 항공사 간에 배분되어 IATA Clearing House를 통해 청산된다. 모든 쿠폰이 검증 항공사에 속하는 단일 항공사 전자항공권과 대비된다.

# Distinctions
- **Interline Electronic Ticket (IET)** vs [E-ticket](/air-ticket/e-ticket.md) — A plain e-ticket may have all coupons on one carrier; an IET is specifically an e-ticket whose coupons span multiple carriers and therefore requires interline e-ticketing agreements and proration.
- **Interline Electronic Ticket (IET)** vs [MITA](/air-partner/mita.md) — MITA is the multilateral agreement that makes interline e-ticketing possible; the IET is the actual ticket document issued under such agreements.
- **Interline Electronic Ticket (IET)** vs [Codeshare](/air-partner/codeshare.md) — Codeshare is a commercial arrangement to sell another carrier's flight under one's own code; an IET is the ticketing/document layer that may carry segments across carriers regardless of whether they are codeshare.
