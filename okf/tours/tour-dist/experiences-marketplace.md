> An online marketplace that aggregates and resells activities and attraction tickets to travelers, the experiences-sector analog of a hotel/air OTA.

Marketplaces such as Viator, GetYourGuide, Klook and Tiqets aggregate operator inventory, standardize content, and provide search, booking, payment, mobile tickets and customer service to travelers, typically as merchant of record. They monetize through a marketplace commission. Their reach depends on connectivity: directly via operator APIs or indirectly through connectivity hubs that aggregate many operators.

**한국어 / Korean** — **익스피리언스 마켓플레이스(Experiences Marketplace)** — 액티비티와 어트랙션 티켓을 모아 여행자에게 재판매하는 온라인 마켓플레이스로, 호텔·항공 OTA에 대응하는 익스피리언스 채널이다.

Viator·GetYourGuide·Klook·Tiqets 같은 마켓플레이스는 운영사 재고를 모으고 콘텐츠를 표준화하며, 여행자에게 검색·예약·결제·모바일 티켓·고객지원을 제공한다(대개 merchant of record로서). 수익은 마켓플레이스 커미션으로 낸다. 이들의 도달 범위는 연동에 좌우되며, 운영사 API로 직접 연결하거나 다수 운영사를 묶는 커넥티비티 허브를 통해 간접 연결한다.

**Aliases:** `Activities OTA`, `Experiences OTA`, `Tours & Activities Marketplace`, `OTA for Experiences`

# Distinctions
- **Experiences Marketplace** vs Activity Operator — A marketplace resells inventory and owns the traveler relationship and (often) payment; the operator owns the inventory and delivers the service on the ground.
