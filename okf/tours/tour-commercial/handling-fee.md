> A handling fee is an administrative charge levied by a tour operator, receptive (inbound) operator, or Destination Management Company (DMC) for processing a booking and providing the associated coordination, documentation, and logistical services — distinct from the cost of the travel components themselves. It compensates the operator for receiving and processing the booking request, preparing the client dossier (vouchers, rooming lists, itineraries, confirmations), coordinating with local suppliers on behalf of the client, and managing changes and operational communications.

Handling fees are a standard B2B charge in the tour operations and DMC sector, particularly in inbound/receptive operator relationships where a foreign wholesaler, outbound tour operator, or travel agency sends individual or group bookings to a local handling agent or DMC. The fee is separate from and additional to the land components (accommodation, transfers, activities, meals), ensuring that the operator recovers the fixed cost of the booking process regardless of booking size. Handling fees may also be called dossier fees (emphasising the per-file administrative cost), booking fees (emphasising the point-of-booking trigger), or service charges. In FIT bookings handled by a DMC, the fee typically applies per booking file; in group bookings it may be structured as a per-person or per-group charge. The fee is usually non-commissionable and must be agreed upon and disclosed in the operator's rate sheet or contract before booking.

**한국어 / Korean** — **핸들링 피(Handling Fee)** — 핸들링 피(Handling Fee)는 투어 운영사, 수신 운영사(receptive/inbound operator), 또는 목적지 관리 기업(DMC)이 예약 처리 및 이에 수반되는 조율·문서화·물류 서비스에 대해 부과하는 행정 수수료로, 여행 구성 요소 자체의 비용과는 별개다. 예약 접수 및 처리, 고객 도시에(바우처·룸 리스트·여정·확인서) 작성, 현지 공급자와의 조율, 변경 및 운영 커뮤니케이션 관리에 대한 보상이다.

핸들링 피는 투어 운영 및 DMC 섹터, 특히 해외 도매업체가 현지 핸들링 에이전트에 예약을 보내는 인바운드/수신 운영사 관계에서 표준 B2B 요금이다. 지상 구성 요소(숙박·이동·액티비티·식사)와는 별개로 부과되며, FIT 예약에서는 예약 파일별, 단체 예약에서는 인당 또는 단체별로 구조화된다. 핸들링 피는 도시에 수수료(dossier fee)·예약 수수료(booking fee)·서비스 수수료(service charge)라고도 불린다.

# Distinctions
- **Handling Fee** vs [Marketplace Commission](/tours/tour-commercial/marketplace-commission.md) — A marketplace commission is a percentage deducted by a marketplace or OTA from the operator-set retail price as the reseller's margin; a handling fee is a flat administrative charge levied by the operator on the booking, compensating for processing and coordination costs. Commission flows to the reseller; the handling fee stays with the operator.
- **Handling Fee** vs [Markup](/tours/tour-commercial/markup.md) — Markup is the amount a reseller adds on top of the operator's net rate to form its own retail price — the reseller's margin in a net-rate model; a handling fee is an administrative charge levied by the operator on top of the land components, applied before any reseller markup. Markup benefits the reseller; the handling fee is revenue for the operator.
