> An Option Date (also called an option expiry or hold date) is the deadline by which a provisional booking must either be confirmed with payment or be cancelled; if no action is taken, the hold on the inventory is automatically released. It is standard practice in group bookings, allotment contracting, and tour operations to prevent inventory being tied up indefinitely by unconfirmed reservations.

When a travel agent, group organiser, or tour operator tentatively reserves seats, rooms, or tour spaces, the supplier grants a hold (or option) for a fixed period, after which the inventory returns to open sale if the booking is not confirmed. The option date is the expiry of that hold. In the group and MICE segment, options are typically three to fourteen days from the hold date; in tour contracting, they may coincide with specific payment-schedule milestones. Some systems send automated reminders before the option date expires. Failing to confirm by the option date does not trigger a cancellation penalty (because no confirmed booking yet exists), but any rate or availability guarantee lapses. In allotment agreements, the concept overlaps with the release period: once past the release date, the allotment block is returned to the hotel's open inventory, which is functionally equivalent to an option lapsing.

**한국어 / Korean** — **옵션 만료일(Option Date)** — 옵션 만료일(Option Date, 또는 hold date)은 가예약을 결제를 통해 확정하거나 취소해야 하는 마감일이다. 이 날짜까지 아무 조치도 없으면 해당 재고에 대한 홀드가 자동으로 해제된다. 미확정 예약으로 재고가 무기한 묶이는 것을 방지하기 위해 단체 예약·allotment 계약·투어 운영에서 표준 관행이다.

여행사, 단체 주최자, 또는 투어 운영사가 좌석·객실·투어 공간을 잠정 예약하면, 공급사는 일정 기간 동안 홀드(또는 옵션)를 부여하며, 이 기간 내 예약이 확정되지 않으면 재고는 일반 판매로 돌아간다. 옵션 만료일은 바로 그 홀드의 만기일이다. 단체·MICE 세그먼트에서 옵션 기간은 홀드일로부터 통상 3~14일이며, 투어 계약에서는 특정 결제 일정 마일스톤과 일치하기도 한다. 일부 시스템은 옵션 만료일 전에 자동 리마인더를 발송한다. 옵션 만료일까지 확정하지 않아도 취소 위약금은 발생하지 않지만(아직 확정 예약이 없으므로), 요율 또는 가용성 보장은 소멸된다.

# Distinctions
- **Option Date** vs [Cut-off Time](/tours/tour-product/cut-off-time.md) — A Cut-off Time is the latest point before an activity's start at which any booking can still be accepted; an Option Date is the deadline specific to a provisional booking, after which the held inventory is released whether or not the activity start date has been reached.
- **Option Date** vs [Cancellation Policy](/lodging/hotel-dist/cancellation-policy.md) — A Cancellation Policy governs what happens when a confirmed booking is cancelled (whether penalties apply and how much); an Option Date is the deadline to confirm a provisional hold — lapsing it is not a cancellation and does not trigger a penalty.
