> A Free-of-Charge (FOC) place is a complimentary seat, room, cabin berth, or admission granted at no cost to a tour operator, group organiser, or travel agent in exchange for delivering a minimum number of paying passengers. Expressed as a ratio such as "1 FOC per 15 paying" or "1 FOC per 20," it is a standard group-booking incentive used across hotels, coaches, cruise lines, rail, and attractions to reward volume commitment and reduce the effective per-person cost for the organiser.

FOC places are negotiated in group contracts between suppliers (hotels, tour operators, cruise lines, attractions) and the buyer (tour operator, DMC, travel agent) and recorded as a ratio in the contract terms. The mechanism reduces the effective cost per passenger without changing the published or contracted per-person rate: if a hotel charges USD 100 per room and offers 1 FOC per 15, the operator pays 15 × USD 100 = USD 1,500 for 16 rooms, yielding an effective cost of USD 93.75 per room. The FOC may be used by the organiser to cover the tour director, local guide, driver, or another member of the group, or it may be converted to a cash discount or ancillary credit at the discretion of the organiser and supplier agreement. In hotels, an FOC room is often offered as "1 complimentary room per X rooms occupied (ROH)." In cruise, the equivalent concept is operationalised as the Tour Conductor Credit (TC) for airline seats and as an FOC berth or complimentary cabin for the group leader. In coaches, "1 in 30" or "1 in 40" is common. The FOC ratio is a key lever in the negotiation of confidential tariffs: a deeper FOC ratio may allow a supplier to maintain a higher headline rate while still providing a competitive net cost to the operator. FOC policy varies widely by supplier, season, and market: peak periods may exclude FOC entitlements entirely, or the ratio may be stepped (e.g. 1/15 for 15–30 pax, 1/12 for 31–50 pax).

**한국어 / Korean** — **무료 탑승·숙박 자리 (FOC, Free-of-Charge Place)** — 무료 자리(FOC)는 최소 지불 승객 수를 충족시키는 대가로 투어 운영사·단체 주선사·여행사에게 무상으로 제공되는 좌석·객실·선실 침대·입장권이다. "15명당 1 FOC" 또는 "20명당 1 FOC" 같은 비율로 표현되며, 호텔·코치·크루즈·철도·명소 등 전 분야에서 거래량 확약에 대한 보상과 주선사의 실질 인당 비용 절감을 위한 표준적인 단체 예약 인센티브이다.

FOC 자리는 공급자와 구매자 간 단체 계약에서 협상되어 계약 조건에 비율로 기록된다. 표제 인당 요금을 변경하지 않고 실질적인 인당 비용을 낮추는 메커니즘이다. FOC는 투어 디렉터·현지 가이드·드라이버에 사용되거나 합의에 따라 현금 할인으로 전환될 수 있다. FOC 비율은 비공개 요율 협상의 핵심 레버이며, FOC 정책은 공급자·시즌·시장에 따라 크게 다르다.

# Distinctions
- **Free-of-Charge Place (FOC)** vs [Tour Conductor Credit (TC)](/cruise/cruise/tour-conductor-credit-tc.md) — A Tour Conductor Credit is specifically the airline industry's provision of a free or reduced-fare air ticket for the tour conductor or group leader, typically 1 TC per 15 full-fare passengers on international flights per IATA conventions; an FOC place is the broader concept of a complimentary unit of inventory (room, berth, seat, admission) in any travel sector, negotiated in group supplier contracts.
- **Free-of-Charge Place (FOC)** vs [Allocation](/tours/tour-commercial/allocation.md) — An Allocation is a block of inventory pre-committed to a distributor at a contracted rate, which the distributor then sells and pays for (or releases before the cutoff); an FOC place is a specific incentive within that allocation or group contract — a unit that is provided at zero cost once the minimum paying-passenger threshold is met.
