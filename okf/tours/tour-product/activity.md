> A bookable in-destination product — a tour, class, attraction admission, or experience — sold to travelers separately from transport and accommodation.

"Activities" (also "experiences" or "tours & activities") form the third major travel category after flights and hotels. Each activity is a discrete product with a date and often a timeslot, capacity, per-person price tiers (e.g. adult/child), a meeting or pickup point, duration, and a cancellation policy. They range from guided city tours and museum admissions to cooking classes, adventure sports, and day trips, and are distributed both directly by operators and through experiences marketplaces.

**한국어 / Korean** — **액티비티(Activity)** — 교통·숙박과 별개로 여행자에게 판매되는 예약 가능한 목적지 내 상품으로, 투어·클래스·어트랙션 입장·체험 등을 가리킨다.

"액티비티"(또는 "익스피리언스", "투어·액티비티")는 항공·호텔에 이은 여행의 세 번째 주요 카테고리다. 각 액티비티는 날짜와 (대개) 시간대, 정원, 인원별 가격(예: 성인/아동), 미팅·픽업 지점, 소요시간, 취소 정책을 가진 개별 상품이다. 가이드 시티투어·박물관 입장부터 쿠킹 클래스·어드벤처 스포츠·당일 투어까지 다양하며, 운영사 직접 판매와 익스피리언스 마켓플레이스를 통한 유통이 모두 이뤄진다.

# Distinctions
- **Activity** vs Shore Excursion — A shore excursion is an activity packaged and sold against a cruise port of call (often with guaranteed return to the ship); an activity is the same kind of in-destination experience sold independently of any cruise.
- **Activity** vs Ancillary Service — An airline ancillary is an extra attached to a flight (bag, seat, meal); an activity is a standalone destination product not tied to a transport segment.
