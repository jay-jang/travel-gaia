> Inclusions are the specific services, items, or experiences that are explicitly covered by the advertised price of a tour, activity, or package — such as transport, meals, admission tickets, guide services, or equipment — forming the defined scope of what a traveler receives. The reciprocal term is exclusions (what is not covered). Clear disclosure of inclusions is a key consumer-protection requirement under package-travel regulations.

The inclusion list is a mandatory element of tour and package product description. Typical inclusions for a guided tour might be: licensed guide, coach transfer, entrance fees to listed attractions, a set lunch or dinner, and insurance. Operators must be explicit about what is included to avoid chargebacks and complaints. Under the EU Package Travel Directive (PTD) and equivalent regulations, pre-contractual information must describe inclusions clearly and changes to inclusions constitute a significant change that may entitle the traveler to cancel without penalty. In distribution, inclusion data is exchanged digitally as part of the product content feed — on platforms using the OCTO specification, inclusions appear in the product content object alongside exclusions, know-before-you-go notes, and accessibility information. The completeness and accuracy of inclusion descriptions directly affects conversion rates on experience marketplaces and OTAs.

**한국어 / Korean** — **포함 사항(Inclusions)** — 포함 사항은 투어, 액티비티, 또는 패키지의 광고 가격에 명시적으로 포함된 서비스·물품·체험의 목록이다. 교통, 식사, 입장권, 가이드 서비스, 장비 등이 해당하며, 여행자가 받게 되는 범위를 정의한다. 반대 개념은 제외 사항(exclusions, 포함되지 않는 것)이다. 포함 사항의 명확한 공개는 패키지 여행 규정에 따른 핵심 소비자 보호 요건이다.

포함 사항 목록은 투어·패키지 상품 설명의 필수 요소이다. 가이드 투어의 전형적인 포함 사항은 공인 가이드, 코치 이동, 명시된 어트랙션 입장료, 정해진 점심 또는 저녁, 보험 등이다. EU 패키지 여행 지침(PTD) 및 이에 상응하는 규정에 따라 포함 사항의 변경은 여행자가 위약금 없이 취소할 수 있는 중대한 변경 사항에 해당한다. OCTO 스펙을 사용하는 플랫폼에서는 포함 사항이 제외 사항, 사전 안내, 접근성 정보와 함께 상품 콘텐츠 객체에 포함된다.

# Distinctions
- **Inclusions** vs [Guided Tour](/tours/tour-product/guided-tour.md) — A guided tour is a product type (an escorted itinerary with a guide); inclusions are the list of services and items covered by the price of any tour product, including guided tours — a guided tour has inclusions, but inclusions are not themselves a type of tour.
- **Inclusions** vs [Voucher](/tours/tour-product/voucher.md) — A voucher is the confirmation/redemption document proving a booking has been made and paid; inclusions describe what services are delivered when that voucher is redeemed.
