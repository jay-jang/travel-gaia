> A FAM Trip (Familiarization Trip) is a complimentary or heavily subsidized hosted travel program organized by a destination, hotel, cruise line, tour operator, DMO, or tourism board for travel advisors, meeting planners, journalists, or content creators, providing first-hand product or destination experience with the expectation that participants will generate future sales, media coverage, or content.

FAM Trips serve a dual trade-marketing purpose: educating travel intermediaries (advisors, MICE planners) so they can confidently sell or recommend a product, and generating press coverage, social media content, or influencer posts. Host organizations cover accommodation, meals, transfers, and activities; participants are typically expected to produce post-trip deliverables (blog articles, social posts, agency bookings, or client recommendations). ASTA (American Society of Travel Advisors) and other trade bodies publish guidelines on FAM trip ethics, including disclosure obligations for advisors accepting hosted travel and minimum productivity requirements (e.g., a minimum number of bookings within six months). FAM trips exist in several formats: (a) Working FAMs focus on inspecting meeting venues or supplier facilities (often called site inspections in the MICE segment); (b) Experiential FAMs allow advisors to live the product as a guest would; (c) Award FAMs are offered to top-producing advisors as incentive recognition. A FAM trip organized specifically for journalists or content creators is typically called a press trip rather than a FAM, though the logistics are similar.

**한국어 / Korean** — **팸 트립(FAM Trip, 팸 투어)** — 팸 트립(FAM Trip, Familiarization Trip)은 목적지·호텔·크루즈선사·여행사·DMO·관광 기관이 여행 어드바이저, 행사 기획자, 언론인, 콘텐츠 크리에이터를 대상으로 조직하는 무상 또는 대폭 지원되는 초청 여행 프로그램으로, 참가자에게 상품이나 목적지에 대한 직접 체험을 제공하고, 그 대가로 향후 판매·미디어 보도·콘텐츠 생성을 기대한다.

FAM 트립은 트레이드 마케팅 목적을 이중으로 수행한다. 첫째, 여행 중개인(어드바이저, MICE 기획자)이 상품을 자신 있게 판매·추천할 수 있도록 교육하는 것이고, 둘째, 언론 보도·소셜 미디어 콘텐츠·인플루언서 게시물을 생성하는 것이다. 주최 기관은 숙박·식사·이동·활동 비용을 부담하며, 참가자는 일반적으로 사후 산출물(블로그 기사, 소셜 게시물, 여행사 예약, 고객 추천)을 제공할 것으로 기대된다. ASTA 및 기타 트레이드 단체는 공개 의무, 최소 생산성 요건 등 FAM 트립 윤리 지침을 게시하고 있다. FAM 트립 형태: (a) 실무형 FAM은 회의 장소나 시설 점검 중심(MICE 부문의 현장 답사), (b) 체험형 FAM은 실제 고객처럼 상품 경험, (c) 어워드 FAM은 최고 실적 어드바이저에 대한 인센티브. 언론인·콘텐츠 크리에이터 대상 FAM 트립은 운영 방식은 유사하지만 일반적으로 프레스 투어(press trip)라고 부른다.

# Distinctions
- **FAM Trip** vs [Receptive Tour Operator](/tours/tour-ops/receptive-tour-operator.md) — A Receptive Tour Operator is the ground-handling company that organizes and delivers inbound travel services for groups; a FAM Trip is a specific type of trade-marketing event that an RTO, hotel, cruise line, or DMO may organize to educate travel intermediaries. The RTO is a potential host or co-organizer of a FAM; the FAM is the event type itself.
- **FAM Trip** vs [Destination Management Company](/tours/tour-ops/destination-management-company.md) — A DMC is the local expert and ground-operations company at a destination; a FAM Trip is a marketing and education event that a DMC or other host may organize. DMCs frequently host FAM trips for travel advisors as a sales tool, but a FAM is the event the DMC organizes rather than what the DMC is.
