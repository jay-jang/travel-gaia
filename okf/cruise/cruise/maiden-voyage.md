> The Maiden Voyage of a cruise ship is the first revenue-producing sailing of a newly built vessel after delivery from the shipyard to the cruise line — the first cruise for which tickets are sold to the general public. It is commercially significant as a high-demand, premium-priced event and marks the formal debut of the ship in revenue service.

Prior to the maiden voyage, the cruise line typically operates one or more preview sailings (media cruises, travel-agent familiarisation trips) that are not open to the general public. The maiden voyage itself is the first publicly ticketed sailing and commands premium pricing due to novelty demand and limited availability. Bookings for maiden voyages often open during the Wave Season, when cruise lines launch new ships and promotion campaigns simultaneously. The term can also apply to a ship returning to service after a major refit or drydock, occasionally called a "re-inaugural" voyage. Inaugural sailings that follow the maiden voyage carry the name of the ship's deployment region (e.g. "maiden Caribbean voyage") and are technically the ship's first revenue sailing in that region. From a risk perspective, maiden voyages carry a higher probability of operational teething problems — unfinished outfitting, crew unfamiliarity — which some experienced cruisers factor into their booking decisions.

**한국어 / Korean** — **처녀 항해(Maiden Voyage)** — 처녀 항해(Maiden Voyage)는 신조 선박이 조선소에서 크루즈 선사에 인도된 후 최초로 판매되는 수익 항해로, 일반 대중에게 처음으로 티켓이 판매되는 크루즈이다. 수요가 높고 프리미엄 가격으로 책정되는 상업적으로 중요한 이벤트이며, 선박의 수익 운항 공식 데뷔를 의미한다.

처녀 항해 전에 크루즈 선사는 통상 일반에게 공개되지 않는 프리뷰 항해(언론 초청 크루즈, 여행사 팸 트립)를 한 차례 이상 운항한다. 처녀 항해 자체는 최초의 일반 티켓 판매 항해로, 신규성 수요와 제한된 가용 객실로 인해 프리미엄 가격이 적용된다. 처녀 항해 예약은 크루즈 선사들이 신규 선박과 프로모션 캠페인을 동시에 출시하는 Wave Season에 오픈되는 경우가 많다.

# Distinctions
- **Maiden Voyage** vs [Drydock](/cruise/cruise/drydock.md) — A Drydock is a scheduled maintenance or refurbishment period when a ship is taken out of service; a Maiden Voyage is the first revenue sailing after a ship is newly built and delivered, not a post-maintenance return.
- **Maiden Voyage** vs [Repositioning Cruise](/cruise/cruise/repositioning-cruise.md) — A Repositioning Cruise moves an existing ship from one region or homeport to another; a Maiden Voyage is the inaugural revenue sailing of a brand-new ship, regardless of itinerary.
