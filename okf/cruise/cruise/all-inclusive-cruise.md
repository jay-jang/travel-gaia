> A cruise pricing model in which one fare covers accommodation, all dining venues, non-alcoholic and typically alcoholic beverages, entertainment, gratuities/service charges, and often specialty services such as Wi-Fi, with minimal or no supplementary on-board charges. Primarily associated with the luxury cruise segment.

The all-inclusive (AI) cruise model is primarily associated with the luxury segment — operators such as Regent Seven Seas Cruises, Silversea, Seabourn, Viking Ocean Cruises, and Scenic market themselves as all-inclusive, meaning alcoholic beverages, specialty dining, shore excursions (at some lines), Wi-Fi, and crew gratuities are bundled into the cruise fare. Contemporary and premium lines often offer opt-in beverage packages while keeping specialty dining, gratuities, and excursions as separately priced add-ons. The AI model is distinct from à la carte pricing in which beverages packages, specialty dining, gratuities, and shore excursions are each priced separately. From a distribution standpoint, the AI fare simplifies pricing for travel advisors and guests but compresses the onboard revenue line, so cruise lines offset this through higher published cruise fares.

**한국어 / Korean** — **올인클루시브 크루즈(All-Inclusive Cruise)** — 숙박, 전 레스토랑 식사, 음료(주류 포함), 엔터테인먼트, 봉사료 등 주요 선상 비용을 하나의 운임에 포함하는 크루즈 요금 모델. 주로 럭셔리 크루즈 세그먼트와 연관된다.

올인클루시브(AI) 크루즈 모델은 주로 럭셔리 세그먼트와 연관된다 — Regent Seven Seas Cruises, Silversea, Seabourn, Viking Ocean Cruises, Scenic 등은 주류, 스페셜티 다이닝, 기항지 투어(일부 선사), Wi-Fi, 봉사료를 크루즈 운임에 포함한다. 컨템포러리·프리미엄 선사는 종종 음료 패키지를 선택형으로 제공하면서 스페셜티 다이닝·봉사료·기항지 투어는 별도 유료로 유지한다. AI 모델은 음료 패키지·스페셜티 다이닝·봉사료·기항지 투어를 각각 개별 가격으로 책정하는 à la carte 방식과 구별된다. 유통 관점에서 AI 운임은 여행사와 고객의 요금 이해를 단순화하지만 선상 수익을 압축하므로, 선사는 이를 높은 공시 크루즈 운임으로 상쇄한다.

# Distinctions
- **All-Inclusive Cruise** vs [Cruise-Only Fare](/cruise/cruise/cruise-only-fare.md) — A cruise-only fare covers transportation and accommodation on the ship but excludes flights, pre/post hotel, and most on-board extras; an all-inclusive cruise fare bundles the majority of on-board expenses — beverages, gratuities, specialty dining, and sometimes Wi-Fi and shore excursions — into one price.
- **All-Inclusive Cruise** vs [Beverages Package](/cruise/cruise/beverages-package.md) — A beverages package is an add-on sold separately on à la carte cruise lines to cover drinks; on an all-inclusive cruise, beverages (including premium spirits) are already bundled into the fare at no extra charge.
