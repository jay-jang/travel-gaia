> Port charges are government-levied taxes and port-authority fees assessed on a per-passenger basis at each port of call in a cruise itinerary, including head taxes, wharfage fees, docking fees, and pilotage costs; they are collected by the cruise line and passed through to the relevant authorities. These fees are typically itemised separately from the base cruise fare on booking invoices and are generally non-commissionable.

Port charges vary by port and itinerary: Caribbean and Alaskan head taxes are among the most commonly cited, but every port in a cruise itinerary contributes its own fee schedule covering harbour pilotage, light dues, passenger facility charges, and sometimes environmental levies. Cruise lines estimate port charges at the time of booking and guarantee the total amount; material changes in the published amount are disclosed and may result in a price adjustment. Port charges are a "pass-through" cost — the cruise line neither profits from nor discounts them — and are excluded from base fare calculations used for commissionable revenue and agent net remit. In marketing materials and search displays, "cruise fare" often refers to the base price excluding port charges and fuel supplements.

**한국어 / Korean** — **기항지 세금·항만 비용(Port Charges)** — 기항지 세금·항만 비용(Port Charges)은 크루즈 여정의 각 기항지에서 선객 1인당 부과되는 정부 세금 및 항만 당국 수수료로, 입항세·부두 사용료·접안료·도선사 비용 등을 포함하며 선사가 수취하여 해당 당국에 납부한다. 이 비용은 예약 인보이스에서 기본 크루즈 요금과 별도로 항목화되며, 일반적으로 커미션 대상이 아니다.

항만 비용은 기항지와 여정에 따라 상이하다. 카리브해와 알래스카의 입항세가 가장 흔히 거론되지만, 크루즈 여정의 모든 기항지는 항만 도선·등대세·여객 시설 요금·환경 부담금 등 자체 요금 체계를 가지고 있다. 선사는 예약 시점에 항만 비용을 추정하고 총액을 보장하며, 공표 금액의 중대한 변동 시 고지하고 요금을 조정할 수 있다. 항만 비용은 ''패스스루(pass-through)'' 비용으로, 선사가 이로부터 수익을 얻거나 할인하지 않으며, 커미션 대상 수익 산정에 사용되는 기본 요금 계산에서 제외된다.

# Distinctions
- **Port Charges** vs [Non-Commissionable Fare (NCF)](/cruise/cruise/non-commissionable-fare-ncf.md) — NCF is the portion of the total cruise price that is excluded from commission calculation, which typically includes port charges plus other non-commissionable line items; port charges are the specific government/port-authority pass-through component within the NCF.
- **Port Charges** vs [Fuel Supplement](/cruise/cruise/fuel-supplement.md) — Both port charges and fuel supplements are itemised additions to the base cruise fare; port charges are fixed government/port fees passed through to authorities, whereas a fuel supplement is a variable carrier surcharge triggered by high oil prices and retained by the cruise line.
- **Port Charges** vs [Daily Service Charge](/cruise/cruise/daily-service-charge.md) — The daily service charge (gratuity) is a per-day per-guest amount that compensates crew and is distributed internally by the cruise line; port charges are per-port government levies entirely passed through to external authorities and unrelated to crew compensation.
