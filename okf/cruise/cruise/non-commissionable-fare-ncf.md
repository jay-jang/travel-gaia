> The portion of a cruise fare — port charges, government fees, and other pass-through costs the line designates as non-commissionable — on which travel advisors earn no commission. Because commission is paid only on the remaining 'commissionable' fare, NCFs directly determine agency economics and how cruise prices are itemized to consumers.

Cruise lines split the total fare into a commissionable component and the NCF; advisors are paid their commission rate only on the former, so a high NCF can sharply cut effective earnings even when the headline commission percentage looks generous. The exact contents of NCFs (port dues, piloting, immigration, waste, administrative fees) and their disclosure have long been criticized as opaque. Policies vary and are shifting: some lines (e.g. Viking, American Cruise Lines) pay commission on port charges, and Norwegian Cruise Line announced the permanent elimination of NCFs — effective for bookings made from 26 December 2025 and applying to all sailings departing on or after 1 May 2026 — paying advisors on the full fare excluding only mandatory government taxes and fees.

**한국어 / Korean** — **비수수료 운임(Non-Commissionable Fare, NCF)** — 크루즈 운임 중 항만 사용료, 정부 부과금, 그 밖에 선사가 비수수료로 지정한 통과성 비용 등 여행 어드바이저가 수수료를 받지 못하는 부분이다. 수수료는 나머지 '수수료 대상(commissionable)' 운임에 대해서만 지급되므로, NCF는 대리점 수익 구조와 소비자에게 크루즈 가격을 항목화하는 방식을 직접 좌우한다.

크루즈 선사는 총 운임을 수수료 대상 부분과 NCF로 나누며, 어드바이저는 전자에 대해서만 수수료율을 적용받는다. 따라서 NCF가 크면 표면 수수료율이 후해 보여도 실효 수익이 크게 깎일 수 있다. NCF에 무엇이 들어가는지(항만세, 도선료, 출입국, 폐기물, 행정 수수료)와 그 공시 방식은 오랫동안 불투명하다는 비판을 받아왔다. 정책은 선사마다 다르고 변화 중이다. 일부 선사(예: Viking, American Cruise Lines)는 항만 사용료에도 수수료를 지급하며, Norwegian Cruise Line은 NCF를 영구 폐지한다고 발표했다. 이는 2025년 12월 26일 이후 예약분부터 적용되며, 2026년 5월 1일 이후 출발하는 모든 항해가 대상으로, 의무적 정부 세금·수수료만 제외한 전체 운임에 대해 어드바이저에게 수수료를 지급한다.

# Distinctions
- **Non-Commissionable Fare (NCF)** vs [Commissionable Rate](/lodging/hotel-dist/commissionable-rate.md) — A commissionable rate is the part of the fare on which the advisor does earn commission; the NCF is the carved-out remainder on which they earn nothing — together they make up the total cruise price.
- **Non-Commissionable Fare (NCF)** vs [Tax (YQ/YR)](/air/air-shop/tax-yq-yr.md) — Airline YQ/YR carrier-imposed charges are a fare-construction tax/surcharge concept in ATPCO filings; cruise NCFs are a commercial commission-exclusion concept — both reduce what reaches the seller, but NCF is defined by commissionability, not by tax codes.
- **Non-Commissionable Fare (NCF)** vs [Daily Service Charge](/cruise/cruise/daily-service-charge.md) — Both are non-fare amounts in a cruise quote, but NCF is the portion of the cruise price on which the line pays no agency commission, whereas the daily service charge is a separate gratuity collected from the guest for the crew and is not part of the fare at all.
