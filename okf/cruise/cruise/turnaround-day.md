> Turnaround Day is the homeport day when a cruise sailing ends and a new one begins in quick succession: disembarking passengers clear the ship while new passengers board, typically within a compressed window of 6–10 hours. It is one of the most operationally intensive events in cruise operations, requiring simultaneous provisioning, refuelling, waste management, deep cleaning, immigration/customs processing, and full cabin preparation.

Turnaround day begins before dawn as the ship docks and disembarkation commences by cabin colour or luggage-tag zone; cleared passengers collect pre-tagged luggage in the terminal, while porters, cleaners, provisioners, and fuelling crews board simultaneously. Customs and immigration inspection of the ship — and sometimes of all crew — may also occur on turnaround day per port-state or flag-state requirements. New passengers typically begin boarding in the early afternoon with access to staterooms when cabin preparation is complete, followed by the mandatory muster drill before departure. The homeport city, infrastructure, and labour resources significantly affect a cruise line's ability to execute efficient turnarounds, making turnaround-day logistics a key factor in ship scheduling decisions.

**한국어 / Korean** — **선박 전환일(Turnaround Day)** — 선박 전환일(Turnaround Day)은 한 크루즈 항해가 끝나고 새 항해가 빠르게 시작되는 모항 당일로, 하선 선객과 승선 선객이 보통 6~10시간 내에 교체된다. 보급·연료 보충·폐기물 처리·심층 청소·출입국/세관 처리·객실 전체 준비가 동시에 진행되는 크루즈 운영의 가장 집중적인 작업일이다.

선박 전환일은 선박 접안 및 하선 개시(객실 색상 또는 수하물 태그 구역별)와 함께 동트기 전부터 시작된다. 하선한 선객은 터미널에서 미리 태그된 수하물을 찾고, 그 사이 청소·보급·연료 보충 인원이 동시에 선박에 탑승한다. 항구국 또는 기국 요건에 따라 선박 전체 세관·출입국 검사 및 때로는 전체 승무원 검사도 전환일에 이루어진다. 신규 선객은 보통 오후 초에 탑승을 시작하며, 객실 준비가 완료되면 입실할 수 있고, 출항 전 의무 muster drill이 진행된다.

# Distinctions
- **Turnaround Day** vs [Embarkation](/cruise/cruise/embarkation.md) — Embarkation is the process of passengers boarding the ship at the start of a voyage; Turnaround Day is the full operational event that combines the prior voyage's disembarkation with the new voyage's embarkation, plus all ship servicing, in a single compressed port call.
- **Turnaround Day** vs [Port of Call](/cruise/cruise/port-of-call.md) — A port of call is a scheduled stop during a voyage where passengers go ashore and return; Turnaround Day is a homeport event that marks the end of one voyage and the beginning of the next — passengers do not return to the same ship, and the operational scope is far greater than a regular port day.
