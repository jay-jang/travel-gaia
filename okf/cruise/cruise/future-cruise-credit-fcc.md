> A cruise-line credit voucher with a set monetary value, applicable toward the purchase of a future sailing. FCCs are issued when a booking is cancelled (often under flexible/cancel-and-rebook programs) or as goodwill compensation; the value is typically based on the cruise fare paid less taxes, fees and add-ons, is non-refundable to cash, and carries an expiration date and book-by/sail-by conditions.

Future Cruise Credits are central to cruise cancellation, rebooking and retention workflows. When a guest cancels (or a line cancels a sailing), value that would otherwise be forfeited or refunded is instead issued as an FCC tied to the guest and usually to the issuing brand; redeeming it normally still requires paying the new booking's deposit. Lines differ on partial use — some require the full FCC to be applied at once (forfeiting any unused balance), others apply a chosen amount and reissue the remainder. Taxes, port fees and prepaid extras are typically refunded separately rather than rolled into the FCC. Because FCCs lock revenue in-house and defer rather than refund cash, they became a primary liquidity and rebooking instrument industry-wide, especially around the 'cruise with confidence' style flexible-cancellation programs. They are distinct in mechanics and governance from the airline's EMD/MCO, which are formal interline-settled accountable documents rather than brand-locked promotional credits.

**한국어 / Korean** — **퓨처 크루즈 크레딧 (Future Cruise Credit, FCC)** — 정해진 금전 가치를 가진 크루즈 선사 발행 크레딧 바우처로, 향후 출항 구매에 사용할 수 있다. FCC는 예약이 취소될 때(흔히 유연 취소·재예약 프로그램에 따라) 또는 보상 차원의 선의로 발행되며, 가치는 보통 지불한 크루즈 운임에서 세금·수수료·부가구매를 뺀 금액을 기준으로 한다. 현금 환불은 불가하며 만료일 및 예약·출항 기한 조건이 붙는다.

퓨처 크루즈 크레딧은 크루즈 취소·재예약·고객 유지 흐름의 핵심이다. 승객이 취소하거나(또는 선사가 출항을 취소하면) 원래 소멸·환불되었을 가치가 대신 FCC로 발행되어 해당 승객과 보통 발행 브랜드에 묶인다. 사용 시에도 일반적으로 신규 예약의 보증금은 별도로 지불해야 한다. 부분 사용 정책은 선사마다 달라, 일부는 FCC 전액을 한 번에 적용하도록 요구해(미사용 잔액은 소멸) 처리하고, 일부는 원하는 금액만 적용하고 잔액을 재발행한다. 세금·항만 수수료·선결제 부가상품은 대개 FCC에 합쳐지지 않고 별도 환불된다. FCC는 수익을 사내에 묶고 현금 환불 대신 이연시키므로, 특히 'cruise with confidence' 류의 유연 취소 프로그램을 중심으로 업계 전반의 핵심 유동성·재예약 수단이 되었다. 이는 정식 인터라인 정산 회계 문서인 항공의 EMD/MCO와는 메커니즘과 거버넌스가 다르며, FCC는 브랜드에 묶인 프로모션성 크레딧이다.

# Distinctions
- **Future Cruise Credit (FCC)** vs [EMD](/air/air-ticket/emd.md) — An EMD is an IATA accountable document settled through BSP/ARC that holds value for airline ancillaries or residual ticket value and is interline-recognized; an FCC is a single-brand cruise promotional credit with no industry settlement, redeemable only against that line's future sailings and not convertible to cash.
- **Future Cruise Credit (FCC)** vs [Onboard Credit (OBC)](/cruise/cruise/onboard-credit-obc.md) — An OBC can only be spent onboard during a sailing and expires at the end of that cruise; an FCC is applied against the fare of a future booking before sailing. OBC reduces onboard spend; FCC reduces the cost of the next cruise itself.
- **Future Cruise Credit (FCC)** vs [Refund](/air/air-ticket/refund.md) — A refund returns money to the original form of payment; an FCC deliberately substitutes a brand-locked, time-limited credit for that cash, keeping the value inside the cruise line. Many flexible programs offer an FCC of equal or greater value in lieu of a cash refund.
