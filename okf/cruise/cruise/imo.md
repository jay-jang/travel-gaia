> The International Maritime Organization (IMO) is the United Nations specialized agency responsible for regulating international shipping for safety, security, and environmental protection. Established by the IMO Convention (adopted 1948, entered into force 1958) and headquartered in London, IMO adopts and administers the primary maritime conventions binding on its member states, including SOLAS (safety), MARPOL (pollution), and the MLC 2006 (seafarer welfare).

IMO currently has 176 member states and 3 associate members. Its work is conducted through five principal committees: the Maritime Safety Committee (MSC), the Marine Environment Protection Committee (MEPC), the Legal Committee, the Technical Co-operation Committee, and the Facilitation Committee. Conventions are adopted by the IMO Assembly and brought into force under the "tacit acceptance" procedure, under which an amendment enters into force automatically unless a specified number of contracting parties object within a set period. For the cruise industry, the most consequential IMO conventions are SOLAS (ship construction and life-saving), MARPOL (emissions limits including the 2020 sulphur cap and 2023 GHG strategy), and the ISM Code (safety management systems). IMO also maintains the GISIS (Global Integrated Shipping Information System) database and publishes IMO numbers — unique seven-digit vessel identifiers assigned at time of build and retained for the life of the ship.

**한국어 / Korean** — **국제해사기구(IMO)** — 국제해사기구(IMO, International Maritime Organization)는 국제 해운의 안전·보안·환경 보호를 규율하는 유엔 전문기구다. 1948년 채택되어 1958년 발효된 IMO 협약에 의해 설립되고 런던에 본부를 두며, 회원국에 구속력 있는 SOLAS(안전), MARPOL(오염), MLC 2006(선원 복지) 등 주요 해사 협약을 채택·관리한다.

IMO는 현재 176개 회원국과 3개 준회원을 보유한다. 업무는 해사안전위원회(MSC), 해양환경보호위원회(MEPC), 법률위원회, 기술협력위원회, 원활화위원회 등 5개 주요 위원회에서 수행된다. 협약은 IMO 총회에서 채택되고 "묵시적 수락" 절차에 따라 발효되는데, 지정된 수의 당사국이 정해진 기간 내에 이의를 제기하지 않으면 개정안이 자동으로 발효된다. 크루즈 산업에 가장 큰 영향을 미치는 IMO 협약은 SOLAS(선박 건조·인명 구조), MARPOL(2020년 황 함량 상한 및 2023년 온실가스 전략을 포함한 배출 한도), ISM 코드(안전 관리 시스템)이다. IMO는 또한 GISIS(전 세계 통합 해운 정보 시스템) 데이터베이스를 관리하며, 건조 시 부여되어 선박 존속 기간 내내 유지되는 고유 7자리 선박 식별 번호인 IMO 번호를 발급한다.

# Distinctions
- **IMO** vs [SOLAS](/cruise/cruise/solas.md) — IMO is the United Nations agency that develops and administers international maritime conventions; SOLAS is one specific convention adopted under IMO's auspices governing ship safety standards.
- **IMO** vs [MARPOL](/cruise/cruise/marpol.md) — IMO is the regulatory body that promulgates international shipping rules; MARPOL is the specific IMO convention addressing prevention of marine and atmospheric pollution from ships.
- **IMO** vs [CLIA (Cruise Lines International Association)](/cruise/cruise/clia.md) — IMO is a United Nations intergovernmental body that sets mandatory international standards binding on flag states; CLIA is a private industry trade association that represents cruise lines and advocates before regulators, including IMO.
