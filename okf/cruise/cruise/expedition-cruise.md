> A cruise to remote or wilderness destinations — such as Antarctica, the Arctic, the Galapagos Islands, or the Amazon — aboard small, purpose-built ships (typically under 200 passengers) equipped for off-the-beaten-path navigation and designed for immersive exploration led by onboard naturalists and scientists. CLIA and IAATO recognize expedition cruising as a distinct, fast-growing segment.

Expedition cruises differ from conventional ocean or river cruises in destination philosophy, vessel design, and onboard expertise. Vessels are engineered for remote navigation — many carry Polar Class ice ratings (e.g., PC6, PC5) enabling Antarctic or Arctic operations — and deploy Zodiac inflatable craft and kayaks to land guests on beaches, glaciers, or remote coastlines where no pier infrastructure exists. Passenger capacities typically range from 12 to 200, though a growing midsize segment carries 200–500. Itineraries are shaped by weather and wildlife rather than a fixed schedule; expedition captains and expedition leaders hold authority to reroute or cancel landings for safety reasons. Onboard enrichment staff — naturalists, glaciologists, ornithologists, historians, and divers — deliver lectures, citizen-science programs, and guided landings. CLIA tracks expedition cruising as a named segment: global expedition ship capacity is projected to grow 150% from 2019 to 2029, and passenger numbers rose 22% year-on-year from 2023 to 2024. For Antarctic voyages, operators must be members of IAATO, which classifies vessels by passenger capacity and landing permission. The Guest Ticket Contract for expedition cruises typically contains explicit itinerary-flexibility clauses, adverse-weather provisions, and conservation-compliance obligations under the Antarctic Treaty System.

**한국어 / Korean** — **익스페디션 크루즈(Expedition Cruise)** — 남극·북극·갈라파고스 제도·아마존 등 오지 또는 자연 야생 지역에 특화된 소형 선박(통상 200명 미만)으로 운항하며, 자연학자·과학자 등 전문가가 동반하는 탐험 중심의 크루즈. CLIA와 IAATO는 익스페디션 크루즈를 빠르게 성장하는 독자적 세그먼트로 인정한다.

익스페디션 크루즈는 목적지 철학, 선박 설계, 승선 전문성 면에서 일반 오션 크루즈나 리버 크루즈와 구별된다. 선박은 원격 항해를 위해 설계되며, 많은 경우 남극·북극 운항을 가능케 하는 극지 등급 빙급 규격을 갖추고, 조디악 보트와 카약을 운용한다. 승객 정원은 통상 12~200명이며, CLIA에 따르면 2019~2029년 수용량 150% 성장이 예상된다. 남극 항해의 경우 운영사는 IAATO 회원이어야 한다.

# Distinctions
- **Expedition Cruise** vs [River Cruise](/cruise/cruise/river-cruise.md) — A river cruise follows fixed cultural routes on navigable inland waterways with established port infrastructure; an expedition cruise reaches uninhabited or remote wilderness destinations with no pier, often requiring ice-class vessels and small-boat landings.
- **Expedition Cruise** vs [Shore Excursion](/cruise/cruise/shore-excursion.md) — In conventional ocean cruising a shore excursion is a structured tour sold for a mainstream port of call; in expedition cruising the equivalent "landing" is a guided wildlife or wilderness experience that may have no formal port at all and is subject to weather and wildlife conditions.
