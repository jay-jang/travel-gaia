> The Maritime Labour Convention 2006 (MLC 2006) is ILO Convention No. 186, adopted in 2006 and entered into force on 20 August 2013, that consolidates virtually all existing ILO maritime labour instruments into a single, comprehensive set of minimum standards covering seafarers' working and living conditions, social protection, and compliance mechanisms. Often called the "Seafarers' Bill of Rights," it requires cruise ships and other commercial vessels of 500 gross tons or more engaged on international voyages to carry a Maritime Labour Certificate and a Declaration of Maritime Labour Compliance.

MLC 2006 is structured into five Titles: Title 1 sets minimum age, medical fitness, training, and recruitment/placement standards; Title 2 governs seafarer employment agreements (SEAs), wages, hours of work and rest, paid leave, repatriation, and compensation for ship loss; Title 3 addresses accommodation, recreational facilities, food and catering; Title 4 covers medical care on board and ashore, health and safety, and access to welfare facilities; Title 5 establishes the compliance and enforcement regime — flag State certification, port State control inspections, and on-board complaint procedures. Cruise lines are substantial MLC stakeholders because they employ large crews; MLC directly shapes collective bargaining agreements, crew-to-guest ratios, rest-hour records, and guest-contract disclosures around crew welfare. The Port State Control inspection right under MLC Title 5 means that non-compliant cruise ships may be detained in any ratifying nation's port. As of 2024, MLC 2006 has been ratified by states representing over 97% of world shipping tonnage.

MLC 2006는 5개 표제(Title)로 구성된다. 표제 1은 최저 연령·건강 적합성·훈련·모집 배치 기준을, 표제 2는 선원 고용계약(SEA)·임금·근로·휴식 시간·유급휴가·본국 송환·선박 손실 보상을, 표제 3은 거처·레저 시설·식사·취사를, 표제 4는 선상·항만 내 의료 서비스·보건·안전·복지 시설 이용을, 표제 5는 이행·집행 체계(기국 증서, 항만국 통제 검사, 선내 고충 절차)를 규율한다. 크루즈 선사는 대규모 승무원을 고용하므로 MLC의 주요 당사자이며, MLC는 단체협약·승객 대비 승무원 비율·휴식시간 기록·승무원 복지 관련 게스트 계약 공시에 직접적인 영향을 미친다. MLC 표제 5의 항만국 통제 검사권으로 인해 불이행 크루즈선은 비준국 어느 항구에서든 억류될 수 있다. 2024년 현재 세계 선박 총톤수의 97% 이상을 보유한 국가들이 MLC 2006을 비준했다.

# Distinctions
- **Maritime Labour Convention 2006 (MLC 2006)** vs [SOLAS](/cruise/cruise/solas.md) — SOLAS (International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea) governs vessel construction, equipment, and operational safety to protect everyone on board, including passengers; MLC 2006 specifically governs the labor rights, welfare, and working conditions of the seafarers who crew those vessels. They are complementary "pillars" of international maritime law — SOLAS for safety, MLC for seafarer labor standards.
