> The Cruise Lines International Association (CLIA) is the world's largest cruise industry trade association, representing more than 50 ocean, river, and expedition cruise lines as well as travel agencies, ports, destinations, and maritime service providers. CLIA advocates for the industry before international and national governments, provides travel agent training and accreditation through the CLIA Academy, and publishes the authoritative annual State of the Cruise Industry Outlook.

CLIA was founded in 1975 and is headquartered in Washington, DC with offices in Brussels, London, and other regions. Its member cruise lines carry over 90% of ocean cruise passengers globally. CLIA runs the CLIA Academy, which certifies travel agents as Accredited Cruise Counsellors (ACC), Master Cruise Counsellors (MCC), or Elite Cruise Counsellors (ECC). Its annual State of the Cruise Industry Outlook provides globally cited data on passenger volumes, ship deployment, market trends, and economic impact. CLIA also leads industry positions on environmental regulations (including the IMO's CII and EEXI), port policy, and health protocols.

**한국어 / Korean** — **크루즈라인 국제협회(CLIA)** — CLIA(Cruise Lines International Association)는 세계 최대의 크루즈 산업 무역협회로, 50개 이상의 오션·리버·원정 크루즈 선사와 여행사·항구·목적지·해운 서비스 공급자를 대표한다. CLIA는 국제 및 각국 정부에 대해 업계를 대변하고, CLIA 아카데미를 통해 여행사 교육 및 인증 자격 부여를 제공하며, 권위 있는 연례 크루즈 산업 전망(State of the Cruise Industry Outlook) 보고서를 발간한다.

CLIA는 1975년에 설립되어 워싱턴 DC에 본부를 두고 브뤼셀, 런던 등에 지역 사무소를 운영한다. 회원 선사들은 전 세계 오션 크루즈 승객의 90% 이상을 수송한다. CLIA 아카데미는 여행사를 ACC(Accredited Cruise Counsellor), MCC(Master Cruise Counsellor), ECC(Elite Cruise Counsellor)로 인증한다. 연례 State of the Cruise Industry Outlook 보고서는 승객 수·선박 배치·시장 동향·경제적 파급 효과에 관한 글로벌 기준 데이터를 제공한다. CLIA는 또한 IMO의 CII·EEXI 등 환경 규제, 항만 정책, 건강 프로토콜에 관한 업계 입장을 주도한다.

# Distinctions
- **CLIA (Cruise Lines International Association)** vs [Shore Excursion](/cruise/cruise/shore-excursion.md) — Shore Excursion is a product type (an escorted port-of-call tour); CLIA is the trade association that represents the cruise lines that sell shore excursions, among many other industry roles.
