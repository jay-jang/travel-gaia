> The Cruise Director is the senior entertainment and activities officer on a cruise ship, responsible for planning, hosting, and managing all onboard entertainment, leisure programs, and guest social events throughout the voyage. The role is a key guest-satisfaction driver and the public face of onboard hospitality recognized by CLIA as a distinct operational position.

The Cruise Director leads the entertainment staff — comprising social hosts/hostesses, activity coordinators, youth counselors, and performers — and is typically the MC for major shows, game nights, theme parties, and departure/arrival ceremonies. Core responsibilities include: programming the daily activity schedule (published in the ship's Daily Programme); coordinating shore-excursion briefings and debarkation logistics; liaising with the captain and hotel director on guest experience standards; managing the entertainment budget; and serving as the primary point of contact for guest feedback and complaints escalated beyond front-desk level. On large contemporary ships, the Cruise Director appears across multiple venues simultaneously via pre-recorded videos and live broadcasts on the in-cabin TV system. The role carries significant commercial weight: positive on-board experiences and a charismatic Cruise Director have been shown in industry research to correlate with repeat booking rates and on-board spend, particularly on Shore Excursion products and specialty dining.

**한국어 / Korean** — **크루즈 디렉터(Cruise Director)** — 크루즈 디렉터는 크루즈 선박의 오락·액티비티 담당 수석 책임자로, 항해 전반에 걸쳐 선상 엔터테인먼트, 레저 프로그램, 게스트 소셜 이벤트의 기획·진행·관리를 담당한다. 이 역할은 선상 환대의 공식 얼굴이자 게스트 만족도의 핵심 동인이며, CLIA는 이를 독립된 운영 직책으로 인정한다.

크루즈 디렉터는 소셜 호스트/호스티스, 액티비티 코디네이터, 청소년 카운슬러, 퍼포머로 구성된 엔터테인먼트 직원을 이끌며, 대형 공연, 게임 나이트, 테마 파티, 출항/입항 행사의 MC 역할을 맡는 경우가 많다. 핵심 업무로는 일일 액티비티 일정 기획(선상 데일리 프로그램에 게재), 기항지 투어 브리핑 및 하선 물류 조정, 게스트 경험 기준 관련 선장·호텔 디렉터와의 협력, 엔터테인먼트 예산 관리가 포함된다. 긍정적인 선상 경험과 카리스마 있는 크루즈 디렉터는 재예약률 및 선상 지출과 상관관계가 있는 것으로 업계 연구에서 밝혀졌다.

# Distinctions
- **Cruise Director** vs [Shore Excursion](/cruise/cruise/shore-excursion.md) — A shore excursion is a port-of-call tour product sold to guests; the Cruise Director manages onboard programming and coordinates shore-excursion briefings but does not operate the excursions themselves.
- **Cruise Director** vs [Daily Service Charge](/cruise/cruise/daily-service-charge.md) — The Daily Service Charge (gratuity) is automatically billed to every guest account and distributed among hotel, dining, and entertainment crew — including the entertainment staff led by the Cruise Director; the Cruise Director is the supervisor of those staff, not the gratuity system itself.
