> An itinerary in which a single ship circumnavigates the globe — or traverses a near-complete circuit of the world's oceans — typically over 100–120 or more days, calling at 30–50+ ports across multiple continents. World cruises are the flagship luxury product of major cruise lines and command the highest per-berth price points in the industry.

A world cruise is defined by geographic scope (circumnavigation or near-circumnavigation), voyage length (typically 100 days or more), and a luxury positioning that places it at the apex of cruise pricing. Itineraries typically depart from a Northern Hemisphere homeport in January or February, traversing the Atlantic, Pacific, and Indian Oceans and returning in April–May. Port calls span multiple continents, and partial "sector" or "grand voyage" segments allow passengers to join or leave at intermediate ports. Revenue concentration is high: per-passenger fares typically exceed USD 30,000 and can reach six figures for luxury suites. Wave Season (January–March) coincides with peak world-cruise booking and departure windows. CLIA reports that world-cruise products attract the highest net promoter scores of any cruise segment, with repeat-passenger rates exceeding 80% on some sailings.

**한국어 / Korean** — **월드 크루즈(World Cruise)** — 단일 선박이 지구를 일주하거나 세계 주요 대양을 광범위하게 순항하는 여정으로, 통상 100~120일 이상 운항하며 5대륙의 30~50개 이상 항구를 기항한다. 월드 크루즈는 주요 선사의 최고급 플래그십 상품으로, 산업 내 최고 수준의 선실 단가를 형성한다.

월드 크루즈는 지리적 범위, 항해 기간, 고가 포지셔닝을 특징으로 한다. 전 구간 탑승이 어려운 승객을 위한 구간(섹터) 또는 그랜드 보야지 상품도 별도로 판매된다. Wave Season(1~3월)이 월드 크루즈 예약과 출항의 최성수기와 겹치며, CLIA에 따르면 반복 탑승객 비율이 일부 항차에서 80%를 초과한다.

**Aliases:** `Grand Voyage`, `Grand World Voyage`, `Around-the-World Cruise`, `Global Voyage`, `Circumnavigation Cruise`

# Distinctions
- **World Cruise** vs [Repositioning Cruise](/cruise/cruise/repositioning-cruise.md) — A repositioning cruise moves a ship one-way between two homeports, typically lasting 10–20 days, and is priced at a discount to standard sailings; a world cruise is a prestige round-trip circumnavigation of 100+ days at premium pricing.
