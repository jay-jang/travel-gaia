> The binding contract of carriage between a cruise guest and the carrier that governs the entire passage — incorporating international liability regimes (notably the Athens Convention 1974/PAL and, in the EU, Regulation 392/2009), and setting out cancellation/refund terms, the right to impose surcharges, forum-selection and time-bar clauses, and limits on carrier liability for injury, death and baggage. It is the cruise analogue of an airline's conditions of carriage.

Issued per booking (often titled 'Cruise/Cruisetour Ticket Contract' or 'Passage Contract'), the guest ticket contract is the master legal instrument of the cruise relationship and acceptance of its terms is a condition of boarding. It typically incorporates the Athens Convention Relating to the Carriage of Passengers and their Luggage by Sea (1974) and its protocols, capping carrier liability for passenger death/injury and for baggage in Special Drawing Rights (SDR), and within the EU it gives effect to Regulation (EC) No 392/2009. The contract also fixes commercial terms a distributor must surface: cancellation-fee schedules and refund mechanics, the carrier's reserved right to impose fuel supplements or other surcharges, itinerary-change and substitution rights, medical/fitness-to-sail requirements, mandatory arbitration or exclusive forum and choice-of-law clauses, and strict notice and suit time limits. Because it overrides marketing materials and binds the guest, the ticket contract is the controlling document behind cruise cancellation policies, surcharges and liability — functionally parallel to (but legally grounded in maritime conventions rather than air law such as the Montreal Convention) an airline's conditions of carriage.

**한국어 / Korean** — **게스트 티켓 컨트랙트(Guest Ticket Contract) / 승선 계약** — 크루즈 승객과 운송인 간의 구속력 있는 운송 계약으로 항해 전반을 규율한다. 국제 책임 체계(특히 1974년 Athens Convention/PAL, EU에서는 Regulation 392/2009)를 편입하고, 취소·환불 조건, 할증료 부과 권리, 재판관할 선택 및 제소기간(time-bar) 조항, 상해·사망·수하물에 대한 운송인 책임 한도를 규정한다. 항공사의 운송약관(conditions of carriage)에 해당하는 크루즈 측 문서다.

예약 건별로 발행되며(흔히 'Cruise/Cruisetour Ticket Contract' 또는 'Passage Contract'로 명명) 크루즈 관계의 기본 법률 문서로서, 약관 동의가 승선의 조건이 된다. 일반적으로 1974년 해상 여객 및 수하물 운송에 관한 Athens Convention과 그 의정서를 편입하여 승객 사망·상해 및 수하물에 대한 운송인 책임을 SDR(Special Drawing Rights)로 제한하며, EU 내에서는 Regulation (EC) No 392/2009을 시행한다. 또한 유통업자가 반드시 고지해야 하는 상업 조건을 정한다. 즉 취소 수수료 일정과 환불 방식, 운송인이 유보한 fuel supplement·기타 할증료 부과 권리, 일정 변경·대체 권리, 의료·승선 적합성 요건, 강제 중재 또는 전속 관할·준거법 조항, 엄격한 통지·제소 기간이 그것이다. 마케팅 자료에 우선하며 승객을 구속하므로, 티켓 컨트랙트는 크루즈 취소 정책·할증료·책임의 지배 문서로서 항공사의 운송약관과 기능적으로 평행하다(다만 Montreal Convention 같은 항공법이 아니라 해사 협약에 법적 근거를 둔다).

# Distinctions
- **Guest Ticket Contract** vs [Montreal Convention 1999](/common/disruption/montreal-convention-1999.md) — The Montreal Convention governs air carrier liability for international carriage of passengers and baggage; the cruise guest ticket contract instead incorporates the maritime Athens Convention 1974/PAL (and EU Reg. 392/2009). Both cap carrier liability in SDR, but they are separate legal regimes for air versus sea, and the cruise contract is the document that imports the maritime regime into the booking.
- **Guest Ticket Contract** vs [Cancellation Policy](/lodging/hotel-dist/cancellation-policy.md) — A cancellation policy is one set of commercial terms; the guest ticket contract is the overarching legal document that contains the cancellation policy along with liability limits, surcharge rights, forum-selection and time-bar provisions. The cancellation policy is binding because the ticket contract incorporates it.
