> A US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) program that conducts twice-yearly unannounced sanitation inspections of ocean-going passenger vessels with 13 or more passengers calling at US ports, assigning a score out of 100. A score of 86 or above indicates a satisfactory sanitation status; inspection results are publicly available on the CDC VSP website.

The VSP was established in 1975 following a series of gastrointestinal illness outbreaks on cruise ships, and it focuses on water safety, food preparation and storage, general cleanliness, and outbreak response. Each inspection uses the VSP Operations Manual as its scoring rubric; inspectors examine water distribution, swimming pools and recreational water facilities, galleys, crew and passenger dining areas, potable water bunkering, and medical facilities. Ships scoring below 86 must undergo re-inspection. The program also manages outbreak investigations under the Vessel Sanitation Program outbreak response protocol. Cruise lines routinely publicise their VSP scores as a quality signal, and some publish live scores via their websites.

**한국어 / Korean** — **선박 위생 프로그램(VSP)** — 미국 CDC가 미국 항구에 기항하는 원양 여객선에 대해 연 2회 예고 없이 실시하는 위생 점검 프로그램으로, 100점 만점에 86점 이상이면 위생 적합으로 판정하며 결과는 CDC 웹사이트에 공개된다.

VSP는 크루즈선 위장관 질환 집단 발병이 잇따른 1975년 설립되었으며, 물 안전, 식품 조리·보관, 전반적 청결, 집단 발병 대응에 초점을 맞춘다. VSP 운영 매뉴얼을 채점 기준으로 사용하며, 86점 미만 선박은 재점검을 받아야 한다. 선사들은 VSP 점수를 품질 지표로 널리 홍보한다.

# Distinctions
- **Vessel Sanitation Program (VSP)** vs [SOLAS](/cruise/cruise/solas.md) — SOLAS is the international IMO treaty setting fire safety, lifeboat, and structural standards for vessel design and operation; VSP is a US domestic public-health inspection program covering food, water, and sanitation on passenger vessels calling at US ports.
- **Vessel Sanitation Program (VSP)** vs [Maritime Labour Convention 2006 (MLC 2006)](/cruise/cruise/mlc-2006.md) — MLC 2006 is the ILO treaty establishing minimum labour rights and living standards for seafarers worldwide; VSP focuses on passenger-facing food and water hygiene. VSP scores reflect sanitation quality for guests; MLC protects crew welfare.
