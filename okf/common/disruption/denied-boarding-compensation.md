> The compensation owed to a passenger holding a confirmed reservation who is involuntarily refused carriage because a flight is oversold. It is defined under US DOT 14 CFR Part 250 (fare-based percentages with dollar caps) and under EU261 (fixed distance-band amounts), and is the monetary remedy for involuntary denied boarding.

In the US, 14 CFR 250.5 sets DBC at 200% of the one-way fare (capped at USD 1,075) when the substitute flight arrives more than one hour but no more than two hours late domestically (four hours internationally), and 400% (capped at USD 2,150) for longer delays; no DBC is due if re-accommodation arrives within one hour. These caps are revised periodically by the DOT for inflation. Under EU261, denied boarding compensation uses the same EUR 250/400/600 distance bands as cancellation compensation. DBC compensates only the involuntary refusal of carriage and is separate from refunds, duty-of-care expenses, and Montreal Convention baggage/delay damages.

**한국어 / Korean** — **탑승 거부 보상 (Denied Boarding Compensation, DBC)** — 항공편 초과 판매로 인해 확약된 예약을 보유한 승객이 비자발적으로 탑승을 거부당했을 때 지급되는 보상이다. 미국 DOT 14 CFR Part 250(운임 기반 비율 및 달러 상한)과 EU261(거리 구간별 정액)에 규정되어 있으며, involuntary denied boarding에 대한 금전적 구제 수단이다.

미국에서 14 CFR 250.5는 대체편이 국내선 기준 1시간 초과 2시간 이내(국제선 4시간 이내) 지연 도착할 경우 DBC를 편도 운임의 200%(상한 USD 1,075), 그보다 긴 지연의 경우 400%(상한 USD 2,150)로 정한다. 1시간 이내 재배정 도착 시 DBC 의무는 없다. 이 상한은 DOT가 물가에 맞춰 주기적으로 개정한다. EU261에서 denied boarding compensation은 취소 보상과 동일한 EUR 250/400/600 거리 구간을 사용한다. DBC는 비자발적 탑승 거부만을 보상하며, 환불, duty-of-care 비용, Montreal Convention 수하물·지연 배상과는 별개다.

# Distinctions
- **Denied Boarding Compensation** vs [Right to Care](/common/disruption/right-to-care.md) — DBC is a fixed cash penalty for involuntary bumping; the right to care covers meals, accommodation, and transport during the disruption and is owed in addition to, not instead of, DBC.
- **Denied Boarding Compensation** vs [Refund](/air/air-ticket/refund.md) — A refund returns the unused ticket value; DBC is a separate penalty paid on top of (or alongside re-routing of) the passenger regardless of any refund.
