> The Package Travel Directive (EU Directive 2015/2302, replacing 90/314/EEC) is the European Union law governing the sale of package holidays and linked travel arrangements. It grants travelers the rights to full information before booking, protection against insolvency of the organizer, the ability to transfer a booking, a price-increase cap and termination right, and remedy or a price reduction for non-conforming services. Its UK equivalent is The Package Travel and Linked Travel Arrangements Regulations 2018.

The Package Travel Directive (PTD) applies to packages combining at least two different types of travel services (transport, accommodation, car rental, or other tourist services) sold together or at an inclusive price, as well as to Linked Travel Arrangements (LTAs). Key traveler rights: (1) Pre-contractual information obligation; (2) Price revision cap at 8% — above which the traveler may terminate at no cost; (3) Transfer to another person; (4) Termination without penalty for unavoidable extraordinary circumstances affecting the destination; (5) Mandatory insolvency protection ensuring refunds or repatriation; (6) Remedy for non-conforming services or price reduction. The UK's Package Travel and Linked Travel Arrangements Regulations 2018 (SI 2018/634) implement equivalent protections.

**한국어 / Korean** — **패키지 여행 지침(Package Travel Directive)** — 패키지 여행 지침(EU 지침 2015/2302, 90/314/EEC 대체)은 패키지 여행 및 연계 여행 상품 판매를 규율하는 EU 법령이다. 예약 전 충분한 정보 제공, 여행 주최자 도산에 대한 보호, 예약 양도 권리, 가격 인상 상한 및 해지 권리, 불이행 서비스에 대한 구제 또는 가격 감액 권리를 여행자에게 부여한다.

패키지 여행 지침은 운송·숙박·렌터카 등 최소 두 종류의 여행 서비스를 함께 또는 일괄 가격으로 판매하는 패키지와 연계 여행 상품(LTA)에 적용된다. 여행자의 핵심 권리: (1) 계약 전 정보 제공 의무; (2) 가격 수정 8% 상한(초과 시 위약금 없이 해지 가능); (3) 제3자 양도; (4) 불가피한 이례적 상황으로 인한 위약금 없는 해지; (5) 환급 또는 귀환을 보장하는 의무적 도산 보호; (6) 불이행 서비스 시정 또는 가격 감액. 영국의 패키지 여행 규정 2018(SI 2018/634)이 동등한 보호를 이행한다.

# Distinctions
- **Package Travel Directive** vs [EU Regulation 261/2004](/common/disruption/eu-regulation-261-2004.md) — EU Regulation 261/2004 governs rights for flight disruptions (cancellations, delays, denied boarding) with fixed compensation per distance band; the Package Travel Directive governs the entire package holiday relationship — pre-sale information, price changes, insolvency protection, non-conformance, and termination. Both can apply simultaneously when a package flight is cancelled.
- **Package Travel Directive** vs [Supplier Default Coverage](/common/insurance/supplier-default-coverage.md) — Supplier Default Coverage is a voluntary travel insurance benefit purchased by the individual traveler to recoup prepaid costs if a supplier fails; the Package Travel Directive imposes a mandatory statutory obligation on the organizer to maintain insolvency protection that actively ensures refunds or repatriation without the traveler needing to claim on personal insurance.
- **Package Travel Directive** vs [ATOL](/common/insurance/atol.md) — ATOL is the UK's statutory licensing scheme that operationalizes insolvency protection specifically for air-inclusive packages sold in the UK — it is the mechanism by which UK-regulated tour operators meet the Package Travel Regulations' insolvency-protection obligation. The Package Travel Directive/Regulations is the overarching legal framework; ATOL is one national implementation mechanism.
