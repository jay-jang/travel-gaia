> Downgrade Compensation is the reimbursement owed to an air passenger who is involuntarily placed in a lower class of service than the one for which they purchased a ticket. Under EU Regulation 261/2004 Article 10, the operating carrier must pay 30%, 50%, or 75% of the one-way ticket price (depending on flight distance) within seven days; the percentage applies to the affected sector only.

EU Regulation 261/2004 Article 10 sets three distance-based reimbursement rates: 30% of the ticket price for flights of 1,500 km or less; 50% for all intra-EU flights over 1,500 km and all other flights between 1,500 km and 3,500 km; and 75% for flights over 3,500 km that are not intra-EU. The ticket price used is the price paid for the seat (excluding taxes and charges), applied only to the downgraded sector if the journey contains multiple legs. The reimbursement must be made within seven days by cash, bank transfer, bank order, or — with the passenger's signed agreement — travel vouchers. Downgrade compensation is distinct from denied boarding compensation (which applies when carriage is refused altogether) and from the duty of care and right to re-routing that apply to cancellations and long delays. The EU regulation applies when the operating carrier's flight departs from an EU airport, or arrives at an EU airport on an EU-licensed carrier. UK Regulation 261 provides equivalent rules post-Brexit for UK-departing flights.

**한국어 / Korean** — **강등 보상 (Downgrade Compensation)** — 강등 보상(Downgrade Compensation)은 구매한 좌석 등급보다 낮은 등급에 비자발적으로 배정된 항공 승객에게 지급되는 환급금이다. EU Regulation 261/2004 제10조에 따라, 운항 항공사는 비행 거리에 따라 편도 항공권 가격의 30%, 50%, 또는 75%를 7일 이내에 지급해야 하며, 이 비율은 해당 구간(sector)에만 적용된다.

EU Regulation 261/2004 제10조는 세 가지 거리 기반 환급율을 규정한다. 1,500 km 이하 항공편은 항공권 가격의 30%, EU 역내 1,500 km 초과 항공편 및 기타 1,500~3,500 km 항공편은 50%, EU 역내가 아닌 3,500 km 초과 항공편은 75%이다. 적용되는 항공권 가격은 (세금·수수료 제외) 좌석 구매 가격이며, 여정이 여러 구간으로 구성된 경우 강등된 구간에만 적용된다. 환급은 현금, 은행 이체, 은행 주문, 또는 승객의 서면 동의를 받은 여행 바우처로 7일 이내에 이루어져야 한다. 강등 보상은 탑승 자체가 거부될 때 적용되는 denied boarding compensation, 취소·장기 지연 시 발생하는 duty of care 및 재배정 권리와는 구별된다. EU 규정은 EU 공항을 출발하는 항공편 또는 EU 면허 항공사의 EU 도착 항공편에 적용된다. Brexit 이후 영국 출발 항공편에는 UK Regulation 261이 동등한 규정을 제공한다.

# Distinctions
- **Downgrade Compensation** vs [Denied Boarding Compensation](/common/disruption/denied-boarding-compensation.md) — Denied Boarding Compensation applies when the carrier refuses to carry the passenger at all (involuntary bumping from an oversold flight); Downgrade Compensation applies when the passenger is carried but in a lower cabin class than booked. Both are EU261 entitlements but arise from different circumstances and have different calculation rules.
- **Downgrade Compensation** vs [Right to Re-routing](/common/disruption/right-to-re-routing.md) — Right to Re-routing gives a passenger alternative travel arrangements to their final destination when a flight is cancelled or significantly delayed; Downgrade Compensation is a retroactive cash reimbursement after the passenger has already been carried in a lower class.
