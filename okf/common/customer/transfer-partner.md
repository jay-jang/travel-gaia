> A loyalty or financial program with which a points/miles currency holder has an affiliation allowing balances to be moved into the partner's program at a defined transfer ratio. Bank/credit-card rewards programs, airline frequent-flyer programs, and hotel programs link as transfer partners to make their currencies interchangeable.

Transfer partnerships are the core plumbing of coalition loyalty and transferable-points strategies: a member converts a flexible bank-points balance into airline miles or hotel points, often at 1:1 but sometimes at less favorable ratios, and occasionally with transfer bonuses. Transfers are typically one-directional (e.g. cards to airlines, not back) and may take minutes to days to post. This convertibility increases the redemption value and Mileage Accrual paths available to a member, while the unredeemed balances and projected transfers feed into a program's Breakage and liability estimates. Transfer relationships move spendable currency, distinct from a Status Match, which moves only tier recognition.

**한국어 / Korean** — **포인트 전환 파트너 (Transfer Partner)** — 포인트·마일 통화 보유자가 정해진 전환 비율로 잔액을 상대 프로그램으로 이동시킬 수 있도록 제휴 관계를 맺은 로열티 또는 금융 프로그램이다. 은행/신용카드 리워드 프로그램, 항공사 FFP, 호텔 프로그램이 서로 transfer partner로 연결되어 각자의 통화를 호환 가능하게 만든다.

전환 파트너십은 연합(coalition) 로열티와 전환 가능 포인트 전략의 핵심 배관이다. 회원은 유연한 은행 포인트 잔액을 항공 마일이나 호텔 포인트로 전환하는데, 흔히 1:1이지만 더 불리한 비율일 때도 있고 때때로 전환 보너스가 붙는다. 전환은 보통 단방향(예: 카드→항공, 역방향 불가)이며 적립까지 수 분에서 수 일이 걸릴 수 있다. 이 호환성은 회원이 이용할 수 있는 상환 가치와 Mileage Accrual 경로를 늘리며, 미상환 잔액과 예상 전환은 프로그램의 Breakage 및 부채 추정에 반영된다. 전환 관계는 사용 가능한 통화를 이동시키므로, 등급 인정만 이동시키는 Status Match와 구별된다.

# Distinctions
- **Transfer Partner** vs [Mileage Accrual](/common/customer/mileage-accrual.md) — Mileage Accrual is earning miles from a member's own flying or spend; a transfer from a Transfer Partner adds miles by converting a balance held in a different program's currency.
- **Transfer Partner** vs [Status Match](/common/customer/status-match.md) — A Transfer Partner moves redeemable points/miles between programs; a Status Match moves elite tier recognition, not currency.
- **Transfer Partner** vs [Co-branded Credit Card](/common/customer/co-branded-credit-card.md) — A co-branded credit card is a single card tied to one program where spend earns that program's currency directly; a transfer partner is a relationship that lets points move between a flexible bank currency and a loyalty program. Many cards are both branded co-brands and feed transferable points, but the two concepts describe different mechanics.
