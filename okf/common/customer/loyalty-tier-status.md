> The graded level a member holds within a Frequent Flyer Program (e.g. Silver, Gold, Platinum), earned through qualifying activity and conferring escalating benefits such as priority services, lounge access, extra baggage and upgrade eligibility. Status is typically tied to the member's Frequent Flyer Number and recalculated each qualification period.

Tiers are usually achieved by accumulating tier/status points, qualifying miles, or qualifying segments within a defined window, and they map to alliance-wide recognition levels (for example Star Alliance Silver/Gold or oneworld Ruby/Sapphire/Emerald) so benefits travel across partner carriers. A member's tier influences service handling encoded in a reservation and drives differentiated offers. Tier status is a property of an individual member, not of the program as a whole.

**한국어 / Korean** — **로열티 등급 상태 (우수 등급)** — 회원이 FFP 내에서 보유하는 등급화된 수준(예: 실버, 골드, 플래티넘)으로, 자격 산정 활동을 통해 획득하며 우선 서비스, 라운지 이용, 추가 수하물, 업그레이드 자격 등 단계적으로 상향되는 혜택을 부여한다. 등급 상태는 통상 회원의 Frequent Flyer Number에 연동되며 자격 산정 기간마다 재산정된다.

등급은 일반적으로 정해진 기간 내에 등급/상태 포인트, 자격 마일리지 또는 자격 구간을 누적하여 달성하며, 동맹 전반의 인정 등급(예: Star Alliance의 Silver/Gold 또는 oneworld의 Ruby/Sapphire/Emerald)에 매핑되어 혜택이 제휴 항공사 간에 이어진다. 회원의 등급은 예약에 기록되는 서비스 처리에 영향을 주고 차별화된 제안을 이끈다. 등급 상태는 프로그램 전체가 아니라 개별 회원의 속성이다.

**Aliases:** `Elite Status`, `Tier Status`, `Membership Tier`, `Frequent Flyer Tier`

# Distinctions
- **Loyalty Tier Status** vs [Frequent Flyer Program](/common/customer/frequent-flyer-program.md) — Tier status is one member's earned standing; the program is the overall scheme that defines the tiers.
- **Loyalty Tier Status** vs [Frequent Flyer Number](/common/customer/frequent-flyer-number.md) — The number identifies the account; the tier status is the benefit level attached to that account.
- **Loyalty Tier Status** vs [Elite Qualifying Metric](/common/customer/elite-qualifying-metric.md) — The elite qualifying metric is the input counted toward status; Loyalty Tier Status is the resulting standing (Silver/Gold/Platinum) a member attains once the threshold is met.
- **Loyalty Tier Status** vs [Mileage Accrual](/common/customer/mileage-accrual.md) — Redeemable miles accrue as spendable currency, while tier/qualifying miles accrue toward earning or retaining status; both can be earned simultaneously but serve different purposes.
- **Loyalty Tier Status** vs [RFM Segmentation](/common/customer/rfm-segmentation.md) — Loyalty Tier Status is a program-defined standing a member earns through qualifying activity; RFM Segmentation is an internal analytic score a brand computes to group travelers by behavior, invisible to the customer.
- **Loyalty Tier Status** vs [Status Match](/common/customer/status-match.md) — Loyalty Tier Status is the earned standing within one program; a Status Match is the acquisition mechanism by which a rival program grants an equivalent tier without the usual qualifying activity.
