> A Travel Management Company (TMC) is a specialist B2B travel agency that provides end-to-end corporate travel services — booking air, hotel, rail, and car; enforcing travel policy; monitoring traveler locations for duty of care; negotiating supplier rates; and delivering management reporting — on behalf of corporate clients.

TMCs sit at the centre of corporate travel programmes, combining booking technology (often a GDS-powered online booking tool, OBT) with dedicated travel consultants, 24/7 traveler support, and sophisticated reporting. A key function is policy enforcement at point-of-sale: the TMC's system applies the client company's travel policy rules (preferred carrier fares, hotel caps, approval workflows) before a booking is confirmed. TMCs also consolidate spend across air, hotel, and ground into unified invoice and data feeds, enabling clients to track total programme cost, benchmark performance, and report carbon emissions. Large global TMCs include American Express Global Business Travel (Amex GBT), BCD Travel, and CWT; the Global Business Travel Association (GBTA) is the primary trade body. TMCs earn revenue through transaction fees, management fees, and supplier commissions, a model distinct from leisure OTAs.

**한국어 / Korean** — **기업 출장 관리 회사(TMC)** — TMC(Travel Management Company, 기업 출장 관리 회사)는 법인 고객을 대신해 항공·호텔·철도·렌터카 예약, 출장 정책 준수 관리, 여행자 위치 모니터링(Duty of Care), 공급사 요율 협상, 비용 보고 등 법인 출장 전 과정을 제공하는 B2B 전문 여행사이다.

TMC는 법인 출장 프로그램의 중심에 위치하며, GDS 기반 온라인 예약 도구(OBT), 전담 출장 컨설턴트, 24시간 여행자 지원, 정교한 리포팅 기능을 결합한다. 핵심 기능은 예약 시점의 정책 준수 적용이다. TMC 시스템은 예약 확정 전 클라이언트 회사의 출장 정책 규칙(선호 항공사 운임, 호텔 상한액, 승인 워크플로)을 적용한다. TMC는 또한 항공·호텔·지상 교통 지출을 통합 청구서 및 데이터 피드로 집계해 클라이언트가 총 프로그램 비용 추적, 성과 벤치마킹, 탄소 배출 보고를 할 수 있게 한다. 주요 글로벌 TMC로는 American Express Global Business Travel(Amex GBT), BCD Travel, CWT가 있으며, GBTA(Global Business Travel Association)가 주요 업계 단체다.

# Distinctions
- **Travel Management Company** vs [GDS](/common/standards/gds.md) — A GDS is the technology platform that aggregates airline seat and hotel room inventory; a TMC is a service company that uses the GDS (and other tools) to book and manage corporate travel on behalf of clients.
- **Travel Management Company** vs [Corporate Profile](/common/customer/corporate-profile.md) — A Corporate Profile is the traveler's personal master record (preferences, loyalty numbers, documents) stored in a booking system; a TMC is the company providing the booking and management services that use that profile.
- **Travel Management Company** vs [Duty of Care](/common/customer/duty-of-care.md) — Duty of Care is the legal and ethical obligation to protect travelling employees; a TMC is a service partner that helps organisations fulfil that obligation through traveler tracking, alerts, and emergency support tools.
