> PADIS is the IATA governance body and standards set that develops and maintains the EDI message standards for passenger travel and airport passenger-service activities, published in both EDIFACT and XML syntaxes. The PADIS Board, now under the Passenger Standards Conference, owns and version-controls these schemas, including the NDC/EDIST XML messages.

Governed historically under Passenger Services Conference Resolution 783, the PADIS Board is composed of airline representatives and is supported by committees and expert working groups that oversee communications-protocol interoperability and semantic consistency across IATA standards. PADIS is the standards-authority layer that explains how airline distribution and data-interchange schemas — from legacy EDIFACT reservations and PNRGOV messages to the EDIST XML implementing NDC — are agreed, governed, and versioned.

**한국어 / Korean** — **여객·공항 데이터 교환 표준(PADIS)** — PADIS는 여객 여행과 공항 여객 서비스 활동을 위한 EDI 메시지 표준을 개발·유지하는 IATA 거버넌스 기구이자 표준 집합으로, EDIFACT와 XML 두 구문으로 발행된다. 현재 여객표준회의(Passenger Standards Conference) 산하에 있는 PADIS Board가 NDC/EDIST XML 메시지를 포함한 이 스키마들을 소유하고 버전을 관리한다.

역사적으로 여객서비스회의 결의 783(Passenger Services Conference Resolution 783) 아래 운영되어 온 PADIS Board는 항공사 대표들로 구성되며, 위원회와 전문 작업그룹의 지원을 받아 통신 프로토콜 상호운용성과 IATA 표준 전반의 의미적 일관성을 감독한다. PADIS는 레거시 EDIFACT 예약 및 PNRGOV 메시지부터 NDC를 구현하는 EDIST XML에 이르기까지 항공 유통·데이터 교환 스키마가 어떻게 합의·관리·버전 관리되는지를 설명하는 표준 권한 계층이다.

# Distinctions
- **Passenger and Airport Data Interchange Standards (PADIS)** vs [EDIFACT](/common/standards/edifact.md) — EDIFACT is the UN-origin EDI syntax; PADIS is the IATA body and air-industry standards set (in both EDIFACT and XML syntaxes) that governs how those messages are defined for passenger and airport use.
- **Passenger and Airport Data Interchange Standards (PADIS)** vs [Enhanced and Simplified Distribution (EDIST)](/common/standards/enhanced-and-simplified-distribution-edist.md) — PADIS is the governance/authority publishing and versioning schemas; EDIST is the specific NDC XML message family published under PADIS governance.
