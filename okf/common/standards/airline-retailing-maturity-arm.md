> The Airline Retailing Maturity (ARM) index is an IATA framework that measures how far airlines and their partners have progressed toward a world of 100% Offers and Orders. Introduced in 2021, it consolidates and replaces IATA's earlier NDC and ONE Order certification registries with a single maturity assessment.

The ARM index is organized around pillars including Capabilities Verification (a registry of proven technical abilities to shop, order, pay, settle, account, and set up, expressed in business rather than technical language), Partnerships Deployment (how effectively airlines and sellers scale across the value chain), and the Value Capture Compass (an airline-only ~50-question self-assessment across eight value drivers). Airlines completing the assessment receive a numbered score out of 100 representing their retailing maturity and strategy, enabling benchmarking against peers.

**한국어 / Korean** — **항공 리테일링 성숙도 지수(ARM index)** — 항공 리테일링 성숙도(Airline Retailing Maturity, ARM) 지수는 항공사와 그 파트너가 100% Offer 및 Order 세계로 얼마나 나아갔는지를 측정하는 IATA 프레임워크이다. 2021년 도입되었으며, 이전의 NDC 및 ONE Order 인증 등록제를 단일 성숙도 평가로 통합·대체한다.

ARM 지수는 Capabilities Verification(조회·주문·결제·정산·회계·셋업 능력을 기술 용어가 아닌 업무 언어로 표현한, 검증된 기술 역량 등록제), Partnerships Deployment(항공사와 판매자가 가치사슬 전반으로 얼마나 효과적으로 확장하는지), Value Capture Compass(항공사 전용으로 8개 가치 동인을 다루는 약 50문항 자가평가) 등의 기둥(pillar)으로 구성된다. 평가를 완료한 항공사는 리테일링 성숙도와 전략을 나타내는 100점 만점의 점수를 받아 동종 업계와 벤치마킹할 수 있다.

# Distinctions
- **Airline Retailing Maturity (ARM)** vs [NDC](/common/standards/ndc.md) — ARM is a maturity measurement and registry of an organization's retailing progress; NDC is the underlying XML distribution standard whose adoption ARM helps assess.
- **Airline Retailing Maturity (ARM)** vs [ONE Order](/common/standards/one-order.md) — ARM is the assessment/registry framework (which replaced the separate NDC and ONE Order certification registries); ONE Order is one of the standards whose maturity ARM measures.
