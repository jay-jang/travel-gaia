> UNECE (United Nations Economic Commission for Europe) is a UN regional commission, established in 1947 and based in Geneva, that develops norms, standards, and conventions to facilitate trade, transport, and economic cooperation. In travel and transport it maintains code standards such as UN/LOCODE and hosts UN/CEFACT, the body behind UN/EDIFACT.

Although 'Europe' is in its name, UNECE's trade-facilitation and transport instruments are used globally. It maintains UN/LOCODE (United Nations Code for Trade and Transport Locations), administers transport agreements and vehicle regulations, and provides the institutional home for UN/CEFACT, which develops electronic-business and EDI standards. These outputs are referenced across multimodal travel and logistics data exchange.

**한국어 / Korean** — **유엔유럽경제위원회(UNECE)** — UNECE(유엔유럽경제위원회)는 1947년 설립되어 제네바에 본부를 둔 유엔 지역 위원회로, 무역·운송·경제 협력을 촉진하기 위한 규범·표준·협약을 개발한다. 여행·운송 분야에서는 UN/LOCODE 같은 코드 표준을 유지하고, UN/EDIFACT를 만든 UN/CEFACT를 산하에 둔다.

명칭에 '유럽'이 들어가지만 UNECE의 무역 간소화·운송 관련 도구는 전 세계적으로 사용된다.
