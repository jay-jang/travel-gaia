> An Online Booking Tool (OBT) is a self-service corporate travel application that enables employees to search, compare, and book flights, hotels, and car rentals in compliance with their company's travel policy, reducing the need for manual agent assistance on routine trips.

An OBT sits between the GDS (or direct supplier connections) and the corporate traveler, applying the company's travel policy rules — preferred suppliers, booking windows, cabin-class restrictions, approval workflows — in real time. Leading OBT platforms include Concur Travel (SAP), Cytric (Amadeus), and Egencia (Amex GBT); they integrate with expense-management systems and can feed data back to the Travel Management Company (TMC) that administers the programme. OBTs are the primary data-collection point for corporate travel analytics, duty-of-care tracking, and policy-compliance reporting. Modern OBTs increasingly connect via NDC alongside legacy GDS content, and some extend to rail, taxi, and hotel-direct content.

**한국어 / Korean** — **온라인 예약 도구(OBT)** — OBT(Online Booking Tool)는 직원이 항공편·호텔·렌터카를 검색·비교·예약할 수 있게 하는 기업 여행 셀프서비스 애플리케이션으로, 회사의 출장 정책을 준수하면서도 일반적인 출장에 대한 수동 대리점 지원 필요성을 줄여 준다.

OBT는 GDS(또는 공급사 직접 연결)와 기업 출장자 사이에 위치하며, 회사의 출장 정책 규칙 — 선호 공급사, 예약 가능 기간, 좌석 등급 제한, 승인 워크플로 — 을 실시간으로 적용한다. 주요 OBT 플랫폼으로는 Concur Travel(SAP), Cytric(Amadeus), Egencia(Amex GBT)가 있으며, 이들은 경비 관리 시스템과 통합되고 해당 프로그램을 관리하는 TMC(Travel Management Company)에 데이터를 제공할 수 있다. OBT는 기업 출장 분석, 안전 배려(duty of care) 추적, 정책 준수 보고를 위한 주요 데이터 수집 지점이다.

**Aliases:** `OBT`, `Self-Booking Tool`, `SBT`, `Corporate Booking Tool`, `CBT`

# Distinctions
- **Online Booking Tool** vs [Travel Management Company](/common/customer/travel-management-company.md) — A Travel Management Company is the specialist agency that manages a corporate travel programme and provides agent servicing; the OBT is the self-service software tool through which employees book directly without agent involvement. Many TMCs deploy and support an OBT as part of their service.
- **Online Booking Tool** vs [GDS](/common/standards/gds.md) — The GDS is the global distribution infrastructure that sources flight, hotel, and car content from suppliers; the OBT is the consumer-facing application layered on top of GDS content (and increasingly on NDC/direct connections) that applies corporate policy.
