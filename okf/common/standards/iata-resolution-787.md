> IATA Resolution 787 is the Passenger Services Conference resolution, titled Enhanced Airline Distribution, that established the New Distribution Capability (NDC) XML data-exchange standard for communication between airlines and travel agents. It is the foundational governing instrument behind the NDC distribution-standards framework.

Adopted through IATA's Passenger Services Conference, Resolution 787 set out the principles and standard for an XML-based messaging capability enabling airlines to distribute richer, personalized offers (including ancillaries and branded fares) directly and via intermediaries, complementing legacy EDIFACT distribution. The resolution and its accompanying standards provide the normative basis for the NDC schemas; subsequent Enhanced and Simplified Distribution (EDIST) work and the Airline Retailing Maturity (ARM) programme build on this foundation. As an industry resolution rather than legislation, it binds participating IATA members and frames how NDC is governed and versioned.

**한국어 / Korean** — **IATA Resolution 787 (IATA 결의 787 / 항공사 유통 강화)** — IATA Resolution 787은 'Enhanced Airline Distribution(항공사 유통 강화)'이라는 제목의 여객서비스회의(Passenger Services Conference) 결의로, 항공사와 여행사 간 통신을 위한 New Distribution Capability(NDC) XML 데이터 교환 표준을 수립했다. NDC 유통 표준 체계의 토대를 이루는 거버닝(governing) 문서이다.

IATA 여객서비스회의를 통해 채택된 Resolution 787은 항공사가 부가서비스와 브랜디드 운임을 포함한 더 풍부하고 개인화된 오퍼를 직접 및 중개 채널로 유통할 수 있게 하는 XML 기반 메시징 역량의 원칙과 표준을 규정했으며, 레거시 EDIFACT 유통을 보완한다. 이 결의와 부속 표준은 NDC 스키마의 규범적 근거를 제공하며, 이후의 Enhanced and Simplified Distribution(EDIST) 작업과 Airline Retailing Maturity(ARM) 프로그램이 이 토대 위에 구축된다. 입법이 아니라 업계 결의이므로 참여 IATA 회원사에 대해 구속력을 가지며 NDC가 어떻게 거버넌스되고 버전 관리되는지를 규정한다.

# Distinctions
- **IATA Resolution 787** vs [NDC](/common/standards/ndc.md) — NDC is the XML standard/capability itself; Resolution 787 is the IATA resolution that established and governs it. One is the technology standard, the other its constitutional basis.
- **IATA Resolution 787** vs [Enhanced and Simplified Distribution (EDIST)](/common/standards/enhanced-and-simplified-distribution-edist.md) — EDIST is the IATA programme/governance umbrella that evolves the NDC and ONE Order standards under Resolution 787; Resolution 787 is the originating resolution that created the NDC standard.
