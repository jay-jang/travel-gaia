> An Online Travel Agency is a web- or app-based retailer that aggregates and sells travel products (flights, hotels, packages, car rental, activities) from multiple suppliers to consumers, typically sourcing content from GDSs, bedbanks, and direct supplier connections. Examples include Expedia, Booking.com, and Trip.com.

OTAs operate under various commercial models including the agency (commissionable) model and the merchant model, and may act as merchant of record. They are distinct from metasearch sites, which redirect users to booking sites rather than completing the transaction themselves. Note: the acronym 'OTA' also separately denotes the OpenTravel Alliance XML standard, an unrelated concept.

**한국어 / Korean** — **온라인 여행사(OTA)** — 온라인 여행사(OTA)는 여러 공급사의 여행 상품(항공편, 호텔, 패키지, 렌터카, 액티비티)을 통합하여 소비자에게 판매하는 웹 또는 앱 기반 소매업체로, 보통 GDS, 베드뱅크(bedbank), 공급사 직접 연결을 통해 콘텐츠를 확보한다. Expedia, Booking.com, Trip.com 등이 그 예이다.

OTA는 에이전시(수수료) 모델과 머천트(merchant) 모델 등 다양한 상업 모델로 운영되며 등록 판매자(merchant of record) 역할을 하기도 한다. 거래를 직접 완료하지 않고 사용자를 예약 사이트로 연결해 주는 메타서치 사이트와는 구별된다. 참고: 'OTA'라는 약어는 별개로 OpenTravel Alliance XML 표준을 가리키기도 하며, 이는 무관한 개념이다.

**Aliases:** `Online Travel Agency`, `Online Travel Agent`

# Distinctions
- **OTA (Online Travel Agency)** vs [OpenTravel Alliance](/common/standards/opentravel-alliance.md) — This OTA is an online travel retailer (agency); the identically abbreviated OpenTravel Alliance is an XML messaging standards body — entirely unrelated.
- **OTA (Online Travel Agency)** vs [Metasearch](/common/standards/metasearch.md) — An OTA sells and processes the booking itself; a metasearch only compares prices and refers the user to an OTA or supplier to book.
- **OTA (Online Travel Agency)** vs [GDS](/common/standards/gds.md) — An OTA is a consumer-facing seller; a GDS is the wholesale distribution platform that supplies content to sellers.
- **OTA (Online Travel Agency)** vs [Bedbank](/lodging/hotel-dist/bedbank.md) — A bedbank is a B2B wholesaler selling to other travel sellers; an OTA sells hotel rooms directly to the traveling public.
- **OTA (Online Travel Agency)** vs [Direct Booking](/lodging/hotel-dist/direct-booking.md) — An OTA is a third-party intermediary that sells the hotel's rooms for a commission and typically owns the guest relationship; a direct booking is made through the hotel's own channels with no intermediary, keeping the guest data and avoiding commission.
- **OTA (Online Travel Agency)** vs [Internet Distribution System (IDS)](/lodging/hotel-dist/internet-distribution-system.md) — An OTA is a single type of online seller; IDS is the broader category that includes OTAs along with travel portals, search engines, and online consolidators.
- **OTA (Online Travel Agency)** vs [Limited Lines Travel Insurance Producer](/common/insurance/limited-lines-travel-insurance-producer.md) — An OTA is the travel retailer selling the trip; to sell insurance alongside it the OTA acts under a limited lines travel insurance producer's authorization, which is the licensing role rather than the retailer itself.
