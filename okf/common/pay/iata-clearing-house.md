> The IATA Clearing House (ICH) is IATA's central facility for the multilateral netting and settlement of accounts between airlines, airline-associated companies, and travel partners, principally covering interline billings. It nets amounts members owe each other and settles the resulting balances, processing tens of billions of US dollars in annual billings.

ICH allows participants to settle many bilateral interline obligations through a single net position rather than paying each counterparty individually, reducing currency exposure and credit risk. Invoices are submitted via IATA's SIS (Simplified Invoicing and Settlement) platform. ICH interoperates with the separate Airlines Clearing House (ACH); settlements between an ICH participant and an ACH participant are conducted in USD.

**한국어 / Korean** — **IATA 정산소(ICH)** — IATA Clearing House(ICH)는 항공사, 항공사 관련 기업, 여행 파트너 간 계정의 다자간 차감 정산과 결제를 위한 IATA의 중앙 기구로, 주로 인터라인 청구를 담당한다. 회원사가 상호 지급할 금액을 차감하여 그 잔액을 정산하며, 연간 수백억 달러 규모의 청구를 처리한다.

ICH를 통해 참여사는 다수의 양자 간 인터라인 채무를 각 거래 상대방에게 개별 지급하는 대신 단일 순포지션으로 정산할 수 있어 환 노출과 신용 위험을 줄인다. 인보이스는 IATA의 SIS(Simplified Invoicing and Settlement) 플랫폼을 통해 제출된다. ICH는 별도의 Airlines Clearing House(ACH)와 상호 운용되며, ICH 참여사와 ACH 참여사 간 정산은 미국 달러(USD)로 이루어진다.

# Distinctions
- **IATA Clearing House** vs [BSP](/common/pay/bsp.md) — ICH settles airline-to-airline (interline) accounts; BSP settles agent-to-airline sales.
- **IATA Clearing House** vs [ARC](/common/pay/arc.md) — ICH handles interline settlement globally; ARC handles US agent-to-airline settlement.
- **IATA Clearing House** vs [Acquirer](/common/pay/acquirer.md) — The acquirer settles consumer card payments between merchants and issuers via the card networks, whereas the IATA Clearing House settles obligations between airlines and other industry members.
- **IATA Clearing House** vs [CASS](/common/pay/cass.md) — CASS settles airline-to-forwarder cargo billings; the IATA Clearing House settles airline-to-airline (interline) obligations.
- **IATA Clearing House** vs [Settlement Cycle](/common/pay/settlement-cycle.md) — The IATA Clearing House is an institution that nets and settles inter-airline accounts; a settlement cycle is the timing structure on which it and other settlement systems operate.
- **IATA Clearing House** vs [Simplified Invoicing and Settlement (SIS)](/common/pay/simplified-invoicing-and-settlement-sis.md) — SIS is the e-invoicing platform that captures, validates and presents invoices; the IATA Clearing House is the settlement facility that nets and settles the resulting balances. SIS feeds the ICH.
