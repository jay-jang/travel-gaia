> Interactive Agent Reporting (IAR) is ARC's electronic weekly sales-reporting and settlement platform for US travel agencies and Corporate Travel Departments. It captures sales, refunds, exchanges, agency memos and Travel Agency Service Fees (TASFs) electronically and lets the agent authorize a reconciled sales report each sales week for settlement through ARC.

IAR is the ARC-side counterpart to the reporting feeds used in IATA's BSP world. Nightly sales data downloads from the CRS/GDS into IAR, where transactions are edited and verified; the agent receives confirmations through the sales week and authorizes a net sales report by the Period Ending Date (PED, always a Sunday), after which ARC drafts the authorized net remittance. Credit-card transactions are released for direct ARC billing, while sales and refunds are output to carriers and the remaining documents flow post-settlement. IAR thus consolidates an agency's weekly air settlement with ARC into a single electronic workflow.

**한국어 / Korean** — **Interactive Agent Reporting(IAR, 대화형 대리점 보고)** — Interactive Agent Reporting(IAR)는 미국 여행사 및 기업출장부서(CTD)를 위한 ARC의 전자식 주간 판매보고·정산 플랫폼이다. 판매, 환불, 재발행(exchange), 대리점 메모, 그리고 여행사 서비스 수수료(TASF, Travel Agency Service Fee)를 전자적으로 수집하고, 대리점이 매 판매 주(週)마다 대사된 판매 보고서를 승인하여 ARC를 통해 정산하도록 한다.

IAR는 IATA의 BSP 세계에서 쓰이는 보고 피드에 대응하는 ARC 측 도구다. 매일 밤 CRS/GDS에서 판매 데이터가 IAR로 다운로드되어 거래가 편집·검증되며, 대리점은 판매 주 동안 확인 정보를 받고 PED(Period Ending Date, 항상 일요일)까지 순(純) 판매 보고서를 승인한다. 이후 ARC가 승인된 순 송금액을 출금한다. 신용카드 거래는 ARC의 직접 청구로 넘겨지고, 판매·환불은 항공사로 산출되며 나머지 문서는 정산 후에 처리된다. 이렇게 IAR는 대리점의 주간 항공 정산을 ARC와의 단일 전자 워크플로로 통합한다.

# Distinctions
- **Interactive Agent Reporting** vs [ARC](/common/pay/arc.md) — ARC is the US settlement company itself; IAR is the specific electronic reporting and settlement application ARC operates through which agencies file their weekly sales reports.
- **Interactive Agent Reporting** vs [BSP](/common/pay/bsp.md) — IAR is the ARC (US) reporting/settlement mechanism, whereas the BSP is IATA's equivalent globally; an agency reports through IAR in the US market and through BSP tools elsewhere.
