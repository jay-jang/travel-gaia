> SEPA (Single Euro Payments Area) is the European Union's standardised framework for cashless euro-denominated payments, enabling credit transfers, direct debits, and instant transfers to be executed across 36 member countries under identical rules, as if they were domestic transactions. It is governed by the European Payments Council (EPC) and overseen by the European Central Bank (ECB), and forms the primary bank-account infrastructure for B2B settlement within the travel industry across Europe.

SEPA comprises three core payment instruments: SEPA Credit Transfer (SCT) for one-off bank-to-bank transfers, SEPA Direct Debit (SDD) for recurring or single pull payments authorised by mandate, and SEPA Instant Credit Transfer (SCT Inst) for 10-second real-time transfers available 24/7/365. For the travel industry, SEPA eliminates the cost and delay of cross-border euro wire transfers; airlines, OTAs, GDS companies, and hotel chains operating in Europe rely on SEPA to settle B2B invoices, agent remittances, and supplier payables. SEPA coverage includes all EU/EEA countries plus Switzerland, the UK (post-Brexit), Monaco, San Marino, and Andorra — 36 countries in total.

**한국어 / Korean** — **단일유로결제구역(SEPA)** — SEPA(Single Euro Payments Area, 단일유로결제구역)는 유럽연합의 유로화 무현금 결제 표준화 프레임워크로, 36개 회원국에서 신용이체·직접출금·즉시이체를 국내 거래와 동일한 규칙으로 처리할 수 있게 한다. 유럽결제위원회(EPC)가 관리하고 유럽중앙은행(ECB)이 감독하며, 유럽 내 여행 산업 B2B 정산을 위한 핵심 은행 인프라다.

SEPA는 세 가지 핵심 결제 수단으로 구성된다: 1회성 은행 간 이체를 위한 SEPA 신용이체(SCT), 위임장으로 승인된 반복·단일 출금을 위한 SEPA 직접출금(SDD), 24/7/365 10초 실시간 이체를 위한 SEPA 즉시 신용이체(SCT Inst). 여행 산업에서 SEPA는 국경 간 유로화 전신송금의 비용과 지연을 제거한다. 유럽에서 운영하는 항공사, OTA, GDS사, 호텔 체인은 SEPA를 활용해 B2B 인보이스, 에이전트 송금, 공급업체 미지급금을 정산한다. SEPA 대상 국가는 모든 EU/EEA 국가 외에 스위스, 영국(브렉시트 후), 모나코, 산마리노, 안도라를 포함한 총 36개국이다.

# Distinctions
- **SEPA (Single Euro Payments Area)** vs [BSP](/common/pay/bsp.md) — BSP (Billing and Settlement Plan) is IATA's multilateral clearing scheme specific to air-ticket sales by travel agents; SEPA is a pan-European bank-account payment network used for any euro-denominated transfer — including the underlying bank movements that actually settle BSP obligations.
- **SEPA (Single Euro Payments Area)** vs [Card Scheme](/common/pay/card-scheme.md) — A card scheme (Visa, Mastercard) is a payment card network defining rules for card-based transactions; SEPA is a bank-account-to-bank-account payment framework covering credit transfers and direct debits, running independently of card schemes.
