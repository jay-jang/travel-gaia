> Comprehensive travel insurance is the bundled single-trip product class that combines trip cancellation and interruption, travel delay, baggage, emergency medical and evacuation benefits into one plan. It is the default offering most consumers buy and serves as the umbrella to which optional riders (such as CFAR or a pre-existing condition waiver) attach.

Rather than buying separate trip-cancellation and travel-medical policies, most leisure travelers buy a single comprehensive plan whose benefit schedule lists each coverage with its own limit. The trip-cost benefits (cancellation/interruption) are the headline and largest premium component, with delay, baggage, medical and evacuation rounding out the package; optional upgrades — Cancel For Any Reason, Interruption For Any Reason, a pre-existing condition waiver, rental-car or adventure add-ons — extend it. Comprehensive plans are typically single-trip, with annual multi-trip variants for frequent travelers.

**한국어 / Korean** — **종합 여행보험(Comprehensive Travel Insurance)** — Comprehensive Travel Insurance(종합 여행보험)는 여행 취소·중단, 여행 지연, 수하물, 응급 의료·후송 보장을 하나의 단일 여행 상품으로 묶은 패키지 상품군이다. 대다수 소비자가 구매하는 기본 상품이며, CFAR이나 기왕증 면제 같은 선택 특약이 붙는 우산 역할을 한다.

대부분의 레저 여행자는 취소 보험과 여행의료 보험을 따로 사는 대신, 각 보장과 한도를 보장 명세표에 나열한 하나의 종합 보험을 구매한다. 여행비용 보장(취소/중단)이 대표 항목이자 최대 보험료 구성요소이며, 지연·수하물·의료·후송이 패키지를 완성한다. 선택 특약 — Cancel For Any Reason, Interruption For Any Reason, 기왕증 면제, 렌터카·액티비티 추가 — 으로 확장된다. 종합 보험은 보통 단일 여행용이며, 빈번한 여행자를 위한 연간 다회 여행 상품도 있다.

# Distinctions
- **Comprehensive Travel Insurance** vs [Travel Medical Insurance](/common/insurance/travel-medical-insurance.md) — Travel medical insurance is one benefit (healthcare abroad) sold standalone; comprehensive insurance bundles that medical benefit together with cancellation, delay and baggage in a single plan.
- **Comprehensive Travel Insurance** vs [Merchant Cancellation Protection](/common/insurance/merchant-cancellation-protection.md) — A comprehensive plan is a regulated multi-benefit insurance contract; merchant cancellation protection is a single-purpose, non-insurance refund product offered by the booking platform itself.
