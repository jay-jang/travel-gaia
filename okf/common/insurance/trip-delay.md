> Trip Delay (also called Travel Delay) is a core benefit of comprehensive travel-insurance policies that reimburses additional out-of-pocket costs — meals, lodging, and local transportation — when a traveler is delayed by a covered cause (typically a common-carrier delay of at least 6 or 12 hours, weather, or mechanical breakdown). It is the insurance counterpart to carrier-side disruption rules, paying the traveler's own incidental expenses rather than compensation owed by the airline.

Coverage activates after a stated minimum delay (commonly 6 or 12 hours, sometimes as little as 3) and reimburses receipted reasonable expenses up to a per-day cap (often $100-$300/day) within an overall per-trip limit (commonly $500-$2,000). Many credit cards bundle a similar trip-delay reimbursement benefit. Trip delay is usually short-duration relief for being stuck mid-journey; if the delay ultimately causes the traveler to abandon or significantly alter the trip, the larger trip-interruption benefit may instead apply. It does not pay the statutory care or compensation a carrier owes under passenger-rights regimes — those are obligations of the airline, and insurance delay benefits are typically secondary to them.

**한국어 / Korean** — **여행 지연 보장(Trip Delay)** — Trip Delay(또는 Travel Delay, 여행 지연)은 보장 사유(통상 common carrier의 6시간 또는 12시간 이상 지연, 악천후, 기계 고장 등)로 여행자가 지연될 때 식비·숙박·현지 교통비 등 추가 자기부담 비용을 보상하는 종합형 여행보험의 핵심 보장이다. 운송인이 부담하는 보상이 아니라 여행자 본인의 부수 비용을 지급한다는 점에서, 운송인 측 운항 차질 규정에 대응하는 보험 측 장치다.

정해진 최소 지연 시간(흔히 6시간 또는 12시간, 때로는 3시간)이 지나면 보장이 시작되며, 영수증 기반의 합리적 비용을 1일 한도(흔히 $100~$300/일) 내에서, 여행당 총한도(흔히 $500~$2,000) 안에서 보상한다. 다수의 신용카드도 유사한 trip-delay 보상 혜택을 제공한다. Trip Delay는 보통 여정 중간에 발이 묶인 상황에 대한 단기 구제이며, 지연이 결국 여행 중단이나 대폭 변경으로 이어지면 더 큰 Trip Interruption 보장이 적용될 수 있다. 승객 권리 제도상 운송인이 부담하는 법정 care(편의 제공)나 보상은 지급하지 않는다. 그것은 항공사의 의무이며, 보험의 지연 보장은 통상 그에 대해 2차적이다.

# Distinctions
- **Trip Delay** vs [Trip Interruption](/common/insurance/trip-interruption.md) — Trip delay covers short-term incidental costs (meals, a hotel night) while you wait out a delay and then continue; trip interruption covers the unused trip cost and return transport when a covered event forces you to cut the trip short. Delay assumes you eventually proceed; interruption assumes you do not.
- **Trip Delay** vs [Right to Care](/common/disruption/right-to-care.md) — Right to Care (EU/UK 261) is the carrier's legal duty to provide meals and accommodation during disruption; trip delay is the traveler's own insurance benefit. Insurance delay coverage is typically secondary to — and net of — what the carrier must supply.
- **Trip Delay** vs [Missed Connection](/common/insurance/missed-connection.md) — Trip delay pays for waiting in place during a delay; missed connection pays the catch-up costs when that delay causes you to miss a connecting flight, cruise, or tour departure.
- **Trip Delay** vs [Baggage Delay](/common/insurance/baggage-delay.md) — Baggage delay reimburses essential purchases while your checked bags are missing; trip delay reimburses meals, lodging, and local transport while you yourself are stranded by a common-carrier delay. The two are separate benefits triggered by different events.
