> Missed Connection is a travel-insurance benefit that reimburses the additional transportation, accommodation, and meal costs a traveler incurs to catch up to their trip when a covered common-carrier delay (typically at least 3 hours) causes them to miss a connecting flight, cruise embarkation, or tour departure. It is especially important for cruisers, where a missed embarkation otherwise means joining the ship at the next port of call at the traveler's own expense.

Coverage typically requires a common-carrier delay of a minimum number of hours (often 3) caused by a covered peril such as weather, mechanical breakdown, or a documented carrier delay, and reimburses the reasonable cost of reaching the missed departure point or the next port/stop, subject to a per-traveler limit (commonly $250-$1,500). Some policies restrict the benefit to cruises and tours, while comprehensive plans extend it to connecting flights; many sell it as part of standard coverage at no extra cost. The benefit addresses the transit between a delay and rejoining the itinerary, picking up where trip-delay relief leaves off.

**한국어 / Korean** — **연결편 놓침 보장(Missed Connection)** — Missed Connection(연결편 놓침)은 보장 대상인 common carrier 지연(통상 3시간 이상)으로 연결 항공편·크루즈 승선(embarkation)·투어 출발을 놓쳤을 때, 여정을 따라잡기 위한 추가 교통비·숙박비·식비를 보상하는 여행보험 보장이다. 특히 크루즈 여행자에게 중요한데, 승선을 놓치면 다음 기항지(port of call)에서 자비로 배에 합류해야 하기 때문이다.

보통 악천후·기계 고장·문서화된 운송인 지연 등 보장 위험으로 인한 최소 시간(흔히 3시간) 이상의 common carrier 지연을 요건으로 하며, 놓친 출발 지점이나 다음 기항지·정차지에 도달하기 위한 합리적 비용을 여행자당 한도(흔히 $250~$1,500) 내에서 보상한다. 일부 약관은 이 보장을 크루즈·투어에 한정하지만, 종합형 플랜은 연결 항공편까지 확장하며, 많은 경우 추가 비용 없이 기본 보장에 포함해 판매한다. 이 보장은 지연과 여정 재합류 사이의 이동을 다루어, trip-delay 구제가 끝나는 지점을 이어받는다.

# Distinctions
- **Missed Connection** vs [Connection](/air/air-partner/connection.md) — A Connection is the operational/scheduling fact of changing between segments of a journey; Missed Connection is the insurance benefit that pays catch-up costs when a delay breaks that connection.
- **Missed Connection** vs [Trip Delay](/common/insurance/trip-delay.md) — Trip delay covers the cost of waiting out a delay in place; missed connection covers the cost of catching up to a departure (flight, cruise, tour) that you have already missed because of the delay.
- **Missed Connection** vs [MCT](/air/air-ops/mct.md) — Minimum Connecting Time (MCT) is the airline-published time needed to make a connection; if a delay defeats the MCT and the connection is missed, a Missed Connection insurance benefit may reimburse the resulting catch-up costs.
