> Repatriation of remains is a standard travel-medical benefit that arranges and pays the cost of returning a deceased traveler's body (or, in some plans, their ashes) to their home country or place of burial. It is a defining component of international travel medical plans and is distinct from medical evacuation, which transports living travelers.

Returning remains across borders involves substantial cost and logistics — local authority documentation, mortuary preparation, an approved transport container, and international shipping — and the benefit covers these arrangements through the insurer's assistance network. Per-event limits are commonly set separately (often roughly $10,000-$25,000), reflecting typical costs that range from a few thousand dollars to over $25,000. It is almost always offered alongside emergency medical evacuation in travel medical and comprehensive plans.

**한국어 / Korean** — **유해 송환(Repatriation of Remains)** — Repatriation of Remains(유해 송환)는 사망한 여행자의 시신(일부 약관은 유골)을 본국 또는 매장지로 돌려보내는 비용을 주선하고 지급하는 표준 여행의료 보장이다. 국제 여행의료 보험의 핵심 구성 요소이며, 생존 여행자를 이송하는 Medical Evacuation과는 구분된다.

국경을 넘는 유해 송환은 현지 당국 서류, 시신 안치 처리, 승인된 운송 용기, 국제 운송 등 상당한 비용과 절차가 따르며, 이 보장은 보험사의 어시스턴스 네트워크를 통해 그러한 절차를 처리한다. 사고당 한도는 보통 별도로 설정되며(흔히 약 1만~2.5만 달러), 수천 달러에서 2.5만 달러를 넘는 실제 비용 범위를 반영한다. 여행의료·종합 보험에서 거의 항상 Emergency Medical Evacuation과 함께 제공된다.

**Aliases:** `Return of Remains`, `Return of Mortal Remains`, `Repatriation`

# Distinctions
- **Repatriation of Remains** vs [Emergency Medical Evacuation](/common/insurance/emergency-medical-evacuation.md) — Evacuation moves a living, ill or injured traveler to adequate care; repatriation of remains returns a deceased traveler home. The two are complementary but separately defined benefits.
