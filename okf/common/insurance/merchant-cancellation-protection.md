> Merchant cancellation protection is the fintech/merchant-of-record alternative to travel insurance, in which the booking platform itself refunds the traveler and absorbs the supplier's cancellation fees as an ancillary product, rather than an insurer paying a claim. Because the platform is forgiving or self-funding the cost rather than indemnifying as a third-party insurer, these products sit outside insurance regulation.

Travel fintechs popularized this model — for example Hopper's HTS sells a 'Cancel For Any Reason' product for flights and hotels in which Hopper, acting as merchant of record, refunds the booking and eats the supplier penalty rather than placing the risk with a licensed insurer. Functionally it resembles a CFAR rider, but structurally it is an ancillary merchant offering (akin to the NAIC's cancellation fee waiver concept) that does not require an insurance filing or producer license. The model is attractive to platforms because it monetizes flexibility while keeping the product, data and customer relationship in-house.

**한국어 / Korean** — **머천트 취소 보호(Merchant Cancellation Protection)** — Merchant Cancellation Protection(머천트 취소 보호)은 여행보험의 핀테크/merchant-of-record 대안으로, 보험사가 클레임을 지급하는 대신 예약 플랫폼 자체가 여행자에게 환불하고 공급자의 취소 수수료를 부가 상품으로 흡수하는 방식이다. 플랫폼이 제3자 보험사로서 손해를 보전하는 것이 아니라 비용을 자가 부담·면제하므로, 이러한 상품은 보험 규제 밖에 있다.

여행 핀테크들이 이 모델을 대중화했다. 예컨대 Hopper의 HTS는 항공·호텔에 대해 'Cancel For Any Reason' 상품을 판매하는데, Hopper가 merchant of record로서 위험을 면허 보험사에 넘기지 않고 직접 예약을 환불하고 공급자 위약금을 부담한다. 기능적으로는 CFAR 특약과 유사하지만, 구조적으로는 보험 신고나 모집인 면허가 필요 없는 부가 머천트 상품(NAIC의 cancellation fee waiver 개념과 유사)이다. 유연성을 수익화하면서 상품·데이터·고객 관계를 내부에 유지할 수 있어 플랫폼에 매력적이다.

# Distinctions
- **Merchant Cancellation Protection** vs [Cancel For Any Reason (CFAR)](/common/insurance/cancel-for-any-reason-cfar.md) — Insurance CFAR transfers the risk to a licensed insurer that pays a claim under a regulated policy; merchant cancellation protection keeps the cost with the booking platform, which self-funds the refund as an ancillary outside insurance regulation.
- **Merchant Cancellation Protection** vs [Cancellation Fee Waiver](/common/insurance/cancellation-fee-waiver.md) — Both are non-insurance flexibility products, but a cancellation fee waiver is a supplier waiving the penalty on its own bookings, while merchant cancellation protection is a third-party platform (merchant of record) refunding bookings made through it.
- **Merchant Cancellation Protection** vs [Comprehensive Travel Insurance](/common/insurance/comprehensive-travel-insurance.md) — A comprehensive plan is a regulated multi-benefit insurance contract; merchant cancellation protection is a single-purpose, non-insurance refund product offered by the booking platform itself.
