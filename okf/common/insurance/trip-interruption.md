> Trip interruption is the post-departure counterpart to trip cancellation, reimbursing the unused non-refundable portion of a trip plus the cost of returning home early (or rejoining the itinerary) when a trip already underway is cut short for a covered reason. Benefit limits are commonly set above 100% of trip cost (often up to 150%) to absorb unplanned return transportation.

Like trip cancellation, interruption is a named-peril benefit and reimburses only for covered reasons (illness, injury or death of the traveler/companion/family member; severe weather; a strike or carrier disruption; certain emergencies at home). It typically reimburses (1) the prepaid non-refundable cost of the unused days and (2) the additional, often last-minute, transportation needed to get home or back on itinerary — which is why limits frequently exceed the insured trip cost. The pair 'cancellation/interruption' constitutes the trip-cost core of a comprehensive plan.

**한국어 / Korean** — **여행 중단 보장(Trip Interruption)** — Trip Interruption(여행 중단)은 Trip Cancellation에 대응하는 출발 후 보장으로, 이미 시작된 여행이 보장 사유로 단축될 때 사용하지 못한 환불 불가 금액과 조기 귀국(또는 일정 복귀) 비용을 보상한다. 예기치 못한 귀국 운송비를 흡수하기 위해 보장 한도가 여행비용의 100%를 넘는 경우(흔히 최대 150%)가 많다.

Trip Cancellation과 마찬가지로 Trip Interruption도 열거 위험 보장이며 보장 사유(본인·동반자·가족의 질병/상해/사망, 악천후, 파업이나 운송 차질, 자택의 특정 비상상황 등)에 대해서만 보상한다. 일반적으로 (1) 사용하지 못한 일수의 선결제 환불 불가 비용과 (2) 귀국 또는 일정 복귀를 위한 추가적·긴급 운송비를 보상하며, 이 때문에 한도가 부보 여행비용을 초과하는 경우가 많다. '취소/중단' 한 쌍이 종합 보험의 여행비용 핵심을 이룬다.

# Distinctions
- **Trip Interruption** vs [Trip Cancellation](/common/insurance/trip-cancellation.md) — Cancellation triggers before the trip begins and covers prepaid costs; interruption triggers after the trip has begun and adds the cost of cutting the trip short and returning home.
- **Trip Interruption** vs [Emergency Medical Evacuation](/common/insurance/emergency-medical-evacuation.md) — Trip interruption reimburses lost trip cost and ordinary return transport for any covered reason; medical evacuation specifically pays the cost of medically necessary transport to an adequate facility when a traveler is too ill or injured to travel normally.
- **Trip Interruption** vs [Trip Delay](/common/insurance/trip-delay.md) — Trip delay covers short-term incidental costs (meals, a hotel night) while you wait out a delay and then continue; trip interruption covers the unused trip cost and return transport when a covered event forces you to cut the trip short. Delay assumes you eventually proceed; interruption assumes you do not.
