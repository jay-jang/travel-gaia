> Emergency assistance is the 24/7/365 real-time support service included in most comprehensive travel insurance policies that connects travelers with immediate, coordinated help during crises abroad — encompassing medical case management, emergency medical evacuation coordination, lost-document replacement referrals, emergency legal referrals, and repatriation logistics. It operates as a service benefit distinct from financial reimbursement, typically provided through a specialist assistance company retained by the insurer.

Emergency assistance is operationally distinct from the insurance policy's financial cover: the insurer underwrites and pays claims, while a 24/7 assistance centre (staffed by multilingual coordinators and medical professionals) takes live calls, assesses situations, and dispatches resources in real time. Core functions include: hospital pre-authorisation to ensure direct payment so the traveler does not front costs; medical evacuation or air ambulance coordination; monitoring and managing hospitalised policyholders; emergency cash advance or bail bond coordination; and arranging repatriation of remains. Assistance companies contracted by insurers include Europ Assistance (Generali), International SOS, Allianz Partners, and AXA Assistance. For travelers, the correct step during a medical emergency is to call the assistance line before seeking treatment whenever possible — not to pay and claim later — because the assistance company can pre-authorise direct billing and arrange the most appropriate care.

**한국어 / Korean** — **긴급 지원 서비스(Emergency Assistance)** — 긴급 지원 서비스(Emergency Assistance)는 대부분의 종합 여행보험에 포함된 24/7/365 실시간 지원 서비스로, 해외에서 위기 상황에 처한 여행자와 즉각적이고 조율된 도움을 연결한다. 의료 사례 관리, 긴급 의료 후송 조율, 분실 서류 교체 안내, 긴급 법률 지원 안내, 본국 송환 물류 등을 포함한다. 금전적 보상과 구별되는 서비스 급부로 운영되며, 일반적으로 보험사가 계약한 전문 어시스턴스 업체가 제공한다.

긴급 지원 서비스는 운영상 보험의 금전 보상과 구별된다: 보험사는 클레임을 인수·지급하고, 24/7 어시스턴스 센터(다국어 코디네이터와 의료 전문가로 구성)는 실시간 전화를 받아 상황을 평가하고 자원을 배치한다. 핵심 기능에는 여행자가 비용을 선지급하지 않도록 병원 선지급 승인, 의료 후송·항공 앰뷸런스 조율, 입원 보험계약자 모니터링·관리, 긴급 현금 지급이나 보석금 조율, 본국 송환 준비가 포함된다. 보험사가 계약하는 어시스턴스 업체에는 Europ Assistance(Generali), International SOS, Allianz Partners, AXA Assistance 등이 있다. 여행자 관점에서 의료 응급 상황 시 올바른 절차는 가능한 한 진료 전 어시스턴스 라인에 먼저 전화하는 것이다. 나중에 선지급·청구하는 방식보다 어시스턴스 업체가 직접 청구를 승인하고 가장 적절한 의료를 준비할 수 있기 때문이다.

# Distinctions
- **Emergency Assistance** vs [Emergency Medical Evacuation](/common/insurance/emergency-medical-evacuation.md) — Emergency medical evacuation is the physical act of transporting a critically ill or injured traveler to an appropriate medical facility (including by air ambulance); emergency assistance is the broader 24/7 service umbrella that coordinates the evacuation alongside case management, pre-authorisation, and other non-transport support.
- **Emergency Assistance** vs [Travel Medical Insurance](/common/insurance/travel-medical-insurance.md) — Travel medical insurance is the financial product that reimburses or directly pays medical expenses abroad; emergency assistance is the live operational service — the phone line and coordination team — that arranges care and authorises direct payment. A policy can include both: the assistance company arranges treatment and the insurer underwrites the cost.
