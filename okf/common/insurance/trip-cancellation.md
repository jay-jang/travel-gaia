> Trip cancellation is the dominant travel-insurance benefit, reimbursing a traveler's prepaid, non-refundable trip costs when a trip is cancelled before departure for a reason named as covered in the policy (such as illness, injury, death, or another listed event). It is the foundational pre-departure coverage and, together with trip interruption, makes up the largest share of the US travel-insurance market.

Trip cancellation pays only for named perils — the policy lists covered reasons (illness/injury of the traveler, a traveling companion or a family member; death; jury duty; severe weather; carrier disruption; and similar events), so a cancellation for an unlisted reason is not reimbursed unless a Cancel For Any Reason upgrade is added. Because the benefit settles before any travel occurs, it covers prepaid non-refundable amounts such as airfare, hotel deposits, tours and cruises. Trip-cost protection (trip cancellation together with trip interruption) is the largest line in the US travel-insurance market, accounting for over two-thirds of industry revenue, and trip cancellation is generally regarded as the dominant single benefit within it.

**한국어 / Korean** — **여행 취소 보장(Trip Cancellation)** — Trip Cancellation(여행 취소)은 여행 출발 전에 약관이 보장 사유로 명시한 이유(질병, 상해, 사망 등 열거된 사고)로 여행을 취소할 때 선결제한 환불 불가 비용을 보상하는, 여행자보험의 핵심 보장이다. 출발 전 단계의 기본 보장이며, 여행 중단 보장과 함께 미국 여행보험 시장에서 가장 큰 비중을 차지한다.

Trip Cancellation은 열거 위험(named perils)만 보상한다. 약관이 보장 사유(본인·동반자·가족의 질병/상해, 사망, 배심원 의무, 악천후, 운송사 운항 차질 등)를 열거하므로, 목록에 없는 사유로 취소하면 Cancel For Any Reason 특약을 추가하지 않는 한 보상되지 않는다. 여행이 시작되기 전에 정산되므로 항공료, 호텔 예약금, 투어, 크루즈 등 선결제한 환불 불가 금액을 보장한다. 미국 여행보험 시장에서 여행비용 보장(취소와 중단을 합한 것)이 가장 큰 비중을 차지하며 업계 매출의 3분의 2를 넘고, 그중 취소가 일반적으로 가장 핵심적인 단일 보장으로 여겨진다.

# Distinctions
- **Trip Cancellation** vs [Trip Interruption](/common/insurance/trip-interruption.md) — Trip cancellation applies before departure (the trip never starts); trip interruption applies after departure (the trip is cut short mid-journey).
- **Trip Cancellation** vs [Cancel For Any Reason (CFAR)](/common/insurance/cancel-for-any-reason-cfar.md) — Standard trip cancellation pays 100% for listed reasons only; CFAR is an optional upgrade that pays a partial amount (typically 50-75%) for reasons not otherwise covered.
- **Trip Cancellation** vs [Cancellation Policy](/lodging/hotel-dist/cancellation-policy.md) — A cancellation policy is the supplier's own refund/penalty rules for a booking; trip cancellation insurance is a third-party indemnity that reimburses the traveler for amounts those rules make non-refundable.
