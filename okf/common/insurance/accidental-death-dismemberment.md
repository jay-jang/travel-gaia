> Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D) is a travel insurance benefit that pays a fixed lump-sum indemnity — the principal sum — to the insured's named beneficiary if the insured dies as a direct result of a covered accident during the trip, or directly to the insured if they suffer a scheduled permanent physical loss (complete severance or irreversible loss of a limb, sight, hearing, or speech) from a covered accident. Coverage is accident-only; illness is excluded. Three common variants exist: 24-Hour AD&D (entire trip), Common Carrier AD&D (while aboard licensed carriers), and Flight AD&D (air travel only).

AD&D benefits are structured around a "principal sum" — the face-value indemnity amount stated in the policy schedule. For accidental death, 100% of the principal sum is paid to the named beneficiary. For dismemberment, the benefit is a percentage of the principal sum as specified in the policy's benefit schedule: typically 100% for the loss of both hands, both feet, or sight in both eyes; 50% for one hand, one foot, or sight in one eye; and lesser percentages for loss of hearing or speech. The triggering event must be a sudden, unintentional accident caused by external means, and the loss must occur within a specified period following the accident (often 365 days). Illness, pre-existing conditions, and losses from high-risk activities (unless a rider is purchased) are excluded. AD&D is a fixed indemnity product, not an expense-reimbursement product: it does not pay medical bills. It is often included as a component of a comprehensive travel insurance policy.

**한국어 / Korean** — **상해사망·후유장애(Accidental Death & Dismemberment, AD&D)** — AD&D(Accidental Death & Dismemberment, 상해사망·후유장애)는 여행 중 보장된 사고로 피보험자가 사망하면 지명 수익자에게, 특정 영구적 신체 손실(사지 절단, 실명, 청각·언어 기능 완전 상실)이 발생하면 피보험자 본인에게 고정 일시금(principal sum)을 지급하는 여행보험 특약이다. 질병은 보장 대상이 아니며 오직 사고에 의한 손실만 보장된다. 24시간 AD&D(전 여행 기간), 정기 운송수단 AD&D(허가된 운송수단 탑승 중), 항공 한정 AD&D의 세 가지 변형이 일반적으로 존재한다.

AD&D 특약은 약관 스케줄에 명시된 principal sum을 기준으로, 상해사망 시 100%, 후유장애 시 손실 유형별 비율(양손·양발·양안 100%, 한 손·한 발·한쪽 눈 50% 등)을 지급한다. 보험 사고는 외부 원인에 의한 갑작스럽고 의도하지 않은 사고여야 하며, 질병·기왕증은 제외된다. AD&D는 의료비를 지급하지 않는 확정 일시금(fixed indemnity) 상품이며, 종합 여행보험의 구성 요소로 흔히 포함된다.

# Distinctions
- **Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D)** vs [Travel Medical Insurance](/common/insurance/travel-medical-insurance.md) — Travel medical insurance reimburses actual medical expenses (hospital bills, surgery, emergency evacuation) incurred due to illness or injury during a trip; AD&D pays a fixed lump-sum indemnity only when an accident results in death or permanent physical loss. TMI covers the cost of treatment; AD&D replaces income and provides financial security for catastrophic permanent loss — it does not pay for any medical bills.
- **Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D)** vs [Emergency Medical Evacuation](/common/insurance/emergency-medical-evacuation.md) — Emergency medical evacuation covers the cost of transporting an ill or injured traveller to the nearest adequate medical facility or back home; AD&D pays a lump sum only when an accident results in death or permanent dismemberment — neither medical bills nor evacuation costs are covered.
