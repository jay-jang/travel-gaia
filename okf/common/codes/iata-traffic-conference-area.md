> IATA's division of the world into three geographic areas — TC1, TC2 and TC3 — that form the backbone for international fare construction, mileage and tariff rules. TC1 covers the Americas (Western Hemisphere); TC2 covers Europe, Africa and the Middle East; TC3 covers Asia, Australasia and the Pacific.

The three areas (also called Areas 1, 2 and 3) are the geographic foundation of the IATA fare and tariff system. TC1 is the Western Hemisphere (North, Central and South America, plus the Caribbean and adjacent islands); TC2 spans Europe, Africa, the Middle East and the part of Asia roughly west of and including Iran/the Urals; TC3 covers the rest of Asia, the Indian sub-continent's eastern reaches, Australasia and the Pacific islands. Together with global indicators (two-letter routing codes) they determine which fare-construction rules, mileage allowances and through-fare provisions apply to an itinerary based on the areas it travels to, from and via.

**한국어 / Korean** — **IATA 운수회의 지역 (IATA Traffic Conference Area)** — IATA가 전 세계를 세 개의 지리적 지역(TC1, TC2, TC3)으로 나눈 구분으로, 국제 운임 산출(fare construction)·마일리지·요율(tariff) 규칙의 기본 골격을 이룬다. TC1은 미주(서반구), TC2는 유럽·아프리카·중동, TC3은 아시아·오세아니아·태평양을 포괄한다.

세 지역(Area 1, 2, 3으로도 불림)은 IATA 운임·요율 체계의 지리적 토대다. TC1은 서반구(북·중·남미와 카리브 및 인접 도서)이고, TC2는 유럽·아프리카·중동과 대략 이란/우랄 이서(以西)를 포함한 아시아 일부를 아우르며, TC3은 그 나머지 아시아, 오세아니아, 태평양 도서를 포괄한다. 이 지역 구분은 글로벌 인디케이터(2자리 라우팅 코드)와 함께, 여정이 어느 지역으로·에서·경유하는지에 따라 어떤 운임 산출 규칙·마일리지 허용량·통과운임(through-fare) 규정이 적용되는지를 결정한다.

**Aliases:** `Traffic Conference Area`, `IATA Area`, `TC1/TC2/TC3`, `Tariff Conference Area`

# Distinctions
- **IATA Traffic Conference Area** vs [Fare Construction](/air/air-shop/fare-construction.md) — Fare construction is the process of building an international fare from components and mileage; the IATA Traffic Conference Area is the geographic framework that process relies on to select the applicable rules and routing logic.
- **IATA Traffic Conference Area** vs [ISO 3166 Country Code](/common/codes/iso-3166-country-code.md) — ISO 3166 codes identify individual countries; IATA Traffic Conference Areas group whole continents into three macro-regions purpose-built for airfare and tariff rules, not for general country identification.
