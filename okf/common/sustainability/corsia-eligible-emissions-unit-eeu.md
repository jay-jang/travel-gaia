> A CORSIA Eligible Emissions Unit is a carbon credit, each representing one tonne of CO2 reduced, avoided, or removed, that ICAO has approved (on the advice of its Technical Advisory Body) for use in meeting CORSIA offsetting requirements. Aeroplane operators purchase and cancel these units to compensate for emissions above the CORSIA baseline.

ICAO's Technical Advisory Body (TAB) assesses carbon-crediting programmes and the emissions unit types they issue against the CORSIA Emissions Unit Eligibility Criteria (covering, e.g., additionality, permanence, no double counting, and corresponding adjustments). Approved units come from programmes such as the Verified Carbon Standard, Gold Standard, and others. Operators surrender (cancel) eligible units in a registry to demonstrate compliance; SAF use as a CORSIA Eligible Fuel reduces the volume of units that must be cancelled.

**한국어 / Korean** — **CORSIA 적격 배출권(EEU)** — CORSIA 적격 배출권(EEU)은 각 단위가 CO2 1톤의 감축·회피·제거를 나타내는 탄소 배출권으로, ICAO가 (기술자문기구(Technical Advisory Body)의 권고에 따라) CORSIA 상쇄 의무 이행에 사용할 수 있도록 승인한 것이다. 항공기 운영자는 CORSIA 기준선을 초과하는 배출을 보상하기 위해 이 배출권을 구매하고 소각(cancel)한다.

ICAO 기술자문기구(TAB)는 탄소크레딧 프로그램과 그들이 발행하는 배출권 유형을 CORSIA 배출권 적격성 기준(추가성, 영속성, 이중계상 방지, 상응조정 등을 포함)에 비추어 평가한다. 승인된 배출권은 Verified Carbon Standard, Gold Standard 등 프로그램에서 발행된다. 운영자는 레지스트리에서 적격 배출권을 제출(소각)하여 의무 이행을 증명하며, CORSIA 적격연료로서의 SAF 사용은 소각해야 하는 배출권 물량을 줄여 준다.

# Distinctions
- **CORSIA Eligible Emissions Unit (EEU)** vs [EU Allowance (EUA)](/common/sustainability/eu-allowance-eua.md) — An Emissions Unit is a project-based offset credit (a reduction/removal achieved by a project) cancelled under CORSIA; an EU Allowance is a permit to emit issued under the EU ETS cap and surrendered there — different instruments under different schemes.
- **CORSIA Eligible Emissions Unit (EEU)** vs [Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)](/common/sustainability/sustainable-aviation-fuel-saf.md) — An Emissions Unit offsets emissions made elsewhere (out-of-sector); SAF reduces the airline's own emissions at the source (in-sector) and lowers how many units must be cancelled.
