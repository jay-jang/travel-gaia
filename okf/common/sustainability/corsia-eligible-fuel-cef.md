> A CORSIA Eligible Fuel is the ICAO-defined category of aviation fuel that meets the CORSIA Sustainability Criteria and so reduces an operator's offsetting requirement. It comprises CORSIA Sustainable Aviation Fuel (renewable/waste-derived SAF with at least 10% lower life-cycle emissions than the fossil baseline) and CORSIA Lower Carbon Aviation Fuel (LCAF, a fossil-derived fuel that achieves at least 10% life-cycle reduction).

Under CORSIA, an operator's offsetting obligation is calculated from emissions above the scheme baseline, but using a CEF lets the operator subtract an emissions reduction proportional to the fuel's certified life-cycle (well-to-wake) saving, lowering how many CORSIA Eligible Emissions Units it must cancel. To qualify, a fuel must be certified by an ICAO-approved Sustainability Certification Scheme against the CORSIA Sustainability Criteria (covering GHG reduction, land-use, and other safeguards) and use the relevant CORSIA default or actual LCA value. CEF is a fuel category; it should not be confused with the CORSIA Eligible Emissions Unit, which is a carbon credit used for the offsetting that remains.

**한국어 / Korean** — **CORSIA 적격연료(CEF)** — CORSIA Eligible Fuel(CEF)은 CORSIA 지속가능성 기준(Sustainability Criteria)을 충족하여 운영자의 상쇄 의무를 줄여 주는 ICAO 정의 항공연료 범주다. CORSIA Sustainable Aviation Fuel(화석 기준선 대비 전 주기 배출이 최소 10% 낮은 재생·폐기물 유래 SAF)과 CORSIA Lower Carbon Aviation Fuel(LCAF, 전 주기 최소 10% 감축을 달성하는 화석 유래 연료)로 구성된다.

CORSIA에서 운영자의 상쇄 의무는 제도 기준선을 초과하는 배출로 계산되지만, CEF를 사용하면 연료의 인증된 전 주기(well-to-wake) 감축에 비례하는 배출 감축분을 차감할 수 있어 소각해야 할 CORSIA 적격 배출권(EEU) 수가 줄어든다. 자격을 얻으려면 연료가 ICAO 승인 지속가능성 인증 체계에 의해 CORSIA 지속가능성 기준(GHG 감축, 토지이용 등 안전장치 포함)에 따라 인증되고 해당 CORSIA 기본값 또는 실측 LCA 값을 사용해야 한다. CEF는 연료 범주이며, 남은 상쇄에 사용되는 탄소 배출권인 CORSIA 적격 배출권(EEU)과 혼동해서는 안 된다.

# Distinctions
- **CORSIA Eligible Fuel (CEF)** vs [CORSIA Eligible Emissions Unit (EEU)](/common/sustainability/corsia-eligible-emissions-unit-eeu.md) — A CORSIA Eligible Fuel is a physical lower-carbon fuel that reduces emissions at the source and thereby shrinks the offsetting requirement; a CORSIA Eligible Emissions Unit is a carbon credit purchased to offset the emissions that remain — a fuel versus a credit.
- **CORSIA Eligible Fuel (CEF)** vs [Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)](/common/sustainability/sustainable-aviation-fuel-saf.md) — SAF is the broad fuel concept; CORSIA Eligible Fuel is the specific ICAO category (CORSIA SAF plus fossil-based LCAF) that has been certified against the CORSIA Sustainability Criteria for use in the scheme.
