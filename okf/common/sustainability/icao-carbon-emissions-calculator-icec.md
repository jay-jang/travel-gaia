> The ICAO Carbon Emissions Calculator is ICAO's globally harmonized, distance-based methodology and tool for estimating per-passenger CO2 emissions of a flight, developed by experts under ICAO's Committee on Aviation Environmental Protection (CAEP). It is positioned as the only internationally approved tool for estimating air-travel carbon emissions.

The ICEC methodology uses Great Circle Distance between airports to estimate fuel burn, applying best-available industry data on aircraft types, route-specific data, passenger load factors, and cargo/passenger allocation, with a multiplicative cabin-class factor for economy, premium economy, business, and first. It is published in an open-source format (e.g., Methodology version 13.1, 2024) so carriers can customize it with their own data, and it serves as a reference standard for offset programmes and traveler emissions estimates.

**한국어 / Korean** — **ICAO 탄소배출량 계산기(ICEC)** — ICAO 탄소배출량 계산기(ICEC)는 항공편의 승객 1인당 CO2 배출량을 추정하기 위한 ICAO의 전 세계적으로 통일된 거리 기반 방법론이자 도구로, ICAO 항공환경보호위원회(CAEP) 산하 전문가들이 개발했다. 항공 여행 탄소배출량 추정을 위한 유일한 국제 승인 도구로 자리매김하고 있다.

ICEC 방법론은 공항 간 대권거리(Great Circle Distance)를 사용해 연료 소모량을 추정하며, 항공기 기종, 노선별 데이터, 승객 탑승률, 화물/승객 배분에 대한 최선의 산업 데이터를 적용하고, 이코노미·프리미엄 이코노미·비즈니스·퍼스트에 대해 객실 등급 승수(cabin-class factor)를 적용한다. 항공사가 자체 데이터로 커스터마이즈할 수 있도록 오픈소스 형식(예: 방법론 버전 13.1, 2024)으로 공개되며, 상쇄 프로그램과 여행자 배출량 추정의 기준 표준 역할을 한다.

# Distinctions
- **ICAO Carbon Emissions Calculator (ICEC)** vs [IATA CO2 Connect](/common/sustainability/iata-co2-connect.md) — ICEC is ICAO's open methodology built on modelled, globally harmonized average data per route/aircraft; IATA CO2 Connect is an industry calculator built on participating airlines' actual fuel-burn data, so per-flight results can differ.
- **ICAO Carbon Emissions Calculator (ICEC)** vs [Flight Emissions Label (FEL)](/common/sustainability/flight-emissions-label-fel.md) — ICEC is a methodology to estimate emissions; the FEL is an EU labelling scheme that surfaces verified per-flight emissions to passengers at booking.
