> Sustainable Aviation Fuel is a non-fossil-derived, chemically jet-fuel-equivalent 'drop-in' fuel made from feedstocks such as used cooking oil, biomass waste, or green hydrogen plus CO2 (e-kerosene), delivering substantial lifecycle CO2 reductions versus conventional kerosene. It is the primary in-sector decarbonization lever for aviation, projected to deliver roughly 65% of the reductions needed for net-zero by 2050.

As a drop-in fuel, SAF uses existing aircraft, engines, and airport infrastructure; commercial flights are currently certified to fly with blends of up to 50% SAF. Most current supply comes from waste oils with lifecycle reductions of roughly 80%, while emerging pathways can exceed 90%. SAF is central to CORSIA (where ICAO-certified SAF as a CORSIA Eligible Fuel reduces an operator's offsetting requirement) and to the EU's ReFuelEU Aviation blending mandates. Because SAF is blended into shared supply chains, its environmental attributes are often traded via book-and-claim systems rather than tied to a specific aircraft.

**한국어 / Korean** — **지속가능항공연료(SAF)** — 지속가능항공연료(SAF)는 폐식용유, 바이오매스 폐기물, 또는 그린수소와 CO2를 결합한 e-케로신(e-kerosene) 등 비화석 원료로 만든, 화학적으로 제트연료와 동등한 '드롭인(drop-in)' 연료로, 기존 등유 대비 상당한 전 주기(lifecycle) CO2 감축을 제공한다. 항공의 핵심적 부문 내(in-sector) 탈탄소 수단으로, 2050년 넷제로(net-zero) 달성에 필요한 감축의 약 65%를 담당할 것으로 전망된다.

드롭인 연료인 SAF는 기존 항공기, 엔진, 공항 인프라를 그대로 사용하며, 상업운항은 현재 최대 50%까지 혼합한 SAF로 운항하도록 인증되어 있다. 현재 공급의 대부분은 폐유 기반으로 전 주기 약 80%의 감축을 제공하며, 신규 생산 경로는 90%를 넘을 수 있다. SAF는 CORSIA(ICAO 인증을 받은 SAF가 CORSIA 적격연료로서 운영자의 상쇄 의무를 줄임)와 EU의 ReFuelEU Aviation 혼합 의무의 핵심이다. SAF는 공동 공급망에 혼합되기 때문에, 그 환경적 속성은 특정 항공기에 귀속되기보다 북앤클레임(book-and-claim) 방식으로 거래되는 경우가 많다.

# Distinctions
- **Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)** vs [CORSIA Eligible Emissions Unit (EEU)](/common/sustainability/corsia-eligible-emissions-unit-eeu.md) — SAF is a physical lower-carbon fuel that reduces emissions at the source (an in-sector reduction); an Emissions Unit is a purchased carbon credit that offsets emissions made elsewhere (out-of-sector).
- **Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)** vs [ReFuelEU Aviation](/common/sustainability/refueleu-aviation.md) — SAF is the fuel itself; ReFuelEU Aviation is the EU regulation that legally mandates minimum SAF blending shares for fuel supplied at EU airports.
