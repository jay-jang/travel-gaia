> IATA CO2 Connect is an industry per-passenger CO2 emissions calculator built on the IATA Passenger CO2 Calculation Methodology (Recommended Practice 1726), using actual airline fuel-burn data rather than purely theoretical models. It is widely used to surface per-flight CO2 estimates in shopping and booking flows.

Developed with an airline working group and validated with aircraft manufacturers, RP 1726 specifies how fuel measurement, passenger-versus-cargo CO2 allocation, and cabin-class factors are handled. IATA CO2 Connect draws on actual fuel-burn data covering a large majority of the active global passenger fleet, making it a de facto distribution-channel emissions standard for sellers needing consistent, airline-grounded numbers at the point of sale.

**한국어 / Korean** — IATA CO2 Connect은 IATA 여객 CO2 산정 방법론(권고관행 1726, Recommended Practice 1726)을 기반으로 한 승객 1인당 CO2 배출량 산업 계산기로, 순수 이론 모델이 아닌 실제 항공사 연료 소모 데이터를 사용한다. 조회·예약(shopping/booking) 흐름에서 항공편별 CO2 추정치를 표시하는 데 널리 쓰인다.

항공사 작업그룹과 함께 개발되고 항공기 제작사가 검증한 RP 1726은 연료 측정, 승객 대 화물 CO2 배분, 객실 등급 승수를 처리하는 방식을 규정한다. IATA CO2 Connect은 전 세계 운항 중인 여객 기단의 대다수를 포괄하는 실제 연료 소모 데이터를 활용하여, 판매 시점에 일관되고 항공사 데이터에 근거한 수치가 필요한 판매자를 위한 사실상의 유통 채널 배출 표준이 되고 있다.

**Aliases:** `IATA CO2 Connect`, `CO2 Connect`

# Distinctions
- **IATA CO2 Connect** vs [ICAO Carbon Emissions Calculator (ICEC)](/common/sustainability/icao-carbon-emissions-calculator-icec.md) — IATA CO2 Connect uses participating airlines' actual fuel-burn data under RP 1726; ICEC is ICAO's open, modelled distance-based methodology using globally harmonized averages — they can yield different figures for the same flight.
- **IATA CO2 Connect** vs [Flight Emissions Label (FEL)](/common/sustainability/flight-emissions-label-fel.md) — IATA CO2 Connect is a calculator/methodology that produces emissions figures; the FEL is the EU regulatory labelling scheme that displays officially verified per-flight emissions.
- **IATA CO2 Connect** vs [GHG Protocol Scope 3 (Business Travel)](/common/sustainability/ghg-protocol-scope-3-business-travel.md) — Scope 3 Category 6 defines what corporate travel emissions to account for and how to report them; IATA CO2 Connect is one data source/calculator that can supply the per-flight figures used in that accounting.
