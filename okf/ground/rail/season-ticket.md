> A season ticket (or commuter pass) is a rail ticket that grants the holder unlimited travel between two specific stations — or within a defined zone — for a fixed calendar period, typically weekly, monthly, or annual. It is primarily a commuter product offering a lower effective per-journey cost than individual tickets at high travel frequency.

Season tickets are the cornerstone of rail revenue from regular commuters. They are typically priced at a discount relative to the equivalent number of single or return journeys (in the UK, a monthly season is roughly equivalent to 43 single trips at a discounted rate). In the UK, season tickets are sold under the National Rail Conditions of Travel; their prices are regulated by the Department for Transport (DfT), which caps annual increases. Season tickets may be point-to-point (specific origin–destination pair) or zonal (valid on any route within a defined zone, as in London Travelcard–rail combinations). When purchased, the holder is typically issued a photocard railcard reference. Distribution occurs at station ticket offices, self-service machines, online, and increasingly via smartcard or mobile app. Season tickets are distinct from travel passes (Eurail, Interrail) because they are tied to a fixed route rather than granting open network access.

**한국어 / Korean** — **정기권(Season Ticket)** — 정기권(Season Ticket 또는 Commuter Pass)은 두 특정 역 사이 또는 정의된 구역 내에서 고정 기간(보통 주간·월간·연간) 동안 무제한 이용을 허용하는 철도 승차권이다. 주로 높은 이용 빈도에서 개별 승차권보다 낮은 1회 실효 비용을 제공하는 통근 상품이다.

정기권은 정기 통근객으로부터의 철도 수익 핵심 상품이다. 동일 횟수의 편도 또는 왕복 여정 합산 대비 할인 가격으로 책정되며(영국에서 월 정기권은 약 43회 편도 할인 요금에 해당), 영국에서는 National Rail Conditions of Travel에 따라 판매되고 교통부(DfT)가 연간 인상 한도를 규제한다. 정기권은 지점 간(출발지-목적지 쌍 지정) 또는 구역 내(런던 Travelcard-철도 결합처럼 정의된 구역 내 모든 노선 유효) 방식이 있다. 구매 시 소지자는 통상 사진이 붙은 레일카드 참조번호를 발급받는다. 역 창구, 자동발매기, 온라인, 스마트카드 또는 모바일 앱을 통해 판매된다. 정기권은 개방형 네트워크 접근이 아니라 고정 노선에 연결된다는 점에서 Eurail·Interrail 여행 패스와 구별된다.

# Distinctions
- **Season Ticket** vs Railcard — A railcard is a discount card that reduces the purchase price of individual tickets; a season ticket is an all-in entitlement for unlimited travel between two fixed points, with no additional ticket purchase required per journey.
- **Season Ticket** vs [Eurail Pass](/ground/rail/eurail-pass.md) — A Eurail Pass grants open-network access across multiple European countries for non-European residents; a season ticket is tied to a single specific route (or zone) and is intended for high-frequency commuter travel on a domestic network.
