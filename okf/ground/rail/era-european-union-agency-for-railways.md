> The European Union Agency for Railways (ERA) is the EU regulatory body responsible for railway safety and interoperability across the European rail area, issuing Technical Specifications for Interoperability (TSIs), maintaining registers of infrastructure and rolling stock, and authorising railway vehicles for cross-border operation.

ERA was established in 2004 by Regulation (EC) 881/2004 and reformed by Regulation (EU) 2016/796 (the Fourth Railway Package) which significantly expanded its powers. It is headquartered in Valenciennes and Lille, France. ERA's core functions are: (1) drafting and updating Technical Specifications for Interoperability (TSIs) — mandatory EU technical rules covering subsystems such as rolling stock, control-command, and telematics applications for passengers (TAP TSI) and freight (TAF TSI); (2) issuing Single Safety Certificates to railway undertakings allowing them to operate across the EU Single European Railway Area (SERA); (3) operating the European Vehicle Register (EVR) and European Register of Authorised Types of Vehicles (ERATV); and (4) issuing type authorisations and vehicle authorisations for cross-border operation. In the distribution sphere, ERA's oversight of TAP TSI directly shapes how passenger information, ticketing APIs, and interoperable timetable data must be exchanged.

**한국어 / Korean** — **유럽연합 철도청(ERA)** — 유럽연합 철도청(ERA, European Union Agency for Railways)은 유럽 철도 구역 전반의 철도 안전 및 상호운용성을 담당하는 EU 규제 기관으로, 상호운용성 기술 사양(TSI)을 발행하고 인프라·차량 등록부를 유지하며 국경 간 운행을 위한 철도 차량 승인을 담당한다.

ERA는 2004년 규정(EC) 881/2004에 의해 설립되었고, 그 권한을 대폭 확대한 규정(EU) 2016/796(제4차 철도 패키지)에 의해 개편되었다. 본부는 프랑스 발렌시엔 및 릴에 있다. ERA의 핵심 기능: TSI 초안 작성·갱신, 철도 운영사에 EU 전역 운행을 위한 단일 안전 인증 발급, 유럽 차량 등록부(EVR)와 유럽 승인 차량 유형 등록부(ERATV) 운영, 국경 간 운행 차량 유형 승인 발급. 유통 분야에서 ERA의 TAP TSI 감독은 승객 정보, 발권 API, 상호운용 가능한 시각표 데이터 교환 방식을 직접 규정한다.

# Distinctions
- **ERA (European Union Agency for Railways)** vs [TAP TSI](/ground/rail/tap-tsi.md) — ERA is the agency that drafts, maintains and enforces the TSIs (including TAP TSI); TAP TSI is the specific EU technical specification for telematics applications for rail passengers that ERA administers. ERA is the institution; TAP TSI is one of its key regulatory products.
- **ERA (European Union Agency for Railways)** vs [Rail Interoperability Directive (EU 2016/797)](/ground/rail/rail-interoperability-directive.md) — The Rail Interoperability Directive is the EU legislation that mandates technical interoperability and under which ERA has authority to issue TSIs and vehicle authorisations; ERA is the executing agency that implements the directive in practice.
