> CIV is the body of uniform railway law that governs the contract of carriage between a railway and an international rail passenger, set out as Appendix A to COTIF (the Convention concerning International Carriage by Rail) and administered by OTIF. It defines the rights, obligations and liability of carrier and passenger — including the passenger's claim for the journey, carrier liability for delay, death/injury and luggage — and is the legal basis on which through tickets and international rail carriage rest.

CIV (Règles uniformes concernant le Contrat de transport international ferroviaire des voyageurs) forms Appendix A of COTIF 1999 and applies, in principle, to every contract of carriage of passengers by rail, whether for reward or free of charge, where the place of departure and destination lie in two different COTIF Member States. It establishes core doctrines such as the carrier's liability for passenger death and personal injury, liability for delay, missed connections and cancellation, liability for registered luggage, and the rules on the carriage document and the passenger's right of recourse. CIV is the convention-level foundation beneath the EU rail-passenger framework and the day-to-day commercial terms: EU Regulation (EU) 2021/782 incorporates and builds on CIV liability rules, and the CIT-drafted GCC-CIV/PRR and SCIC translate CIV obligations into operational contractual conditions applied to actual tickets. It is the rail analogue of the air-carriage conventions (Warsaw/Montreal) and is paired with CIM, the equivalent uniform rules for the international carriage of goods.

**한국어 / Korean** — **CIV(국제 철도여객운송계약에 관한 통일규칙)** — CIV는 철도와 국제 철도 여객 사이의 운송계약을 규율하는 통일 철도법으로, COTIF(국제철도운송협약) 부속서 A(Appendix A)로 규정되어 OTIF가 관리한다. 운송인과 여객의 권리·의무·책임 — 여정에 대한 청구권, 지연·사상(死傷)·수하물에 대한 운송인 책임 등 — 을 정하며, 통과 승차권(through ticket)과 국제 철도운송이 의거하는 법적 근거를 이룬다.

CIV(Règles uniformes concernant le Contrat de transport international ferroviaire des voyageurs)는 COTIF 1999의 부속서 A를 구성하며, 원칙적으로 출발지와 도착지가 서로 다른 두 COTIF 회원국에 있는 모든 철도 여객운송계약에 유상·무상 여부를 불문하고 적용된다. 여객의 사망·신체상해에 대한 운송인 책임, 지연·연계 실패·운행취소에 대한 책임, 위탁수하물 책임, 운송증서와 여객의 구상권에 관한 규칙 등 핵심 법리를 규정한다. CIV는 EU 철도여객 체계와 일상적 상업조건의 협약 차원 기반으로, EU 규정 (EU) 2021/782은 CIV 책임규정을 수용·확장하며, CIT가 작성한 GCC-CIV/PRR와 SCIC는 CIV 의무를 실제 승차권에 적용되는 운영상 계약조건으로 옮긴다. 항공운송협약(Warsaw/Montreal)의 철도 대응물이며, 국제 물품운송에 대한 통일규칙인 CIM과 짝을 이룬다.

# Distinctions
- **CIV (Uniform Rules concerning the Contract of International Carriage of Passengers by Rail)** vs [Montreal Convention 1999](/common/disruption/montreal-convention-1999.md) — CIV is the convention-level contract-of-carriage and carrier-liability regime for international rail passengers (COTIF Appendix A); the Montreal Convention is the equivalent regime for international air carriage. They are mode-specific analogues addressing the same legal layer (carriage contract and liability) in rail versus air.
- **CIV (Uniform Rules concerning the Contract of International Carriage of Passengers by Rail)** vs [Rail Passenger Rights Regulation (EU) 2021/782](/ground/rail/rail-passenger-rights-regulation-eu-2021-782.md) — CIV is the international COTIF treaty law defining the carriage contract and liability; Regulation (EU) 2021/782 is EU-specific legislation on passenger rights that incorporates CIV liability rules and adds compensation thresholds, assistance and PRM rights. CIV is the broader treaty foundation; the 2021/782 PRR is the EU regulatory overlay.
