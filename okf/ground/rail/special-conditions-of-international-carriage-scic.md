> The Special Conditions of International Carriage (SCIC) are the special carriage conditions, published per ticket type, that define how international rail journeys may be sold, interrupted, refunded and resumed. Issued in product-specific variants such as SCIC-NRT (non-integrated reservation tickets), SCIC-IRT (integrated reservation tickets), SCIC-NT (night trains) and SCIC-RPT (rail-pass tickets), they are the operative international rulebook layered on the general conditions (GCC-CIV/PRR) and tied to the underlying CIV law.

SCIC documents are published in the international tariffs compilation and define the rules that apply to specific international ticket products. Each variant addresses a ticket type: SCIC-NRT for journeys using non-integrated reservation tickets, SCIC-IRT for integrated reservation tickets, SCIC-NT for night trains, SCIC-RPT for rail-pass tickets, and others such as SCIC-EWT for East-West Traffic. They set the conditions for journey interruption, validity, refund and exchange, delay handling and compensation appropriate to each product, and operate alongside the carrier-specific conditions of the participating carriers and the relevant train/offer conditions. SCIC is the umbrella that ties together the NRT and IRT ticket concepts: it is the operative rulebook implementing CIV obligations and the EU passenger rights regime at the level of each international product, sitting on top of the general GCC-CIV/PRR baseline and the Standard International Passenger Tariff (TCV).

**한국어 / Korean** — **국제 특별운송조건(SCIC)** — 국제 특별운송조건(SCIC)은 승차권 유형별로 발행되는 특별운송조건으로, 국제 철도 여정의 판매·중단·환불·재개 방식을 정한다. SCIC-NRT(비통합 예약권), SCIC-IRT(통합 예약권), SCIC-NT(야간열차), SCIC-RPT(철도 패스권) 등 상품별 변형으로 발행되며, 일반조건(GCC-CIV/PRR) 위에 얹히고 기저 CIV 법과 연결되는, 실효성 있는 국제 규칙집이다.

SCIC 문서는 국제 운임 편람에 발행되어 특정 국제 승차권 상품에 적용되는 규칙을 정한다. 각 변형은 승차권 유형을 다룬다: SCIC-NRT는 비통합 예약권 여정, SCIC-IRT는 통합 예약권, SCIC-NT는 야간열차, SCIC-RPT는 철도 패스권, 그리고 동서교통(East-West Traffic)을 위한 SCIC-EWT 등이 있다. 각 상품에 맞는 여정 중단·유효성·환불·교환, 지연 처리·보상 조건을 정하며, 참여 운송인의 운송인별 조건 및 해당 열차/오퍼 조건과 함께 작동한다. SCIC는 NRT·IRT 승차권 개념을 묶는 우산 격으로, CIV 의무와 EU 여객권리 체계를 각 국제 상품 차원에서 구현하는 실효성 있는 규칙집이며, 일반 GCC-CIV/PRR 기준선과 국제 여객운임(TCV) 위에 자리한다.

**Aliases:** `SCIC`, `SCIC-NRT`, `SCIC-IRT`, `SCIC-NT`, `SCIC-RPT`

# Distinctions
- **Special Conditions of International Carriage (SCIC)** vs [General Conditions of Carriage for Rail Passengers (GCC-CIV/PRR)](/ground/rail/general-conditions-of-carriage-for-rail-passengers-gcc-civ-prr.md) — The GCC-CIV/PRR are the general baseline contractual conditions for rail passenger tickets; SCIC are the product-specific special conditions per international ticket type (NRT, IRT, NT, RPT). Where the GCC sets the general terms, the relevant SCIC adds the rules specific to that international product.
- **Special Conditions of International Carriage (SCIC)** vs [Standard International Passenger Tariff (TCV)](/ground/rail/standard-international-passenger-tariff-tcv.md) — The TCV is the tariff defining international fares and the prices/conditions of carriage; SCIC are the special carriage conditions governing how a given international ticket type is used, interrupted and refunded. TCV is fare/price-centric; SCIC is carriage-conditions-centric, applied per ticket product.
