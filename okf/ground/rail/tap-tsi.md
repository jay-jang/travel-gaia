> TAP TSI is the EU Technical Specification for Interoperability for Telematics Applications for Passengers, originally adopted as Commission Regulation (EU) No 454/2011, prescribing standardized interfaces between rail actors for timetables, tariffs, reservations, and fulfillment. It was the legal and technical backbone of interoperable rail ticketing and distribution in Europe until February 2026, when Reg (EU) No 454/2011 was repealed and merged with the freight TAF TSI into the consolidated 'TSI Telematics' (Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2026/253).

Adopted under EU railway interoperability legislation as Regulation (EU) No 454/2011, TAP TSI defined the data and message interfaces that railway undertakings, infrastructure managers, ticket vendors, and other actors must use, with detailed Technical Documents (e.g., timetable, tariff, reservation, and the barcode specification TD B.12 for the Flexible Content Barcode). It did not itself dictate commercial products but mandated interoperable exchange; modern open standards such as OSDM provide concrete implementations of the retail interfaces it required. On 6 February 2026, Reg (EU) No 454/2011 was repealed and consolidated with the freight TAF TSI into the single 'TSI Telematics' under Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2026/253; the TAP TSI body of work (technical documents, retail/distribution interfaces) carries forward within that framework.

**한국어 / Korean** — **여객 텔레매틱스 상호운용 기술사양(TAP TSI)** — TAP TSI(여객 텔레매틱스 응용 상호운용 기술사양)는 시각표·운임·예약·발권에 대해 철도 주체 간 표준 인터페이스를 규정하는 EU 기술사양으로, 본래 EU 규정(Regulation (EU) No 454/2011)으로 채택되었다. 유럽에서 상호운용 가능한 철도 발권·유통의 법적·기술적 근간이었으나, 2026년 2월 해당 규정이 폐지되고 화물용 TAF TSI와 통합되어 단일 'TSI Telematics'(Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2026/253)로 대체되었다.

EU 철도 상호운용 법제 하에서 Regulation (EU) No 454/2011로 채택된 TAP TSI는 철도사업자·인프라관리자·승차권 판매자 등 주체들이 사용해야 하는 데이터·메시지 인터페이스를 정의하며, 상세 기술문서(예: 시각표·운임·예약 및 Flexible Content Barcode 바코드 사양인 TD B.12)를 두었다. 상업 상품 자체를 규정하지는 않고 상호운용 교환을 의무화하였으며, OSDM 같은 현대 개방형 표준이 그것이 요구하는 리테일 인터페이스의 구체적 구현을 제공한다. 2026년 2월 6일 Reg (EU) No 454/2011은 폐지되어 화물용 TAF TSI와 함께 단일 'TSI Telematics'(Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2026/253)로 통합되었으며, TAP TSI의 기술문서·리테일/유통 인터페이스 체계는 그 틀 안에서 계승된다.

# Distinctions
- **TAP TSI** vs [OSDM](/ground/rail/osdm.md) — TAP TSI is the binding EU regulation mandating interoperable rail interfaces; OSDM is a voluntary open API/data-model standard that can implement TAP TSI's retail/distribution requirements.
- **TAP TSI** vs [Flexible Content Barcode (FCB)](/ground/rail/flexible-content-barcode-fcb.md) — TAP TSI is the overarching regulation; FCB is one specific technical artifact (the barcode standard, referenced as TD B.12) that helps satisfy its fulfillment/control requirements.
