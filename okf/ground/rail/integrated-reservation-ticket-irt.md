> An IRT is a UIC ticket type in which the seat reservation is integral to the ticket, forming one inseparable product. It is typical of high-speed and yield-managed trains and is the rail counterpart to the NRT.

With IRT, the admission and reservation are sold together as a single yield-managed product, so the fare is tied to a specific train and place — much like an airline-style booking class. This bundling is essential for operators that apply revenue management and capacity control to individual departures, and it stands in contrast to the NRT model where ticket and reservation are separable.

**한국어 / Korean** — **통합 예약권(IRT)** — IRT(Integrated Reservation Ticket)는 좌석 예약이 승차권에 일체화되어 분리할 수 없는 단일 상품을 이루는 UIC 승차권 유형이다. 고속·수익관리 열차에서 일반적이며 NRT에 대응되는 철도 개념이다.

IRT에서는 admission과 reservation이 하나의 수익관리 상품으로 함께 판매되어, 운임이 특정 열차와 좌석에 묶인다 — 항공의 예약 클래스(booking class)와 유사하다. 이러한 묶음 판매는 개별 출발편에 수익관리와 좌석 통제를 적용하는 운영사에 필수적이며, 승차권과 예약이 분리되는 NRT 모델과 대비된다.

**Aliases:** `Integrated Reservation Ticket`, `IRT ticket`

# Distinctions
- **Integrated Reservation Ticket (IRT)** vs [Non-integrated Reservation Ticket (NRT)](/ground/rail/non-integrated-reservation-ticket-nrt.md) — IRT bundles the reservation into the ticket as one inseparable product; NRT keeps the entitlement and reservation as separate, independently sellable parts.
- **Integrated Reservation Ticket (IRT)** vs [Revenue Management](/air/air-shop/revenue-management.md) — Revenue Management is the pricing/inventory discipline; IRT is the rail ticket form that bundles ticket and reservation so that per-departure revenue management can be applied.
