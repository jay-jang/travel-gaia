> Regulation (EU) 2021/782 on rail passengers' rights and obligations is the EU law, applicable since 7 June 2023, that governs the rights of rail passengers — including delay compensation (25% of the fare for 60–119 minutes, 50% from 120 minutes), re-routing and assistance during disruption, through-ticket obligations, and rights for persons with reduced mobility (PRM). It is the rail counterpart of the air passenger-rights regulation EU 261/2004 and repealed the earlier Regulation (EC) No 1371/2007.

Regulation (EU) 2021/782 modernised and recast the previous rail passenger rights regime (Reg (EC) No 1371/2007). It sets minimum delay-compensation percentages of the ticket price (25% for delays of 60 to 119 minutes and 50% for delays of 120 minutes or more), the right to reimbursement or re-routing under comparable conditions, the right to care (meals, refreshments, accommodation), and obligations on through-tickets — notably that tickets sold by a single railway undertaking in one transaction are treated as a through-ticket triggering connection-protection duties. It introduces a force-majeure exemption for compensation in defined extraordinary circumstances, strengthens rights and free assistance for persons with disabilities and reduced mobility, and addresses bicycle carriage. It operates on top of the COTIF/CIV liability framework, while the operational contractual terms carriers apply (GCC-CIV/PRR) and the international special conditions (SCIC) implement and reference its requirements.

**한국어 / Korean** — **철도 여객권리 규정 (EU) 2021/782(PRR)** — 규정 (EU) 2021/782(철도 여객의 권리와 의무)은 2023년 6월 7일부터 적용되는 EU 법으로, 지연보상(60~119분은 운임의 25%, 120분부터는 50%), 운행 차질 시 대체경로·지원, 통과 승차권(through-ticket) 의무, 이동약자(PRM) 권리 등 철도 여객의 권리를 규율한다. 항공 여객권리 규정 EU 261/2004의 철도 대응물이며, 종전 규정 (EC) No 1371/2007을 대체(폐지)하였다.

규정 (EU) 2021/782은 종전 철도 여객권리 체계(Reg (EC) No 1371/2007)를 현대화·개편(recast)한 것이다. 운임 대비 최소 지연보상 비율(60~119분 지연 25%, 120분 이상 지연 50%), 환급 또는 동등한 조건의 대체경로 권리, 돌봄 권리(식사·다과·숙박), 통과 승차권 의무 — 특히 한 철도사업자가 한 거래로 판매한 승차권은 연계보호 의무를 발생시키는 통과 승차권으로 취급 — 를 정한다. 정의된 특별한 상황(extraordinary circumstances)에서의 보상 면제(force majeure)를 도입하고, 장애인·이동약자(PRM)의 권리와 무료 지원을 강화하며, 자전거 운송도 다룬다. COTIF/CIV 책임체계 위에서 작동하며, 운송인이 적용하는 운영 계약조건(GCC-CIV/PRR)과 국제 특별조건(SCIC)이 그 요구사항을 구현·참조한다.

# Distinctions
- **Rail Passenger Rights Regulation (EU) 2021/782** vs [EU Regulation 261/2004](/common/disruption/eu-regulation-261-2004.md) — Regulation (EU) 2021/782 governs rail passenger rights (delay compensation as a percentage of the fare, re-routing, care, PRM and through-ticket rules); EU 261/2004 is the parallel regulation for air passengers (cancellation/long-delay/denied-boarding compensation in fixed euro amounts). They are mode-specific siblings — rail versus air — with different compensation mechanics.
- **Rail Passenger Rights Regulation (EU) 2021/782** vs [CIV (Uniform Rules concerning the Contract of International Carriage of Passengers by Rail)](/ground/rail/civ-uniform-rules.md) — PRR (2021/782) is EU regulation creating passenger-facing rights and compensation; CIV is the underlying COTIF treaty law defining the carriage contract and carrier liability that PRR incorporates. PRR is the EU statutory layer on top of the CIV convention foundation.
- **Rail Passenger Rights Regulation (EU) 2021/782** vs [General Conditions of Carriage for Rail Passengers (GCC-CIV/PRR)](/ground/rail/general-conditions-of-carriage-for-rail-passengers-gcc-civ-prr.md) — Regulation (EU) 2021/782 is binding EU law setting passenger rights; the GCC-CIV/PRR is the CIT-drafted contractual document that carriers voluntarily adopt to implement those rights (and CIV obligations) in actual ticket terms. One is statute, the other is the contractual T&C that operationalises it.
