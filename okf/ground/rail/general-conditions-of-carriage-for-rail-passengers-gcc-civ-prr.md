> The GCC-CIV/PRR are the uniform contractual terms drawn up by the CIT (International Rail Transport Committee) that railway undertakings apply to passenger tickets, putting the CIV Uniform Rules and the EU Passenger Rights Regulation into day-to-day operational contract conditions. They are the everyday terms-and-conditions layer that carriers and rail passes reference for the contract of carriage, refunds, exchanges and after-sales handling.

Drafted and maintained by the CIT and recommended to its member transport undertakings, the GCC-CIV/PRR ensure that, as far as feasible, uniform contractual conditions apply to both international and domestic rail passenger traffic. They translate the legal obligations of the CIV Uniform Rules (COTIF Appendix A) and EU Regulation (EU) 2021/782 into the practical terms governing ticket purchase and use, conditions of carriage, liability, complaints, and after-sales rights. The text of the GCC-CIV/PRR and the list of participating undertakings are published by the CIT and via the railpassenger.info portal, and new editions are issued periodically (for example, the edition applicable from 14 December 2025). For international journeys, the GCC-CIV/PRR sit alongside the Special Conditions of International Carriage (SCIC), which add product-specific rules per ticket type (NRT, IRT, NT, etc.).

**한국어 / Korean** — **철도 여객 일반운송조건(GCC-CIV/PRR)** — GCC-CIV/PRR는 CIT(국제철도운송위원회)가 작성한 통일 계약조건으로, 철도사업자가 여객 승차권에 적용하며 CIV 통일규칙과 EU 여객권리 규정을 일상적 운영 계약조건으로 구체화한다. 운송인과 철도 패스가 운송계약·환불·교환·사후 처리(after-sales)와 관련해 참조하는, 일상적인 약관(T&C) 레이어다.

CIT가 작성·관리하고 회원 운송사업자에게 적용을 권고하는 GCC-CIV/PRR는 가능한 한 국제 및 국내 철도 여객운송 모두에 통일된 계약조건이 적용되도록 한다. CIV 통일규칙(COTIF 부속서 A)과 EU 규정 (EU) 2021/782의 법적 의무를, 승차권 구매·사용, 운송조건, 책임, 불만 처리, 사후 권리를 규율하는 실무 조건으로 옮긴다. GCC-CIV/PRR 본문과 참여 사업자 목록은 CIT와 railpassenger.info 포털을 통해 공표되며, 새 판이 주기적으로 발행된다(예: 2025년 12월 14일부터 적용되는 판). 국제 여정에서는 승차권 유형별(NRT·IRT·NT 등) 상품 규칙을 더하는 국제 특별운송조건(SCIC)과 나란히 적용된다.

# Distinctions
- **General Conditions of Carriage for Rail Passengers (GCC-CIV/PRR)** vs [Special Conditions of International Carriage (SCIC)](/ground/rail/special-conditions-of-international-carriage-scic.md) — The GCC-CIV/PRR are the general, cross-cutting contractual terms applied to rail passenger tickets (international and domestic); SCIC are the product-specific special conditions for particular international ticket types (SCIC-NRT, SCIC-IRT, SCIC-NT, etc.). GCC is the general baseline; SCIC adds the per-product international rules on top.
- **General Conditions of Carriage for Rail Passengers (GCC-CIV/PRR)** vs [Rail Passenger Rights Regulation (EU) 2021/782](/ground/rail/rail-passenger-rights-regulation-eu-2021-782.md) — Regulation (EU) 2021/782 is binding EU law setting passenger rights; the GCC-CIV/PRR is the CIT-drafted contractual document that carriers voluntarily adopt to implement those rights (and CIV obligations) in actual ticket terms. One is statute, the other is the contractual T&C that operationalises it.
