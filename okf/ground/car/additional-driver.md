> An additional driver is a person other than the primary renter who is authorized and named on the rental agreement to operate the rental vehicle. Rental companies typically charge a daily fee per additional driver, though membership programmes, corporate accounts, or spouse/domestic-partner policies can waive the surcharge.

Adding an authorized driver to the rental agreement is a legal and insurance prerequisite: an unnamed person driving the vehicle is typically in breach of the rental terms, which can void the collision damage waiver and expose the renter to full liability. Most companies charge USD 10–15 per day per additional driver, capped at the full rental period cost; loyalty programme members (Gold, Emerald, Avis Preferred Plus, etc.) and corporate contract holders frequently receive one additional driver at no charge. Age restrictions and the minimum driving-licence age applicable in the rental country also apply to additional drivers, with young-driver surcharges potentially layering on top. The fee and rules are governed by the rental agreement and must comply with local consumer-protection law.

**한국어 / Korean** — **추가 운전자(Additional Driver)** — 추가 운전자(Additional Driver)는 주 임차인 이외에 렌탈 계약서에 승인·기재되어 렌터카를 운전할 수 있는 사람이다. 렌터카 업체는 일반적으로 추가 운전자당 일일 요금을 부과하지만, 멤버십 프로그램, 법인 계약, 배우자/동거인 정책에 따라 면제될 수 있다.

임차인 이외의 승인된 운전자를 렌탈 계약서에 추가하는 것은 법적·보험적 전제 조건이다. 계약서에 미등재된 사람이 차량을 운전하면 렌탈 조건 위반으로, 충돌 손해 면책(CDW)이 무효화되고 임차인이 전액 책임을 지게 될 수 있다. 대부분의 업체는 추가 운전자당 하루 USD 10~15(전체 렌탈 기간 총액 상한)를 부과하며, 로열티 프로그램 회원(Gold, Emerald, Avis Preferred Plus 등)과 법인 계약 고객은 추가 운전자 1명을 무료로 받는 경우가 많다. 나이 제한 및 렌탈 국가의 최소 면허 연령도 추가 운전자에게 적용되며, 여기에 영 드라이버 요금이 더해질 수 있다. 요금과 규칙은 렌탈 계약서가 규정하며 현지 소비자 보호법을 준수해야 한다.

# Distinctions
- **Additional Driver** vs [Collision Damage Waiver](/ground/car/collision-damage-waiver.md) — CDW protects the primary renter against vehicle damage costs; an additional driver must be explicitly named on the rental agreement for that CDW protection to extend to them — an unnamed driver is typically excluded from coverage.
- **Additional Driver** vs [Young Driver Surcharge](/ground/car/young-driver-surcharge.md) — A young driver surcharge is an age-based fee applied when a driver is under a defined age threshold (typically 25); an additional driver fee is charged regardless of age whenever a second driver is added. Both can apply simultaneously if the additional driver is young.
