> Personal Accident Insurance (PAI) is a car-rental counter product that provides accident-benefit coverage for the renter and any passengers against bodily injury, death, and disability arising from a collision or accident involving the rented vehicle. Unlike a Collision Damage Waiver, which addresses damage to the vehicle, PAI is true insurance covering people rather than property.

PAI typically pays a lump-sum benefit for accidental death (AD&D) and may also cover accident-related medical expenses for the renter and all passengers who were in the vehicle at the time of the accident. Coverage limits, eligible beneficiaries, exclusions (e.g. acts of war, driving under the influence, off-road use), and the distinction between single-incident limits and per-person sub-limits vary by country and rental brand. In many markets an equivalent product is sold under the name Personal Accident Cover (PAC). Some travelers already carry equivalent protection through their personal auto or health insurance or through credit card benefit programmes, making PAI genuinely redundant for them — rental companies are required in many jurisdictions to disclose pre-existing coverage options to the renter. The ACRISS Counter Products taxonomy identifies PAI as a separate product code alongside CDW, SLI, TP, and the Excess waiver, so it appears as a distinct line item on rental agreements and is exchanged as such between suppliers, brokers, and booking platforms via OpenTravel vehicle messages.

**한국어 / Korean** — **탑승자 상해 보험(PAI, Personal Accident Insurance)** — 탑승자 상해 보험(PAI)은 렌터카 카운터에서 판매하는 상품으로, 대여 차량과 관련된 충돌·사고로 인한 임차인 및 탑승객의 신체 상해, 사망, 장해에 대해 사고 급부금을 제공한다. 차량 손해를 다루는 충돌 손해 면책(CDW)과 달리 PAI는 재산이 아닌 사람을 대상으로 하는 실제 보험이다.

PAI는 일반적으로 운전자 및 사고 당시 차량에 탑승한 모든 승객에 대해 사망(AD&D) 시 일시금과 사고 관련 의료비를 지급한다. 보상 한도, 수혜 대상, 면책 사항(전쟁 행위, 음주운전, 비포장 주행 등), 사고 건별 한도와 1인당 부한도의 구분은 국가 및 렌털 브랜드마다 다르다. 많은 시장에서 동일한 상품이 Personal Accident Cover(PAC)라는 명칭으로 판매된다. 일부 여행자는 이미 개인 자동차보험, 건강보험 또는 신용카드 부가 혜택을 통해 동등한 보호를 받고 있어 PAI가 실질적으로 중복될 수 있다. 많은 법역에서 렌털 업체는 기존 보장 옵션을 임차인에게 고지해야 한다. ACRISS 카운터 상품 분류 체계는 PAI를 CDW, SLI, TP, 자기부담금 면제와 별도의 상품 코드로 식별하므로, 렌털 계약서에서 독립된 항목으로 표시되며 OpenTravel 차량 메시지를 통해 공급자·브로커·예약 플랫폼 간에 그렇게 교환된다.

**Aliases:** `PAI`, `Personal Accident Cover`, `PAC`, `Accidental Death and Disability`, `ADD`

# Distinctions
- **Personal Accident Insurance (PAI)** vs [Collision Damage Waiver](/ground/car/collision-damage-waiver.md) — CDW (or LDW) waives the renter's financial liability for damage to the rental vehicle itself; PAI is personal injury insurance covering bodily harm, death, and disability for people inside the vehicle. CDW protects the asset; PAI protects the occupants.
- **Personal Accident Insurance (PAI)** vs [Supplemental Liability Insurance](/ground/car/supplemental-liability-insurance.md) — SLI covers the renter's legal liability to third parties (other drivers, pedestrians, property owners) who suffer harm caused by the renter; PAI covers the renter and passengers inside the rented car, regardless of fault.
- **Personal Accident Insurance (PAI)** vs [Excess](/ground/car/excess.md) — The excess is the out-of-pocket amount a renter pays for vehicle damage even when CDW is in force; PAI has no excess in this sense — it pays the stated benefit directly to the covered person(s) without deducting a prior charge.
