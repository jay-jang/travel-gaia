> The excess is the capped maximum amount a renter remains liable to pay the rental company for loss of or damage to (and often theft of) the hire vehicle, even when a damage waiver such as CDW or LDW is in place. It is the British-English equivalent of the American 'deductible' and is typically pre-authorized against the renter's credit card at pickup.

In UK and European car hire, a damage waiver (CDW) and theft protection are usually bundled into the base rate, but they do not reduce the renter's liability to zero: a non-waivable excess, often ranging from around EUR 100 to over EUR 2,000, remains payable for each incident of damage or theft. The excess is normally blocked on the renter's credit card as part of the security deposit at the counter and is charged or retained if the vehicle is returned damaged. Renters can reduce or eliminate the excess by purchasing the rental company's excess-reduction product (e.g. Super CDW / Excess Waiver) at the counter, or by buying standalone third-party 'car hire excess insurance' which reimburses the excess they have to pay. The same figure is referred to as the deductible in North American agreements.

**한국어 / Korean** — **자기부담금(Excess)** — Excess(자기부담금)는 CDW나 LDW 같은 손해 면책이 적용되더라도 이용자가 대여 차량의 멸실·손해(흔히 도난 포함)에 대해 렌터카 회사에 부담해야 하는 책임의 상한 금액이다. 미국식 표현인 'deductible(공제액)'에 해당하며, 통상 인수 시 이용자 신용카드에 가승인으로 잡힌다.

영국·유럽 렌터카에서는 충돌 손해 면책(CDW, Collision Damage Waiver)과 도난 보호가 기본 요율에 포함되는 경우가 많지만, 이것이 이용자의 책임을 0으로 줄여 주지는 않는다. 즉, 통상 약 EUR 100에서 EUR 2,000 이상에 이르는 면제 불가 자기부담금(excess)이 손해·도난 사고 건마다 남아 있다. excess는 보통 카운터에서 보증금의 일부로 이용자 신용카드에 가승인(hold)으로 잡히며, 차량이 손상된 채 반납되면 청구되거나 차감된다. 이용자는 카운터에서 렌터카 회사의 자기부담금 경감 상품(예: Super CDW / Excess Waiver)을 구매하거나, 자신이 부담한 excess를 보전해 주는 별도의 제3자 'car hire excess insurance'에 가입해 excess를 줄이거나 없앨 수 있다. 북미 계약에서는 동일한 금액을 deductible(공제액)이라 부른다.

# Distinctions
- **Excess** vs [Collision Damage Waiver](/ground/car/collision-damage-waiver.md) — CDW is the waiver itself — the supplier's contractual promise not to pursue the renter for covered collision damage; the excess is the carve-out from that waiver, i.e. the amount the renter still owes despite holding CDW.
- **Excess** vs [Authorization Hold](/common/pay/authorization-hold.md) — An authorization hold is the temporary card block taken as a deposit; the excess is the underlying contractual liability cap that determines how large that hold may be and how much can ultimately be charged for damage.
