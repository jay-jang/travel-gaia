> A Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) is a counter product by which the rental supplier contractually waives the renter's financial responsibility for accident or collision damage to the rented vehicle, usually subject to conditions and any excess. It is a waiver, not an insurance policy, and is the most-sold counter protection product.

Because CDW is a contractual waiver rather than insurance, the supplier agrees not to pursue the renter for covered collision damage, but coverage can be voided by breaches such as unauthorized drivers, off-road use, or driving under the influence, and an excess/deductible often remains payable. CDW typically covers damage to the vehicle but not theft, third-party liability, personal injury, or personal belongings — those are addressed by separate products. In much of the US market the broader Loss Damage Waiver (which adds theft protection and often loss-of-use) is now sold in place of a standalone CDW, so the two terms are frequently conflated.

**한국어 / Korean** — **충돌 손해 면책(CDW, Collision Damage Waiver)** — 충돌 손해 면책(CDW, Collision Damage Waiver)은 렌터카 공급사가 대여 차량의 사고·충돌 손해에 대한 이용자의 재정적 책임을 계약상 면제하는 카운터 상품으로, 통상 조건 및 자기부담금(excess)이 적용된다. 보험 증권이 아니라 면책(waiver)이며, 카운터에서 가장 많이 판매되는 보호 상품이다.

CDW는 보험이 아니라 계약상 면책이므로, 공급사는 면책 대상 충돌 손해에 대해 이용자에게 청구하지 않기로 합의하지만, 무단 운전자, 비포장 도로 주행, 음주운전 등 위반 시 보장이 무효가 될 수 있고 자기부담금이 남는 경우가 많다. CDW는 통상 차량 손해는 보장하지만 도난, 제3자 배상책임, 신체 상해, 개인 소지품은 보장하지 않으며, 이는 별도 상품으로 다뤄진다. 미국 시장의 상당 부분에서는 도난 보호와 흔히 휴차 손실(loss-of-use)까지 더한 더 넓은 Loss Damage Waiver가 단독 CDW를 대체해 판매되므로, 두 용어가 자주 혼용된다.

**Aliases:** `Collision Damage Waiver`, `Damage Waiver`, `CDW`

# Distinctions
- **Collision Damage Waiver** vs [Loss Damage Waiver](/ground/car/loss-damage-waiver.md) — CDW waives liability for collision/accident damage to the vehicle only; LDW is broader, typically adding theft protection and loss-of-use on top. In the US, LDW is now the standard product sold.
- **Collision Damage Waiver** vs [Supplemental Liability Insurance](/ground/car/supplemental-liability-insurance.md) — CDW (a waiver) addresses damage to the rented vehicle itself; SLI is true insurance covering the renter's legal liability for third-party injury and property damage to others.
- **Collision Damage Waiver** vs [Excess](/ground/car/excess.md) — CDW is the waiver itself — the supplier's contractual promise not to pursue the renter for covered collision damage; the excess is the carve-out from that waiver, i.e. the amount the renter still owes despite holding CDW.
