> Mobility as a Service (MaaS) is the integration of, and access to, multiple transport services — public transport, ride-hailing, car-share, bike/scooter-share, taxi and car rental — into a single digital offer that lets a user plan, book and pay for an end-to-end journey across modes. UITP frames it as a tailored, on-demand service built on an efficient public-transport backbone.

MaaS shifts the customer relationship from buying individual tickets and rentals mode-by-mode to consuming mobility through one digital interface that aggregates supply, plans point-to-point journeys, and handles ticketing and payment — sometimes as pay-as-you-go and sometimes as bundled subscription packages. Levels of integration are commonly described on a scale from no integration, through integration of information (journey planning), integration of booking and payment, to integration of the service offer (bundles/subscriptions) and societal/policy integration. For the car-rental and shared-mobility industry, MaaS is significant because car rental, car-share and ride-hail become components of a larger mobility ecosystem, which in turn depends on machine-readable, real-time supply data shared through specifications such as GBFS and on regulatory data exchange such as MDS. UITP, the International Association of Public Transport, is a leading body shaping the MaaS definition and best practice.

**한국어 / Korean** — **서비스형 모빌리티(MaaS, Mobility as a Service)** — Mobility as a Service(MaaS, 서비스형 모빌리티)는 대중교통·라이드헤일·카셰어·자전거/스쿠터 공유·택시·렌터카 등 여러 교통 서비스를 하나의 디지털 제공으로 통합·접근하게 하여, 이용자가 여러 수단을 아우르는 출발지-목적지 여정을 계획·예약·결제할 수 있게 하는 개념이다. UITP는 이를 효율적 대중교통을 기반으로 한 맞춤형 온디맨드 서비스로 규정한다.

MaaS는 고객 관계를 수단별로 개별 승차권·대여를 구매하던 방식에서, 공급을 통합하고 출발지-목적지 여정을 계획하며 발권·결제까지 처리하는 하나의 디지털 인터페이스로 모빌리티를 소비하는 방식으로 전환한다. 이는 종량제(pay-as-you-go)일 수도, 묶음 구독(subscription) 패키지일 수도 있다. 통합 수준은 흔히 무통합 → 정보 통합(여정 계획) → 예약·결제 통합 → 서비스 제공 통합(번들/구독) → 사회·정책 통합의 단계로 설명된다. 렌터카·공유 모빌리티 산업에서 MaaS가 중요한 이유는, 렌터카·카셰어·라이드헤일이 더 큰 모빌리티 생태계의 구성요소가 되며, 이 생태계는 GBFS 같은 명세를 통한 기계 판독 가능한 실시간 공급 데이터와 MDS 같은 규제 데이터 교환에 의존하기 때문이다. 국제대중교통연맹(UITP, International Association of Public Transport)은 MaaS 정의와 모범 사례를 이끄는 주요 기관이다.

# Distinctions
- **Mobility as a Service** vs [Multimodal Transport](/ground/rail/multimodal-transport.md) — Multimodal transport describes a journey physically using more than one mode; MaaS is the digital integration layer that aggregates, plans, books and pays for those modes through one offer — a commercial/technical concept, not just the physical use of several modes.
- **Mobility as a Service** vs [General Bikeshare Feed Specification](/ground/car/general-bikeshare-feed-specification.md) — GBFS is a data standard that publishes real-time shared-mobility availability; MaaS is the consumer-facing service concept that consumes such feeds to assemble a journey — GBFS is plumbing, MaaS is the offer.
